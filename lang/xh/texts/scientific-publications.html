Iimpapasho Zenzululwazi

Iinyaniso Zokutya Ezivulekileyo kuphando: iimpapasho, izicatshulwa, kunye nezixhobo zabaphandi

Ukususela ngo-2012, i-Open Food Facts ibe ngumthombo obalulekileyo kubaphandi kwihlabathi liphela abasebenzisa idatha yethu yokutya kwizifundo ngesondlo, okusingqongileyo, impilo yoluntu, kunye nezinye iinkalo ezininzi. Isiseko sethu sedatha esivulekileyo sikhankanyiwe kumakhulu eephepha zenzululwazi.

Kwabaphandi

Ngaba usebenzisa okanye ucinga ngokusebenzisa i-Open Food Facts njengenxalenye yeprojekthi yophando?

Nceda usazise ku- contact@openfoodfacts.org

Siyavuya ukunceda nokusebenzisana namaqela ophando ukuze sibone indlela esinokuwuxhasa ngayo umsebenzi wenu.

Isondlo kunye neMpilo yoLuntu

Abaphandi basebenzisa isiseko sethu sedatha ukufunda umgangatho wesondlo, ukwakheka kokutya, kunye nokungenelela kwempilo yoluntu. Idatha ye-Open Food Facts ibe negalelo elikhulu kuphando novavanyo lweNutri-Score.

Impembelelo yokusingqongileyo

Izifundo malunga nokunyuka kwekhabhoni, i-Eco-Score, kunye nokuzinza kokusingqongileyo kweemveliso zokutya ziyazuza kwisiseko sethu esibanzi solwazi lwemveliso.

Ukulungiswa kokutya

Uphando malunga nokutya okucutshungulwe kakhulu kusetyenziswa udidi lwe-NOVA luxhomekeke kuluhlu lwethu lwezithako oluneenkcukacha kunye nolwazi lokucubungula.

Isayensi yeKhompyutha kunye ne-AI

Ukufunda koomatshini, ukuqondwa kwemifanekiso, ukucutshungulwa kolwimi lwendalo, kunye neeprojekthi zesayensi yedatha zisebenzisa iiseti zethu zedatha ezivulekileyo kunye nemifanekiso.

Ukuziphatha Kwabathengi

Izifundo ezihlola ukhetho lokutya, imiphumo yokulebhelisha, kunye nokungafihlisi nto kwishishini lokutya zisebenzisa idatha ye-Open Food Facts.

Umgaqo-nkqubo woKutya

Abenzi bemigaqo-nkqubo nabaphandi bahlalutya imithetho yokutya, iimfuno zokulebhelisha, kunye neendlela zokwenza ishishini besebenzisa isiseko sethu sedatha esicacileyo.

Ukufikelela kwiDatha

I-Open Food Facts inika iindlela ezininzi zokufikelela kulwazi lwethu:

  • Ukuthunyelwa kwedatabase ngaphandle: Khuphela ii-database dumps ezipheleleyo kwiifomathi ezahlukeneyo. Jonga iphepha lethu ledatha ukuze ufumane iinkcukacha.
  • API: Buza ngedathabheyisi yethu ngokwenkqubo usebenzisa i-API yethu epheleleyo. Uxwebhu lwe-API
  • Ujongano lokukhangela: Jonga iimveliso ngqo kwiwebhusayithi yethu ngezakhono zokukhangela eziphambili kunye nokucoca.

Data Quality

Njengesiseko sedatha esinemithombo emininzi, umgangatho wedatha unokwahluka. Xa usebenzisa i-Open Food Facts kuphando:

  • Cinga ngokucoca ngamanqaku okugqibelela okanye izalathi zomgangatho wedatha
  • Qinisekisa amanqaku edatha abalulekileyo ukuba ukuchaneka kubalulekile
  • Qaphela ucalucalulo lweendawo kunye nexesha elithile ekugubungeleni kwethu idatha
  • Nxibelelana nathi ukuba ufuna uncedo ngovavanyo lomgangatho wedatha kuphando lwakho

Sebenzisana Nathi

Siyayamkela intsebenziswano namaqela ophando:

  • Iimfuno zedatha: Sazise ukuba ufuna idatha ethile okanye ukuphuculwa kwendlela esimi ngayo isiseko sedatha yethu
  • Amaqela okuskena: Cwangcisa amaphulo okuqokelelwa kwedatha ajoliswe kuwo kummandla wakho wophando okanye kudidi lwemveliso
  • Ukwabelana ngeziphumo: Yabelana ngezinto ozifumeneyo noluntu lwethu kwaye uncede uphucule ulwazi lwakho
  • Uphuhliso lwezixhobo: Sebenzisana nezixhobo eziza kunceda abaphandi kunye nabathengi

Nxibelelana nathi ku- contact@openfoodfacts.org ukuze sixoxe ngamathuba entsebenziswano.
