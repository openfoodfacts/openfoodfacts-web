Dɔnniya gafew

Dumuni dafalenw tiɲɛ ɲininiw na: sɛbɛnw, kumasenw, ani nafolo ɲininikɛlaw ye

Kabini san 2012, Open Food Facts kɛra nafolo nafama ye ɲininikɛlaw bolo diɲɛ kɔnɔ minnu bɛ baara kɛ n’an ka dumuni kunnafoniw ye walasa ka kalanw kɛ balocogo, sigida, foroba kɛnɛyako ani ko caman wɛrɛw kan. An ka kunnafonidilan dafalen kofɔra dɔnniya sɛbɛn kɛmɛ caman kɔnɔ.

Ɲininikalaw fɛ

Yala aw bɛ baara kɛ ni Open Food Facts ye walima aw bɛ ka baara kɛ ni o ye ɲinini baara dɔ kɔnɔ wa?

Aw k’a fɔ an ye contact@openfoodfacts.org

An nisɔndiyara ka dɛmɛ don ani ka jɛɲɔgɔnya kɛ ni ɲininikɛlaw ye walasa k’a dɔn an bɛ se k’aw ka baara dɛmɛ cogo min na.

Sɛbɛn minnu bɛ dumuni dafalenw kofɔ

Dɔnniya barokunw sɛgɛsɛgɛ minnu bɛ an ka kunnafonidilanw ɲɛfɔ

Open Food Facts kofɔra dɔnniya sɛbɛn 600 ni kɔ kɔnɔ. Gafe minnu bɛ Open Food Facts ni porozɛ minnu bɛ tali kɛ o la, olu lajɛcogo ladɛrɛsiw bɛ duguma:

  • Barokun minnu bɛ "Cɛɲi dafalenw" kofɔ.
  • Barokun minnu bɛ "OpenBeautyFacts" kofɔ.
  • Open *Facts porozɛw bɛɛ (ɲini faralen ɲɔgɔn kan) .

Ɲininikalan yɔrɔ kolomaw

An ka kunnafoniw bɛ ɲininikɛyɔrɔ suguya caman dɛmɛ

Balocogo ɲuman & Foroba kɛnɛyako

Ɲininikalaw bɛ baara kɛ n’an ka kunnafonidilan ye walasa ka balocogo ɲuman, dumuni kɛcogo, ani foroba kɛnɛyako fɛɛrɛw sɛgɛsɛgɛ. Dumuni dafalenw kunnafoniw kɛra sababu ye ka Nutri-Score ɲinini ni jateminɛ kɛ.

Laminiko nɔfɛkow

Kalan minnu bɛ kɛ dumunifɛnw ka gazi bɔlenw senna, Eco-Score, ani sigida sabatili kan, olu bɛ nafa sɔrɔ an ka fɛn dilannenw kunnafoniw kunnafonidilanba la.

Dumunikɛcogo

Sɛgɛsɛgɛli minnu kɛra dumuniw kan minnu dilannen don kosɛbɛ ni NOVA ka danfara ye, olu bɛ tali kɛ an ka fɛn dilannenw lisɛli caman na ani u dilanni kunnafoniw na.

Ɔridinatɛri dɔnniya & AI

Masina kalanni, ja dɔnni, kan nafama baarakɛcogo, ani kunnafonidilanw dɔnniya porozɛw bɛ an ka kunnafonidilan dafalenw ni jaw nafa.

Kunnafoni-di-bagaw ka taamacogo

Kalan minnu bɛ dumuni suganditaw, u taamasiɲɛw nɔfɛkow, ani kɛnɛyako sɛgɛsɛgɛ dumuniko siratigɛ la, olu bɛ baara kɛ ni Open Food Facts kunnafoniw ye.

Dumuniko politiki

Politikitɔnw ni ɲininikɛlaw bɛ dumuni sariyaw, taamasiyɛnw wajibiyalenw, ani izini kɛcogo sɛgɛsɛgɛ ni an ka kunnafonidilan jɛlen ye.

Nafolo minnu bɛ sɔrɔ ɲininikɛlaw bolo

Baara kɛcogo ani a fɔcogo Open Food Facts

Donanw sɔrɔli

Open Food Facts bɛ fɛɛrɛ caman di walasa ka se ka an ka kunnafoniw sɔrɔ:

  • Donanw bɔli : Donanw ka dump dafalenw telesarse cogo caman na. Aw ye an ka Donanw ɲɛ lajɛ walasa ka kunnafoni wɛrɛw sɔrɔ.
  • API : An ka kunnafonidilan ɲininkali kɛ porogaramu siratigɛ la an ka API bɛɛjɛfanga fɛ. API Sɛbɛnniw
  • Ɲini kɛcogo : Fɛnw sɛgɛsɛgɛ an ka siti kan k’a ɲɛsin ɲinini ni filɛli seko kɔrɔw ma.

Dumuni dafalenw tiɲɛniw fɔli

Ni aw bɛ baara kɛ ni Open Food Facts ye aw ka ɲininiw na, aw bɛ a fɔ ka ɲɛ. Nin ye citation format sugandilen ye:

Site web citation:

Dumuni Tiɲɛniw Dabɔ. (San). Dumuni Dabɔlenw Tiɲɛw - Diɲɛ. A bɔra https://world.openfoodfacts.org kan


Donanw ka citation:

Dumuni Tiɲɛniw Dabɔ. (San). Dumuniko tiɲɛkow kunnafonidilan da wuli [Data set]. A bɔra https://world.openfoodfacts.org/data kan

Lasegin-ka-bɔnye

An ka kunnafoniw dabɔlen don ani u bɛ se ka baara kɛ kokura hɔrɔnya la:

  • Donanw bɛ sɔrɔ Database Open License (ODbL)kɔnɔ
  • Donanw kɔnɔkow bɛ Donanw kɔnɔkow lase (DbCL)jukɔrɔ
  • Fɛn dilannenw ja bɛ sɔrɔ Creative Commons Attribution ShareAlike (CC BY-SA 3.0)kɔnɔ

Aw ye an ka Baarakɛcogo sariyaw ni baarakɛcogo kura lajɛ walasa ka kunnafoni dafalenw sɔrɔ.

Data Quality

I n’a fɔ jamanadenw ka kunnafonidilan, kunnafonidilanw cogoya bɛ se ka ɲɔgɔn ta. Ni aw bɛ baara kɛ ni Open Food Facts ye ɲinini kama:

  • Aw bɛ jateminɛ kɛ filɛli la ni dafalen hakɛw ye walima kunnafonidilanw jogo jiralanw ye
  • Donanw yɔrɔ kɔrɔlenw sinsin ni tiɲɛni nafa ka bon
  • Aw ye aw janto dugukoloko ni waati cogoyaw la an ka kunnafonidilanw kɔnɔ
  • Aw ye anw sɔrɔ ni aw mago bɛ dɛmɛ na kunnafonidilanw jogoɲini na aw ka ɲininiw kama

Aw ka jɛkafɔ kɛ ni Anw ye

An bɛ jɛ-ka-baara ni ɲininikɛlaw ka jɛkuluw bisimila:

  • Donanw magow : Aw k’a fɔ an ye n’aw mago bɛ kunnafoni kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenw na walima n’aw mago bɛ ɲɛtaa la an ka kunnafonidilanw sigicogo la
  • Scan parties : Aw ye kunnafonidi kanpaɲi laɲinitaw labɛn aw ka ɲinini mara walima fɛn dilannen suguya kama
  • Jateminɛw tilatilali : aw ka sɔrɔw tila an ka sigidamɔgɔw cɛ ani ka dɛmɛ don ka dɔnniya yiriwa
  • Baarakɛminɛnw dilanni : jɛkafɔ kɛ baarakɛminɛnw kan minnu bɛ ɲininikɛlaw ni feerekɛlaw bɛɛ nafa

Aw bɛ se ka anw sɔrɔ contact@openfoodfacts.org walasa ka baro kɛ jɛkafɔ siraw kan.

Sɛgɛsɛgɛli nɔfɛkow

Open Food Facts ye dɛmɛba don dɔnniya ɲininiw ni foroba kɛnɛyako la:

  • Dɔnniya gafe 600 ni kɔ ye an ka kunnafonidilanw kofɔ
  • Ɲininikalanjɛkulubaw minnu ye EREN (France), USP (Brezil), ani Liège Iniwɛrisite (Bɛljiki) ye, olu ye baara kɛ n’an ka kunnafoniw ye
  • An ka kunnafonidilan ye dɛmɛ don ɲininiw na minnu bɛ na ni politiki caman caman cili ye ani foroba kɛnɛyako ladilikanw
  • An ye sira di jamanaw ni ɲɔgɔn cɛ danfara ni diɲɛ kalanw ma dumuniko ɲuman ni balocogo kan
