Descubra

Open Pet Food Facts is a pet food products database made by everyone, for everyone.

Pode usá-lo para fazer melhores escolhas alimentares, e como são dados abertos, qualquer um pode reutilizá-lo para qualquer finalidade.

Contribuir

Open Pet Food Facts is a non-profit project developed by thousands of volunteers from around the world. You can start contributing by adding a petfood product, and we have lots of exciting projects you can contribute to in many different ways.

You can use it to make better choices about pet food, and as it is open data, anyone can re-use it for any purpose.
