Manyan Yankunan Bincike

Bayananmu suna tallafawa fannoni daban-daban na bincike

Abinci Mai Gina Jiki & Lafiyar Jama'a

Masu bincike suna amfani da bayananmu don nazarin ingancin abinci mai gina jiki, tsarin abinci, da kuma hanyoyin magance matsalolin lafiyar jama'a. Bayanan Buɗaɗɗen Abinci sun taimaka sosai wajen bincike da kimantawa na Nutri-Score.

Tasirin Muhalli

Nazarin da aka yi kan sawun carbon, Eco-Score, da kuma dorewar muhalli na kayayyakin abinci suna amfana daga cikakken bayananmu na bayanai kan samfura.

Sarrafa Abinci

Bincike kan abinci mai matuƙar sarrafawa ta amfani da rarrabuwar NOVA ya dogara ne akan jerin sinadaran da muka tattara da kuma bayanan sarrafawa.

Kimiyyar Kwamfuta da AI

Koyon na'ura, gane hoto, sarrafa harshe na halitta, da ayyukan kimiyyar bayanai suna amfani da bayananmu da hotunanmu na buɗe.

Halayyar Masu Amfani

Nazarin da ke nazarin zaɓin abinci, tasirin lakabi, da kuma bayyana gaskiya a masana'antar abinci yana amfani da bayanan Buɗaɗɗen Bayanan Abinci.

Manufofin Abinci

Masu tsara manufofi da masu bincike suna nazarin ƙa'idodin abinci, buƙatun lakabi, da ayyukan masana'antu ta amfani da bayananmu masu gaskiya.

Albarkatu ga Masu Bincike

Yadda ake amfani da kuma ambaton Bayanan Abinci na Buɗewa

Samun damar bayanai

Buɗaɗɗen Bayani game da Abinci yana ba da hanyoyi da yawa don samun damar bayanai:

  • Fitar da bayanai daga rumbun adana bayanai: Sauke cikakken rumbun adana bayanai ta hanyoyi daban-daban. Duba shafin namu don ƙarin bayani.
  • API: Yi tambaya ta hanyar shirye-shiryen bayanai ta hanyar API ɗinmu mai cikakken bayani. Takardun API
  • Tsarin bincike: Bincika samfura kai tsaye a gidan yanar gizon mu tare da ingantattun hanyoyin bincike da tacewa.

Lasisi

Bayananmu a buɗe suke kuma ana iya sake amfani da su kyauta:

  • Ana samun bayanan a ƙarƙashin Lasisin Buɗe Bayanan Bayani (ODbL)
  • Abubuwan da ke cikin rumbun adana bayanai suna ƙarƙashin lasisin na Abubuwan da ke cikin rumbun adana bayanai (DbCL)
  • Hotunan samfurin suna samuwa a ƙarƙashin Creative Commons Attribution Attribution (CC BY-SA 3.0)

Duba Sharuɗɗa da ƙa'idojin amfani da sake amfani don cikakkun bayanai.

Data Quality

A matsayin rumbun adana bayanai na jama'a, ingancin bayanai na iya bambanta. Lokacin amfani da Buɗaɗɗen Bayani game da Abinci don bincike:

  • Yi la'akari da tacewa ta hanyar ma'aunin cikawa ko alamun ingancin bayanai
  • Tabbatar da mahimman bayanai idan daidaito yana da mahimmanci
  • Ku sani game da son zuciya a fannin ƙasa da na lokaci a cikin bayananmu
  • Tuntube mu idan kuna buƙatar taimako game da kimanta ingancin bayanai don binciken ku

Yi Aiki Tare Da Mu

Muna maraba da haɗin gwiwa da ƙungiyoyin bincike:

  • Bukatun bayanai: Sanar da mu idan kuna buƙatar takamaiman bayanai ko haɓakawa ga tsarin bayanan mu
  • Duba ɓangarorin: Shirya kamfen ɗin tattara bayanai da aka yi niyya don yankin bincikenku ko rukunin samfura
  • Raba sakamako: Raba abubuwan da ka gano ga al'ummarmu kuma ka taimaka wajen ci gaba da ilimi
  • Haɓaka Kayan Aiki: Yi aiki tare kan kayan aikin da ke amfanar masu bincike da masu amfani

Tuntube mu a contact@openfoodfacts.org domin tattauna damar yin aiki tare.

Tasirin Bincike

Buɗaɗɗen Bayani game da Abinci ya ba da gudummawa mai yawa ga binciken kimiyya da lafiyar jama'a:

  • Mujallu sama da 600 na kimiyya sun yi nuni da bayananmu
  • Manyan ƙungiyoyin bincike ciki har da EREN (Faransa), USP (Brazil), da Jami'ar Liège (Belgium) sun yi amfani da bayananmu.
  • Bayananmu sun goyi bayan bincike da ke haifar da canje-canjen manufofi da shawarwarin lafiyar jama'a
  • Mun ba da damar kwatantawa tsakanin ƙasashe da nazarin duniya kan ingancin abinci da abinci mai gina jiki
