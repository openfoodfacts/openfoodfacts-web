人工智能

你可能见过“为了产品好，我们使用人工智能”的口号。 为了食品好，我们使用人工智能。 :-) 在这里，人类贡献者和机器贡献者一起为提高食品信息透明度而努力。

营养成分表视图网络

Open Food Facts 使用人工智能来增强志愿者为共同利益所做的努力。

Nutri-Score vision network

Our vision algorithms are able to recognize a Nutri-Score in colors, black and white, and also recognize the right letter.

食品类别预测网络

我们试图根据已经掌握的关于该产品的各种数据来预测食品类别。 我们使用多种互补且务实的方法，包括 NLP、正则表达式和数据向量。

Robotoff

Robotoff 可以分析照片，并且根据图片中食品信息的确定性自行编辑 Open Food Facts，或者向人类提出有关食品信息的问题以进行验证。

What technologies ?

We have a very pragmatic approach to AI. The same problem can be solved by various techniques, depending on the data we already have for a specific product. Also, sometimes classic techniques like REGEX achieve the same result as sophisticated networks. So whether you're into NLP, CNN, Vision or anything else, there's likely an impact you can have.

Why Open Food Facts ?

If you are interested in Data science, computer vision, and artificial intelligence in general, Open Food Facts is a fantastic playground. We have a very large annotated datasets, users to ensure initial and continuous learning for the models, and a crack, agile AI volunteer team.
