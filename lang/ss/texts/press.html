Key Facts about Open Food Facts

Sikholwa ngalokucinile kutsi lwati ngaloko lesikudlako luyinshisekelo yemmango futsi kufanele lufinyeleleke kuwo wonkhe umuntfu ngekukhululeka. Yasungulwa nga 2012, i-Open Food Facts yinhlangano lengasebenti inzuzo ngemuva kwe-database lenkhulu levulekile mayelana nemikhicito yekudla emhlabeni.

Njengeluhlu lwemininingwane yekubhekisa, siyatigcabha kakhulu ngekutsi sisite ekwakheni emaphrojekthi langetulu kwa-200, tinhlelo tekusebenta kanye nemawebhusayithi emkhakheni wekudla. Ngekusebentisa kabusha luhlu lwetfu lwemininingwane, lamaphrojekthi enta kutsi ifinyeleleke kubantfu labanyenti emhlabeni wonkhe, ngaleyo ndlela kufaka sandla ekubekeni tintfo ngalokusebaleni lokukhulu kwekudla.
