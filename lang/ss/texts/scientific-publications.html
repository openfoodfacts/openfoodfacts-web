Tincwadzi Tesayensi

Emaciniso Ekudla Lavulekile elucwaningweni: tincwadzi, ticaphuni, kanye nemitfombolusito yebacwaningi

Kusukela nga 2012, Emaciniso Ekudla Lavulekile abe ngumtfombolusito lobalulekile kubacwaningi emhlabeni wonkhe labasebentisa idatha yetfu yekudla etifundvweni tekudla, imvelo, imphilo yemmango, kanye naletinye tindzawo letinyenti. Database yetfu levulekile icashunwe emakhulu emaphepha esayensi.

Kubacwaningi

Uyasebentisa noma ucabanga kusebentisa Emaciniso Ekudla Lokuvulekile njengencenye yephrojekthi yelucwaningo?

Sicela usatise ku tsintsana@kuvula emaciniso ekudla.org

Siyajabula kusita nekuhlanganyela nemacembu elucwaningo kute sibone kutsi singawusekela njani umsebenti wakho.

Tindzawo Letimcoka Telucwaningo

Idatha yetfu isekela tindzawo letehlukahlukene telucwaningo

Umsoco & Imphilo Yemmango

Bacwaningi basebentisa i-database yetfu kute bafundze lizinga lekudla, kwakheka kwekudla, kanye nekungenelela kwemphilo yemmango. Idatha yeMaciniso Ekudla Lokuvulekile ibe yintfo lebalulekile elucwaningweni nasekuhloleni kwe Nutri-Score.

Umtselela Wemvelo

Tifundvo nge-carbon footprint, i-Eco-Score, kanye nekusimama kwemvelo kwemikhicito yekudla tiyazuza ku-database yetfu lephelele yelwati lwemkhicito.

Kucubungula Kudla

Lucwaningo ngekudla lokucubungulwe kakhulu kusetjentiswa kuhlukaniswa kwe NOVA kutsembele etinhlu tetfu letinemininingwane yetitsako kanye nelwati lwekucubungula.

Isayensi Yemakhompyutha & I-AI

Kufundza ngemshini, kubona titfombe, kucubungula lulwimi lwemvelo, kanye nemaphrojekthi esayensi yedatha kukhutsata emasethi etfu e-data lavulekile kanye netitfombe.

Kutiphatsa Kwebatsengisi

Tifundvo letihlola kukhetsa kudla, imiphumela yekulebula, kanye nekuba sobala embonini yekudla kusebentisa idatha ye-Open Food Facts.

Inchubomgomo Yekudla

Abenti benchubomgomo kanye nebacwaningi bahlatiya imitsetfo yekudla, tidzingo tekulebula, kanye nemikhuba yemboni basebentisa idathabeyisi yetfu lesebaleni.

Tinsita Tebacwaningi

Indlela yekusebentisa nekucaphuna Emaciniso Ekudla Lokuvulekile .

Kufinyelela ku-Data

Emaciniso Ekudla Lavulekile aniketa tindlela letinyenti tekufinyelela idatha yetfu:

  • Kutfunyelwa kwemininingwane ngaphandle: Landa ema-dumps laphelele e-database ngetindlela letehlukahlukene. Bona likhasi letfu lemininingwane kute utfole imininingwane.
  • API: Buta idathabeyisi yetfu ngeluhlelo nge-API yetfu lephelele. Imibhalo ye-API
  • Sikhombisi-mbono sekuphenya: Hlola imikhicito ngco kuwebhusayithi yetfu lenemandla ekuphenya nekuhlunga lasetulu.

Kunika emalayisensi

Idatha yetfu ivulekile futsi iyaphindze isetjentiswe ngekukhululeka:

  • Lemininingwane iyatfolakala ngaphansi kwe Lilayisensi Lelivulekile Lemininingwane (ODbL)
  • Lokucuketfwe luhlu lwemininingwane kungaphansi kwe Lilayisensi Lekucuketfwe Luhlu lwemininingwane (DbCL)
  • Titfombe temkhicito tiyatfolakala ngaphansi kwe Kudala Lokufanako Kwekwabelana (CC BY-SA 3.0)

Bona Imigomo nemibandzela yetfu yekusebentisa kanye nekuphindze usebentise kute utfole imininingwane lephelele.

Data Quality

Njenge-database lehlanganisa bantfu labanyenti, lizinga ledatha lingahluka. Uma usebentisa Emaciniso Ekudla Lokuvulekile kwenta lucwaningo:

  • Cabanga ngekuhlunga ngemaphuzu laphelele noma tinkhomba telizinga ledatha
  • Cinisekisa emaphuzu emininingwane lebalulekile uma kuciniseka kubalulekile
  • Caphela kukhetsa kwendzawo kanye nekwesikhatsi ekusakateni kwetfu imininingwane
  • Xhumana natsi uma udzinga lusito ngekuhlola lizinga ledatha yelucwaningo lwakho

Bambisana natsi

Siyakwemukela kubambisana nemacembu elucwaningo:

  • Tidzingo tedatha: Satise nangabe udzinga idatha letsite nobe kwenta ncono sakhiwo setfu sedathabeyisi
  • Skena emacembu: Hlela imikhankhaso yekugcogca imininingwane lehlosiwe yesigodzi sakho selucwaningo noma sigaba semkhicito
  • Kwabelana ngemiphumela: Yabelana ngaloko lokutfolile nemmango wetfu futsi usite kutfutfukisa lwati
  • Kutfutfukiswa kwemathuluzi: Bambisana ngemathuluzi lazuzisa bobabili bacwaningi kanye nebatsengisi

Xhumana natsi ku kuchumana@openfoodfacts.org kute sicoce ngematfuba ekubambisana.
