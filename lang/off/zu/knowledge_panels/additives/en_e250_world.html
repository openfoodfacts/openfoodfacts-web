I-sodium nitrite (E250) usawoti osetshenziselwa ukwelapha nokugcina inyama imikhiqizo.

Idlala indima ebalulekile ekuvimbeleni ukukhula kwe-Clostridium botulinum, igciwane elibangela i-botulism, enyameni eselashiwe efana no- ubhekeni, i-ham, namasoseji. Iphinde inikeze isici sombala ophinki kanye nokunambitheka.

Nokho, ama-nitrites angasabela nama-amine esiswini ukuze akhe ama-nitrosamine, angu-ama-carcinogens anamandla. Ukudla okuphezulu kwenyama egayiwe equkethe ama-nitrites kuhlotshaniswa nengozi eyandayo yomdlavuza we-colorectal. Izindikimba ezilawulayo zibeke imikhawulo eqinile ekusetshenzisweni kwayo ekudleni.
