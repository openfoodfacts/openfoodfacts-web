I-cochineal extract noma i-carmine (E120) umbala obomvu wemvelo osuselwe emizimbeni eyomisiwe yesinambuzane sesifazane.

Isetshenziswa kakhulu ukudlulisa umbala obomvana, obomvu, noma onsomi ekudleni okuhlukahlukene nemikhiqizo yesiphuzo, okufaka iyogathi, okhilimu baka-ayisikhilimu, isinkwa esimuncu, namajusi wezithelo .

Nakuba kuyisithako semvelo, i-E120 yaziwa ngokubangela okubi ukungezwani komzimba, okuhlanganisa i-anaphylaxis, kwabanye abantu. I- futhi ayifanele abantu abadla imifino noma imifino ngenxa yemvelaphi yesinambuzane .
