I-Aspartame (E951) iyi-sweetener yokwenziwa enekhalori ephansi eyakhiwe ama-amino acid amabili .

Isetshenziswa esikhundleni sikashukela ezinkulungwaneni zemikhiqizo "engenashukela" noma "yokudla" , njengeziphuzo ezibandayo ezine-carbonated, ishungamu, ama-yogurts, kanye nama-sweeteners angu- , ukunikeza ubumnandi ngaphandle kwamakholori kashukela.

Naphezu kokuba esinye sezithasiselo zokudla ezivivinywe kanzima, ukuphepha kwakho okungu- kusalokhu kuyimpikiswano. Nakuba izindikimba ezinkulu ezilawulayo ezifana ne-FDA kanye ne-EFSA ikubona kuphephile emphakathini jikelele, i-IARC iyifake kusigaba njengokuthi "okungenzeka ukuthi i-carcinogenic kubantu" (Iqembu 2B). Kufanele futhi igwenywe abantu abanesimo sofuzo phenylketonuria (PKU).
