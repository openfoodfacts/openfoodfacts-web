I-Monosodium glutamate (MSG), noma i-E621, ingusawoti wesodium we-glutamic acid, i-amino acid etholakala ngokwemvelo ekudleni okuningi.

Isetshenziswa kakhulu njengesithuthukisi ukunambitheka ukuhlinzeka "umami" noma ukunambitheka okunezinhlobonhlobo zokudla okugayiwe, okuhlanganisa amasobho, ama-snack chips, kanye nokudla okufriziwe.

I-MSG ivamise ukubonwa njengephephile (GRAS) yiziphathimandla zokudla. Nokho, abanye abantu babika ukuzwela ku-MSG, baba nezimpawu zesikhathi esifushane njengokuphathwa yikhanda nokushaywa, nakuba ucwaningo lwesayensi lungakabonisi isixhumanisi esingashintshi.
