I-Tartrazine (E102) idayi ye-azo yokwenziwa kalamula-ophuzi esetshenziswa njengombala wokudla .

Itholakala enhlobonhlobo yemikhiqizo efana neziphuzo ezibandayo, ama-dessert, amaswidi angu- , kanye nokudla okulula ukuwanikeza ukubukeka okuphuzi okunempilo.

Nakuba kuvunywe ama-ejensi amaningi alawulayo, i-tartrazine iye yaxhunyaniswa nokungezwani nokungezwani komzimba, njengezinyosi, engxenyeni encane yabantu . Olunye ucwaningo luphinde lwaphakamisa isixhumanisi kokuthi ukwanda ekusebenzeni ngokweqile ezinganeni, ikakhulukazi uma kusetshenziswe nezinye izithasiselo ezifana nama-benzoate.
