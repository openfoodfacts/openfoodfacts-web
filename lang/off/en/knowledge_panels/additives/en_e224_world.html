Potassium metabisulfite (E224) is a preservative and antioxidant used in the food industry.

It is commonly added to alcoholic beverages (responsible for 85.4% of sulfite intake), as well as to industrial sauces to prevent oxidation and deterioration.

Two recent large-scale epidemiological studies (NutriNet-Sant√© cohort, 2025) have suggested links between its consumption and increased risks of chronic diseases: * An 11% increase in overall cancer incidence and 20% increase in breast cancer was observed in highest consumers compared to lowest consumers. * A 29% increase in type 2 diabetes risk was observed in highest consumers.

Sulfites can cause allergic reactions in some people, particularly respiratory symptoms in individuals with asthma.
