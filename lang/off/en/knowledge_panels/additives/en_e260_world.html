Acetic acid (E260), also known as vinegar, is a natural preservative and acidulant widely used in the food industry.

It is widely present in fats and sauces (dressings, condiments), as well as in processed meats, dairy preparations, and prepared dishes.

While these results require confirmation, acetic acid is generally recognized as safe when used in moderation in foods.
