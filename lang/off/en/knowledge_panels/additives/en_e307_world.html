Alpha-tocopherol (E307), a form of vitamin E, is a natural antioxidant used in the food industry.

It is added to foods to prevent fat oxidation and extend shelf life, particularly in oils, margarines, baked goods, and cereals.

While vitamin E is essential for health, these results suggest that its use as a food additive may require re-evaluation. Alpha-tocopherol naturally present in foods remains beneficial for health.
