Tartrazine (E102) i dayi ya azo yotshuka ya lemon leyi endliweke tani hi muhlovo wa swakudya .

Yi kumeka eka switirhisiwa swohambana hambana swofana na swakunwa swo kufumela, ti dessert, ti candies, na swakudya swo olova kuva swinyika xivumbeko xotshuka lexi hanyaka.

Hambi leswi yi pfumeleriweke hi tiejensi to tala to lawula, tartrazine yi hlanganisiwile na allergic reactions, ku fana na hives, eka xiphemu lexitsongo xa vaaki. Mindzavisiso yin’wana yi tlhele yi ringanyeta ku yelana na ku tlakuka ka hyperactivity eka vana, ngopfu ngopfu loko swi dyiwa na swin’wana swiengeteri swo fana na ti benzoates.
