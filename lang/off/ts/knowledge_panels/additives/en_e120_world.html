Cochineal extract kumbe carmine (E120) i muhlovo wo tshwuka wa ntumbuluko lowu humaka kusuka eka mimiri leyi omisiweke ya xitsotswana xa xisati xa cochineal.

Yi tirhisiwa ngopfu ku nyika muhlovo wa pink, wo tshwuka, kumbe purple eka swakudya swo hambana hambana na switirhisiwa swa swakunwa, ku katsa na ti yogurts, ice creams, confectionery, na juzi ya mihandzu.

Hambi leswi ku nga xiengeteri xa ntumbuluko, E120 yi tiveka ku vanga switlhavelo swo tika swa swa ku nga twanani, ku katsa na anaphylaxis, eka vanhu van'wana. Yi nakambe a yi ringanelanga eka vadyi va matsavu kumbe va vegans hikokwalaho ka masungulo ya yona ya switsotswana .
