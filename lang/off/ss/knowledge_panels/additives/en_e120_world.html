Sicumbi se Cochineal noma icarmine (E120) ngumbala lobovu wemvelo losuselwa ku kusuka emitimbeni leyomile yesinambuzane se cochineal lesinsikati.

Isetjentiswa kakhulu kuniketa umbala lopinki, lobovu, noma lobukhwebeletana ekudleni lokuhlukahlukene kanye nemikhicito yekunatsa, kufaka ekhatsi ema yogurt, ema ayisikhilimu, ema confectionery, kanye ne emajusi etitselo.

Noma nje kuyintfo leyengetiwe yemvelo, i-E120 yatiwa ngekubangela kugula lokukhulu , kufaka ekhatsi i-anaphylaxis, kulabanye bantfu. I futsi ayikulungeli bantfu labadla imifino noma labadla imifino ngenca yemvelaphi yayo yetinambuzane .
