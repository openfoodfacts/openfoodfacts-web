I-Tartrazine (E102) ngudayi we-azo lomtfubi-welamula-wekwentiwa losetjentiswa njengekudla umbala.

Itfolakala emikhicitweni leyehlukahlukene lefana netiphuzo letinongotelako, ema dessert, emaswidi, kanye nekudla lokulula kwentela kutsi kubukeke kumtfubi lokuphilako.

Ngesikhatsi ivunywe ngema-ejensi lamanyenti lalawulako, i-tartrazine ihlanganiswe ne kugula, njengetindlu tetindlu, engcenyeni lencane yebantfu laba . Letinye tifundvo tiphindze taphakamisa kuchumana ne kwandziswa kwe kusebenta kakhulu ebantfwaneni, ikakhulukati uma kudliwa naletinye tintfo letingetiwe njenge benzoates.
