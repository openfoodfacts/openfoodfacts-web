I-MSG, noma i-E621, luswayi lwe-sodium lwe-esidi ye-glutamic , i-amino acid letfolakala ngekwemvelo ekudleni lokunyenti.

Isetjentiswa kakhulu njengentfo lekhutsata kunambitseka kuniketa "umami" noma kunambitseka lokunongotelako ekudleni lokuhlukahlukene lokucubunguliwe, kufaka ekhatsi emasobho, emachips ekudla lokulula, kanye nekudla lokufriziwe .

I-MSG ivame kutsatfwa njengalephephile (GRAS) tiphatsimandla tekudla. Kepha, labanye bantfu babika kuzwela ku-MSG, bahlangabetana netimphawu tesikhatsi lesifishane njengekuphatfwa yinhloko kanye nekuvuvuka, noma tifundvo tesayensi atizange tikhombise sixhumanisi lesihambisanako.
