I-Aspartame (E951) ngumnandzi lofakelwako lonekhalori lephansi lowakhiwe ngema-amino esidi lamabili .

Isetjentiswa njengesikhundla sashukela etinkulungwaneni temikhicito "lengenashukela" noma "yekudla" , njengetiphuzo letine-carbonated, i-chewing gum, ema-yogurt, kanye ne- tintfo letinongotelako letisetafuleni, kuniketa bumnandzi bashukela ngaphandle kwemakhalori.

Noma ngabe ngulenye yetintfo letingetiwe tekudla letihlolwe kakhulu, kuphepha kwayo kuhlala kuphikisana. Ngesikhatsi imitimba lemikhulu lecondzisako njenge FDA kanye ne EFSA ikubona kuphephile emmangweni jikelele, i-IARC ikuhlukanise ngekutsi "kungaba nemdlavuza kubantfu" (Licembu 2B). Kufanele futsi kugwenywe bantfu labanesimo sefutfo le-phenylketonuria (PKU).
