Monosodium glutamate (MSG), anaa E621, yɛ sodium nkyene a ɛwɔ glutamic acid, amino acid a wohu wɔ abɔde mu wɔ nnuan pii mu.

Wɔde di dwuma kɛse sɛ ade a ɛma ɛyɛ dɛ de ma "umami" anaasɛ dɛ a ɛyɛ dɛ ma nnuan ahorow a wɔayɛ, a soup, snack chips, ne nnuan a wɔahyɛ no nwini ka ho.

MSG yɛ nea aduan ho mpanyimfo gye tom sɛ ahobammɔ wom (GRAS). Nanso, nnipa binom bɔ amanneɛ sɛ wɔte nka wɔ MSG ho, wonya bere tiaa mu sɛnkyerɛnne te sɛ tiyɛ ne flushing, ɛwom sɛ nyansahu mu nhwehwɛmu nkyerɛɛ abusuabɔ a ɛkɔ so daa de.
