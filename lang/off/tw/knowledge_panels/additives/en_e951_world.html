Aspartame (E951) yɛ aduru a wɔde yɛ dɛ a ahoɔdennuru kakraa bi na ɛwom a amino acid abien na ɛwom.

Wɔde si asikre ananmu wɔ nneɛma mpempem pii a "asikre nnim" anaa "aduan" mu, te sɛ anonne a ɛyɛ dɛ a carbonated wom, chewing gum, yogurts, ne tabletop sweeteners, de ma dɛ a asikre mu calories nni mu.

Ɛmfa ho sɛ ɛyɛ nnuan a wɔde ka ho a wɔasɔ ahwɛ denneennen no mu biako no, ne ahobammɔ da so ara yɛ akyinnyegye. Bere a mmarahyɛ bagua atitiriw te sɛ FDA ne EFSA bu no sɛ ɛyɛ nea ahobammɔ wom ma ɔmanfo nyinaa no, IARC akyekyɛ mu sɛ "ebia ɛde kokoram ba nnipa" (Kuw 2B). Ɛsɛ sɛ ankorankoro a wɔwɔ awosu mu tebea phenylketonuria (PKU) nso kwati .
