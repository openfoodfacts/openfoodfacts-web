• ***What it is: Easter chocolates are a category of confectionery specifically produced and marketed for the Easter holiday season. They typically feature chocolate molded into symbolic shapes associated with Easter, such as eggs, bunnies (rabbits), bells, chicks, and lambs. Primarily made from milk, dark, or white chocolate, they are a traditional part of Easter celebrations and gift-giving in many cultures, often linked to themes of spring, rebirth, and festivities.
