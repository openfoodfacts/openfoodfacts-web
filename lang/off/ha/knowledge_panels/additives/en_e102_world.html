Tartrazine (E102) lemun tsami-rawaya ce rini na roba da ake amfani dashi azaman abinci canza launi.

Ana samunsa a cikin nau'o'in samfura iri-iri kamar abubuwan sha masu laushi, kayan zaki, alewa, da abincin ciye-ciye don ba su haske mai rawaya.

Yayin da hukumomi da yawa suka amince da su, an danganta tartrazine zuwa halolin rashin lafiyan, kamar amya, a cikin ƙaramin yanki na yawan jama'a. Wasu nazarin kuma sun ba da shawarar hanyar haɗi zuwa ** ƙara hyperactivity a cikin yara**, musamman idan aka sha tare da sauran ƙari kamar benzoates.
