Cochineal tsantsa ko carmine (E120) wani nau'in launin ja ne na halitta wanda aka samo daga busassun jikin kwarin mata.

Ana amfani da shi sosai don ba da launin ruwan hoda, ja, ko shunayya ga abinci daban-daban da kayan sha, gami da yogurts, ice creams, kayan zaki, da ruwan 'ya'yan itace.

Ko da yake Æ™ari ne na halitta, E120 an san shi yana haifar da ** matsananciyar rashin lafiyan rashin lafiyar jiki, gami da anaphylaxis, a wasu mutane. Yana da shima bai dace da masu cin ganyayyaki ko masu cin ganyayyaki ba** saboda kwarin asalinsa.
