Sodium nitrite (E250) gishiri ne da ake amfani da shi don warkewa da adana nama kayayyakin.

Yana taka muhimmiyar rawa wajen hana ci gaban Clostridium botulinum, kwayoyin da ke da alhakin botulism, a cikin nama da aka warke kamar naman alade, naman alade, da tsiran alade. Hakanan yana ba da yanayin launin ruwan hoda da dandano.

Duk da haka, nitrites na iya amsawa tare da amines a cikin ciki don samar da nitrosamines, wadanda sune ** masu karfin carcinogens . Yawan cin naman da aka sarrafa naman da ke ɗauke da nitrites yana da alaƙa da ƙarin haɗarin cancer colorectal**. Hukumomin tsaro sun ƙulla ƙayyadaddun iyaka kan amfani da a abinci.
