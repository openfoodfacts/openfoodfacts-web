Sodium nitrite (E250) munyu unoshandiswa kurapa nekuchengetedza nyama zvigadzirwa.

Inobata basa rakakosha mukudzivirira kukura kweClostridium botulinum, bhakitiriya rinokonzera botulism, munyama yakarapwa se bacon, ham, uye soseji. Inopawo hunhu hwepingi ruvara uye kuravira.

Zvisinei, nitrites inogona kuita nemaamine mudumbu kuti iite nitrosamines, iyo ** inokonzera carcinogens . Kudya kwakanyanya kweakagadzirwa nyama ine nitrites inosanganiswa ne ** yakawedzera njodzi ye colorectal cancer . Masangano anodzora akaisa miganho yakasimba pa kushandiswa kwayo muchikafu.
