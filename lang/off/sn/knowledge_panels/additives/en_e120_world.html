Cochineal inobvisa kana carmine (E120) ndeye yakasikwa tsvuku colorant inotorwa kubva kumiviri yakaomeswa yechikadzi cochineal tumbuyu.

Inoshandiswa zvakanyanya kupa ruvara rwepingi, dzvuku, kana ruvara rwepepuru kune dzakasiyana siyana chikafu uye zvigadzirwa zvezvinwiwa, zvinosanganisira yoghurt, ice cream, confectionery, uye muto wemichero.

Kunyange zvazvo chiri chekuwedzera, E120 inozivikanwa kuti inokonzera yakakomba allergic reactions , kusanganisira anaphylaxis, mune vamwe vanhu. Iyo zvakare **isina kukodzera kune vanodya miriwo kana vegans ** nekuda kwehupembenene hwayo kwakabva.
