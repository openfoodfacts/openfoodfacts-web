Aspartame (E951) ndeye yakaderera-calori artificial sweetener inoumbwa maviri amino acids.

Inoshandiswa sechinotsiva shuga muzviuru zve "zvisina shuga" kana "kudya" zvigadzirwa, senge carbonated soft drink, chewing gum, yoghurts, uye tabletop sweeteners, kupa kutapira kusina macalories eshuga.

Pasinei nekuve imwe yezvakanyanya kuedzwa zvinowedzerwa zvekudya, iyo chengetedzo inoramba ichinetsa. Nepo masangano makuru anodzora seFDA uye EFSA ichiona yakachengeteka kune veruzhinji, IARC yakaiisa se "zvichida carcinogenic kuvanhu" (Boka 2B). Inofanirwawo kuve kudziviswa nevanhu vane genetic mamiriro phenylketonuria (PKU).
