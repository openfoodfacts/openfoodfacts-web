• Zvazviri: Tsananguro iyi inoratidza kuti mutambo unoreva kana kuratidza kushandiswa kwezvinodhaka, doro, kana fodya zvisiri pamutemo.
  • Mazinga Ekuoma:
  • Kurudziro/Kuratidza Kushandiswa (PEGI 16): Kurudziro yekushandisa fodya kana doro (semuenzaniso, hunhu hunowedzera mukana) kana kuratidzwa kwekushandisa zvinodhaka zvisiri pamutemo.
  • Glamorization/Promotion (PEGI 18): Mifananidzo inoyevedza yezvinodhaka zvisiri pamutemo kana kusimudzirwa kwakatanhamara kwezvinhu zvinodzorwa.
  • Minimum Rating: Tsananguro iyi inongowanikwa pamitambo yakatarwaPEGI 16 kana PEGI 18.
  • Mabviro: PEGI Yepamutemo Webhusaiti, Games Rating Authority
