• Zvazviri: Tsananguro iyi inoratidza kuti mutambo uyu une mifananidzo yemarudzi, chitendero, nyika, kana mamwe mafungiro anogona kukurudzira ruvengo.
  • Mashoko: Kuyera uku kunoitwa kana mutambo uchisimudzira kana kurumbidza rusarura kumapoka zvichienderana nekwakabva, chitendero, nyika, murume kana mukadzi, kana humwe hunhu.
  • Minimum Rating: Iyi tsananguro inowanzoitika pamitambo yakarongwa PEGI 18, nekuda kwekuomarara kwezvakadai.
