• Zvazviri: Tsananguro iyi inoratidza kuti mutambo une zvinhu zvinokurudzira kana kudzidzisa kubhejera.
  • Chikamu: Zvinoreva kuenzanisa kwemitambo yechinyakare yemukana inowanzowanikwa mumakasino kana muhoro dzekubhejera (semuenzaniso, mitambo yemakadhi inotambirwa mari, kubheja pamijaho).
  • Minimum Rating: Tsananguro iyi inongowanikwa pamitambo yakatarwa PEGI 12, 16, kana 18*. Kubva muna 2020, mitambo ichangobva kupihwa miyero ine echokwadi yekubhejera simulations inogara iri PEGI 18.
  • Cherechedzo paRoot Boxes: Kuvapo kwe "Paid Random Items" (inowanzo kunzi mabhokisi ekubhejera) itsananguro yemukati yakaparadzana ("Mu-Game Mutambo") uye zera reKutenga PE (7 zera rinogona kuwanikwa mumitambo yeGI).
