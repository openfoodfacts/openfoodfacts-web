Add products

Shandisa Open Food Facts app ye Android kana iPhone kuti utarise zviri nyore barcode yezvigadzirwa kubva kumba kwako kana zvitoro zvemuno woisa mapikicha ezvinyorwa zvazvo.

Iwo nharembozha akabatanidzwa, saka unogona ikozvino kuongorora zvigadzirwa, chikafu, zvekuzora, uye chikafu chemhuka kubva kune imwecheteyo Open Chikafu Chokwadi app!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Taratidza Vhura Zvigadzirwa Chokwadi kuvateereri vakasiyana kwazvo munzvimbo dzakasiyana kwazvo, uye tinogara tichiwana mhinduro inechido kana tichiratidza zvingaitwe kana data rezvigadzirwa rakavhurwa. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Tinogona kushanda pamwe chete kutsigira nekuwedzera mavara ezvakatipoteredza, zvibodzwa zvekugadzirisa, uye zviyero zvechigadzirwa.

Start or join a local contributors community: add local products, recruit friends, present the project in local meetups and conferences etc.

Expert help needed

Kugadzira Vhura Zvigadzirwa Chokwadi zvinodawo ruzivo rwakanyanya uye hunyanzvi munzvimbo dzakawanda dzakasiyana:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    Tinoda rubatsiro rwekuvaka zvirinani mushandisi ruzivo pawebhusaiti yeVhura Zvigadzirwa Chokwadi uye nharembozha, kuvandudza dhizaini yavo, kugadzira zvinyorwa zvinokurudzira zvekuratidzira nezvimwe.

  • Development

    Tine basa rakawanda rekusimudzira rekuita: paOpen Products Facts backend (Perl neMongoDB), API (JSON), webhusaiti (HTML5, JS, Foundation), uye nharembozha. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Zvakanyanya kukosha, pane zvinhu zvakawanda zvinotonhorera izvo vashandisi, vabatsiri uye vashandisi vanoita neVhura Zvigadzirwa Chokwadi, zvingave zvakanaka kuti vanhu vazhinji vazive nezvazvo. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    Pane zvakawanda zvinonakidza uye zvepakutanga zvekushandisa zvechigadzirwa zvakavhurika data zvataigona kushanda pazviri masangano ezvakatipoteredza, vagadziri, vaongorori, mayunivhesiti, zvikoro, NGOs nezvimwe. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    Unogona kukurukura nenharaunda pane yedu forum. Inzvimbo yakanaka yekubvunza mibvunzo, kugovana mazano, uye kubatana pamapurojekiti.

  • Wiki

    Tine zvakare wiki yatinoshandisa kunyora nekubatana Open Products Chokwadi nemapurojekiti ayo madiki. Isu tinoshandisawo wiki kubata shanduro uye kushanda pa taxonomies / hierarchies yezvikamu, zvinyorwa nezvimwe.

Kune dzimwe nzira dzakawanda dzekupa kuVhura Zvigadzirwa Chokwadi, ndapota tibatane nesu paSlack uye ngatitange nhaurirano!
