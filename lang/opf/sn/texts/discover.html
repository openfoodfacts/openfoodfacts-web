Ita sarudzo dzakasimba dzechigadzirwa

Knowledge is power. Ruzivo rwechigadzirwa isimba rekudya zvirinani!

  • Environmental Impact

    Discover the CO2 impact of products and make informed decisions about your environmental footprint. Open Products Facts helps you understand the environmental cost of the things you buy.

  • Repairability Score

    Check the repairability score of electronics and appliances. Tsigira hupfumi hwakatenderera nekusarudza zvigadzirwa zvinogona kugadziriswa pane kuchinjwa.

  • Find products

    Yedu yekutsvaga fomu inosanganisira akawanda emaitiro kuitira kuti iwe uwane zvigadzirwa zvinoenderana nemamiriro ako ekunze uye kuenderera.

Zvigadzirwa zvekuvandudza nesainzi

Our data can be reused for education, new products and services, research and more!

Iyo Vhura Zvigadzirwa Chokwadi dhatabhesi yakavhurika data yakaburitswa pasi peiyo Open Database Rezinesi. Anyone can use it for any purpose - commercial or non-commercial - provided the source of the data is attributed and improvements to the database are shared in a similar way.
