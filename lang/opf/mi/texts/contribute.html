Everyone can contribute

Me whai waahi ki a maatau mahi tahi, kore utu me te tuwhera papaa raraunga o nga hua puta noa i te ao!

Ko te Open Products Facts he kaupapa kore-painga i mahia katoatia e nga kaitūao, e hiahia ana matou ki a koe.

Add products

Whakamahia te taupānga Open Food Facts mo Android ranei iPhone kia ngawari te matawai i te tohupae o nga hua mai i to kaainga, i nga toa o to rohe me te tuku pikitia o o raatau tapanga.

Kua hanumihia nga taupānga waea, no reira ka taea e koe te matawai i nga hua, kai, whakapaipai, me nga kai mokai mai i te taupānga Open Food Facts kotahi!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Tāutahia te taupānga hei whai wāhi ki te mārama o ngā hua

Tell the world

He pai ki a koe nga Tuwhera Hua Meka? Tell others about it!

Ka taea e koe te whakaatu i te kaupapa ki to whanau me o hoa, whakaatu ki a raatau me pehea te whakauru i te taupānga me te koha, tuhia he panui rangitaki, tohatoha i nga Hua Tuwhera Meka ki te paapori paapori...

And present it live! We have presented Open Products Facts to very different audiences in very different settings, and we always get a very enthusiastic response when we show what can be done when the data for products is open. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Ka taea e tatou te mahi tahi ki te tautoko me te taapiri i nga tapanga taiao a-rohe, nga tohu whakatikatika, me nga paerewa hua.

Start or join a local contributors community: add local products, recruit friends, present the project in local meetups and conferences etc.

Expert help needed

Ko te Whakawhanake i nga Hua Tuwhera meka me whai matauranga motuhake me nga tohungatanga ki nga waahi maha:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    Me awhina matou ki te hanga i tetahi wheako kaiwhakamahi pai ake i runga i te paetukutuku Open Products Facts me te taupānga pūkoro, ki te whakapai atu to ratou hoahoa, ki te hanga rauemi whakaaturanga whai hua aha atu.

  • Development

    He maha nga mahi whanaketanga hei mahi: i runga i te Open Products Facts backend (Perl and MongoDB), API (JSON), pae tukutuku (HTML5, JS, Foundation), me te taupānga waea. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    More seriously, there are lot of cool things that users, contributors and reusers do with Open Products Facts, it would be great to get more people to know about it. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    There are lots of interesting and original applications of product open data that we could work on with environmental agencies, manufacturers, researchers, universities, schools, NGOs etc. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    Ka taea e koe te korerorero me te hapori i runga i ta maatau huinga huinga. He waahi pai ki te patapatai, ki te tiri whakaaro, ki te mahi tahi ki nga kaupapa.

  • Wiki

    Kei a matou ano he wiki ka whakamahia e matou ki te tuhi tahi i nga Tuwhera Hua Meka me ona kaupapa-iti. We also use the wiki to manage translations and to work on the taxonomies / hierarchies of categories, labels etc.

He maha atu ano nga huarahi ki te koha ki te Open Products Facts, tena koa uru mai ki a matou i runga i te Slack ka timata te korerorero!
