Hua mo te auaha me te pūtaiao

Our data can be reused for education, new products and services, research and more!

Ko te papaaa raraunga Open Products Facts he raraunga tuwhera i whakaputaina i raro i te Raihana Raihana Tuwhera. Anyone can use it for any purpose - commercial or non-commercial - provided the source of the data is attributed and improvements to the database are shared in a similar way.
