All you need is a scan-do attitude! :-)

An féidir linn faisnéis faoi tháirgí a dhéanamh níos trédhearcaí agus níos inbhuanaithe? Yes we scan!

Everyone can contribute

Bí i do bhall dár mbunachar sonraí comhoibríoch, saor in aisce agus oscailte de tháirgí ó gach cearn den domhan!

Is tionscadal neamhbhrabúis é Open Products Facts atá déanta go hiomlán ag oibrithe deonacha, tá tú de dhíth orainn.

Add products

Bain úsáid as an aip Open Food Facts le haghaidh Android nó iPhone chun barrachód táirgí ó do theach nó ó shiopaí áitiúla a scanadh go héasca agus pictiúir dá lipéid a uaslódáil.

Tá na haipeanna soghluaiste cumasctha, mar sin is féidir leat táirgí, bia, cosmaidí agus bia peataí a scanadh anois ón aip chéanna Open Food Facts!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Suiteáil an aip chun cur le trédhearcacht táirgí

Tell the world

An maith leat Fíricí faoi Tháirgí Oscailte? Tell others about it!

Is féidir leat an tionscadal a chur i láthair do do theaghlach agus do chairde, a thaispeáint dóibh conas an aip a shuiteáil agus cur leis, post blag a scríobh, Facts faoi Tháirgí Oscailte a roinnt ar na meáin shóisialta...

And present it live! Chuireamar Fíricí Táirgí Oscailte i láthair lucht féachana an-difriúil i suíomhanna an-difriúla, agus faighimid freagra an-díograiseach i gcónaí nuair a thaispeánaimid cad is féidir a dhéanamh nuair a bhíonn na sonraí le haghaidh táirgí oscailte. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Is féidir linn oibriú le chéile chun tacú le lipéid chomhshaoil áitiúla, scóir in-deisiúcháin agus caighdeáin táirgí agus iad a chur leis.

Start or join a local contributors community: add local products, recruit friends, present the project in local meetups and conferences etc.

Expert help needed

Éilíonn forbairt Fíricí Táirgí Oscailte eolas agus saineolas speisialaithe i go leor réimsí éagsúla freisin:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Dearadh

    We need help to build a better user experience on the Open Products Facts web site and mobile app, to improve their design, to create impactful presentation materials etc.

  • Forbairt

    Tá go leor oibre forbartha le déanamh againn: ar chúlra Open Products Facts (Perl agus MongoDB), API (JSON), suíomh gréasáin (HTML5, JS, Foundation), agus an aip shoghluaiste. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Níos dáiríre fós, tá a lán rudaí fionnuara a dhéanann úsáideoirí, rannpháirtithe agus athúsáideoirí le Open Products Facts, bheadh sé iontach níos mó daoine a chur ar an eolas faoi. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Tionscadail speisialta

    There are lots of interesting and original applications of product open data that we could work on with environmental agencies, manufacturers, researchers, universities, schools, NGOs etc. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    Is féidir leat plé a dhéanamh leis an bpobal ar ár bhfóram . Is áit iontach í chun ceisteanna a chur, smaointe a roinnt, agus comhoibriú ar thionscadail.

  • Wiki

    Tá vicí againn freisin a úsáidimid chun Open Products Facts agus a fhothionscadail a dhoiciméadú go comhoibríoch. We also use the wiki to manage translations and to work on the taxonomies / hierarchies of categories, labels etc.

There are many more ways to contribute to Open Products Facts, please join us on Slack and let's start the discussion!
