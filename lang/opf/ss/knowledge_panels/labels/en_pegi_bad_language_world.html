• Yini: Lenchazelo ikhombisa kutsi lomdlalo unelulwimi loluhlambalatako noma inhlamba.
  • Emazinga eBumatima:
  • Kumnene (PEGI 12): Kuthuka lokumnene kanye/noma lulwimi loluhlambalatako, lokufishane kwemagama lahlambalatako ekuya ecasini (sib., "kuhlambalata," "sihogo," "umbhedo").
  • Kunemandla (PEGI 16/18): Emagama lahlambalatako ngekwelicansi, kuhlambalata, noma lulwimi loluhlambalatako kakhulu.
  • Umtselela: Kuba khona kanye nemandla elulwimi lolubi kuyakhula ngekulinganisa kweminyaka lephakeme ye-PEGI.
  • Imithombo: Iwebhusayithi Lesemtsetfweni Ye-PEGI, Ligunya Lekukala Imidlalo
