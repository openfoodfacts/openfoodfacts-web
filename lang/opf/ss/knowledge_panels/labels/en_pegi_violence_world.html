• Yini: Lenchazelo ikhombisa kutsi lomdlalo unemifanekiso yebudlova.
  • Emazinga eBumatima:
  • **Budlova lobuncane kakhulu, lobungasibo mbamba (sib., kuhlambalata, kulimata lokufana nekhathuni, noma budlova lobucondziswe kubalingisi labacabangako.
  • Lobulinganiselwe (PEGI 12): Budlova lobubonakalako kakhulu noma lobukhona mbamba kubalingisi labacabangako, noma budlova lobungasilo mbamba kubalingisi labafana nebantfu (sib., kulimala lokuncane, kute ingati).
  • Kunemandla (PEGI 16): Budlova lobungibo lobubhekiswe kubalingiswa bebantfu, kufaka ekhatsi kuveta kufa nekulimala. Budlova lobunegazi noma lobunengati bungakhona kuba khona uma ngabe busesitayeleni se-arcade.
  • Gross (PEGI 18): Tindlela letetfusako tekuletsa kufa noma kulimala lokukhulu, kufaka ekhatsi kuhlukunyetwa, kuncunywa inhloko, kuncunywa ticu, noma ludlame lolungenasizatfu lolubhekiswe kubalingisi labasengotini/labamsulwa. Budlova bekuya ecasini kanye nekusongelwa nako kuhlukaniswe lapha.
  • Umtselela: Uma silinganiso seminyaka ye-PEGI lehambisanako siphakeme, budlova lobuvetwako bucinile futsi bukhona mbamba.
