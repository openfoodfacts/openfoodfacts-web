• Yini: Lenchazelo ikhombisa kutsi lomdlalo ucuketse imifanekiso yebuhlanga, yenkholo, buve, noma letinye tintfo letingakhutsata inzondo.
  • Ingcikitsi: Lesilinganiso sisetjentiswa nangabe lomdlalo ugcugcutela nobe udvumisa kubandlululwa kwemacembu ngekuya ngemvelaphi, inkholo, buve, bulili, bulili, nobe letinye timphawu.
  • Silinganiso Lesincane: Lenchazelo ivame kuvela emidlalweni lelinganiswe PEGI 18, ngenca yesimo lesibucayi saloko lokucuketfwe.
