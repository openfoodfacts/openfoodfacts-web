• Yini: Lokucuketfwe kulungele bantfu labaneminyaka lengu 12 kuya etulu.
  • Timphawu Letimcoka:
  • Budlova: Budlova lobubonakalako kakhulu noma lobukhona mbamba kubalingisi labacabangako, noma budlova lobungasibo mbamba kubalingisi labafana nebantfu. Loku kungafaka ekhatsi umtselela lobonakala kakhulu, kepha ngalokuvamile ngaphandle kwe gore. Budlova lobushiwo kubantfu lapho khona budlova mbamba bungakhonjiswa nako kuyahlanganiswa.
  • Kwesaba/Kwesaba: Kusongela lokucinile, kwesaba, noma imininingwane yekulimala lokubonakalako, kanye nekulandzelana kwekwesaba lokusimeme kakhulu noma titfombe letiphazamisako.
  • Lulwimi: Kuthuka lokuncane kanye/noma lulwimi loluhlambalatako (lolufishane lwekuhlambalata ngekwelicasi).
  • Kuya ecasini: Kukhuluma ngekuya ecasini noma kuhlala ngekwelicasi kungaba khona. Kungcunu lokuvusa inkhanuko noma kuya ecasini lokungakacaci.
  • Kugembula: Kungafaka ekhatsi tintfo letikhutsata noma letifundzisa kugembula (kulingisa imidlalo yekhasino, njll.).
  • Umtselela: Kuphawula sinyatselo lesibalulekile sekuvutfwa, kufaka ekhatsi budlova lobukhona mbamba kakhulu, tihloko letisikiselako, kanye netinhlamba letincane.
