• Yini: Lokucuketfwe kulungele bantfu labaneminyaka lengu 16 kuya etulu.
  • Timphawu Letimcoka:
  • Budlova: Kuveta budlova (noma umsebenti wekuya ecasini) lofika esigabeni lesibukeka sifana naleso lebesingalindzeleka empilweni yangempela. Loku kungafaka ekhatsi budlova lobungibo lobubhekiswe kubalingiswa bebantfu, imvamisa ngekuveta kufa nekulimala. Budlova lobunegazi noma lobunengati bungakhona kuba khona uma ngabe busesitayeleni se-arcade.
  • Lulwimi: Lulwimi lolubi kakhulu, kufaka ekhatsi kuhlambalata ngekwelicasi noma kuhlambalata.
  • Tidzakamiva: Tingafaka ekhatsi kukhutsata kusetjentiswa kweligwayi nobe tjwala, nobe kuveta kusetjentiswa kwetidzakamiva letingekho emtsetfweni.
  • Kuya ecasini: Kungcunu lokuvusa inkhanuko noma kuya ecasini ngaphandle kwetitfo tangasense letibonakalako.
  • Kugembula: Kungafaka ekhatsi tintfo letikhutsata nobe letifundzisa kugembula.
  • Umtselela: Lokucuketfwe kulelizinga kuvutsiwe kakhulu futsi kuvame kufaka ekhatsi tingcikitsi letikhona mbamba naletingaphazamisa.
