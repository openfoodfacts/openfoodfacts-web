• Yini: Lenchazelo ikhombisa kutsi lomdlalo uniketa kutsenga kwedijithali lokukhetsako ngemali yangempela.
  • Ububanti: Kufaka ekhatsi tinhlobo letehlukene tekutsenga emdlalweni:
  • Tintfo tekutilungisa: Tikhumba, ema-emotes, kutfutfukisa kubonakala.
  • Tinzuzo temdlalo: Emandla-ups, imali, boosters.
  • Kwandziswa/DLC: Lokucuketfwe lokwengetiwe noma indzaba.
  • Tintfo Letikhokhelwako Letingahleleki (Emabhokisi Emphango): Lapho khona badlali bangati kahle kutsi batfolani ngaphambi kwekutsenga (sib., emaphakethe emakhadi, emakhredithi etintfo).
  • Caphela: Lenchazelo akusiyo silinganiso seminyaka kepha sithonjana selwatiso. Ingavela emidlalweni lenesilinganiso seminyaka ye-PEGI, kufaka ekhatsi i-PEGI 3.
  • Kukhatsateka (ikakhulukati kubantfwana): Kungenteka kutsi kusetjentiswe imali lenyenti kakhulu, kukhutsatwa kwekutiphatsa lokufana nekugembula ngetintfo letingahleliwe, kanye nekucindzetelwa kutsi uthenge kute uchubeke.
  • Imithombo: Iwebhusayithi Lesemtsetfweni ye-PEGI, Indzawo Yemtali - Kuvisisa tilinganiso te-PEGI
