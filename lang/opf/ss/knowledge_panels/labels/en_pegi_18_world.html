• Yini: Lokucuketfwe kulungele bantfu labadzala kuphela.
  • Timphawu Letimcoka:
  • Budlova: Busetjentiswa uma lizinga lebudlova liba "budlova lobukhulu." Loku kufaka ekhatsi tindlela letetfusako tekuletsa kufa noma kulimala lokukhulu, kuhlukunyetwa, kuncunywa inhloko, kuncunywa ticu, kubulala ngaphandle kwesizatfu, noma ludlame kubantfu labangativikeli. Budlova bekuya ecasini kanye nekusongelwa nako kuhlukaniswe lapha.
  • Tidzakamiva: Kuvetwa lokukhanyako kwetidzakamiva letingekho emtsetfweni noma kukhutsatwa lokuvelele kwetintfo letilawulwako.
  • Kuya ecasini: Umsebenti wekuya ecasini lonemininingwane noma locacile, kufaka ekhatsi titfo tangasense letibonakalako.
  • Kugembula: Kulingisa kugembula lokuliciniso.
  • Kubandlulula: Kungaba netintfo letikhutsata noma letifundzisa kubandlulula.
  • Implication: Lesi silinganiso seminyaka lesiphakeme kakhulu futsi sikhombisa lokucuketfwe lokucinile kwebalaleli labavutsiwe ngenca yesimo sayo lesinemifanekiso futsi lesingaphazamisa.
