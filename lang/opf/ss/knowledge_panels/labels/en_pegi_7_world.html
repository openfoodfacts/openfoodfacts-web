• Yini: Lokucuketfwe kulungele bantfwana labaneminyaka lengu 7 kuya etulu.
  • Timphawu Letimcoka:
  • Budlova: Tinhlobo tebudlova letincane kakhulu temukelekile. Loku kungaba ludlame lolushiwo, lolungakachazwa, noma lolungasilo liciniso, loluvame kucondziswa kubalingisi labacabangako. Budlova lobuncane kakhulu lobungasibo mbamba kubalingiswa labafana nebantfu bungaphindze buvele.
  • Kwesaba: Imidlalo lenetindzawo nobe imisindvo lengahle yetfuse bantfwana labancane (sib., umoya longahlaliseki, leminye imisindvo nobe titfombe letetfusako) iwela kulesigaba.
  • Lulwimi: Kute lulwimi lolubi, noma kuthuka lokumnene kakhulu kuphela, lokungesiko kwekuya ecasini.
  • Lokunye: Kusete lokucuketfwe lokucacile kwekuya ecasini, tidzakamiva, nobe kugembula.
  • Implication: Kwetfula tintfo letingaba nemandla kakhulu kubantfwana labancane kakhulu, ikakhulukati ngekwesaba kanye nebudlova bekucabanga lobubonakala kancane.
