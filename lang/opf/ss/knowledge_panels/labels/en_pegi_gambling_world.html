• Yini: Lenchazelo ikhombisa kutsi lomdlalo unetintfo letikhutsata noma letifundzisa kugembula.
  • Ububanti: Kubhekiswe ekulingisa imidlalo yesintfu yematfuba levame kutfolakala emakhasino noma emahholo ekugembula (sib., imidlalo yemakhadi ledlalwa imali, kubheja emijahweni).
  • Silinganiso Lesincane: Lenchazelo ivela kuphela emidlalweni lelinganiswe PEGI 12, 16, noma 18. Kusukela nga-2020, imidlalo lesanda kulinganiswa ngekulingisa kwekugembula lokukhona mbamba kuhlala ku-PEGI 18.
  • Caphela Emabhokisi Laphangwako: Kuba khona kwe "Tintfo Letikhokhelwako Letingakahleleki" (letivame kubitwa ngekutsi ngemabhokisi ekuphangwa) kuyinchazelo leyehlukile yekucuketfwe ("In-Game Purses be kutfolakala ku" (sib., i-PEGI 7).
  • Imithombo: Iwebhusayithi Lesemtsetfweni Ye-PEGI, Ligunya Lekukala Imidlalo
