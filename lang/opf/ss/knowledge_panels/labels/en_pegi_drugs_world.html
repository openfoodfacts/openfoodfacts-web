• Kuyini: Lenchazelo ikhombisa kutsi lomdlalo ubhekisela noma ukhombisa kusetjentiswa kwetidzakamiva letingekho emtsetfweni, tjwala, noma ligwayi.
  • Emazinga eBumatima:
  • Kukhutsata/Kuveta Kusebentisa (PEGI 16): Kukhutsata kusebentisa ligwayi noma tjwala (sib., umlingisi utfola inzuzo) noma kuveta kusetjentiswa kwetidzakamiva letingekho emtsetfweni.
  • Kugcobisa/Kukhutsata (PEGI 18): Kuveta lokugcobisako kwetidzakamiva letingekho emtsetfweni noma kukhutsata lokuvelele kwetintfo letilawulwako.
  • Silinganiso Lesincane: Lenchazelo itfolakala kuphela emidlalweni lelinganiswe PEGI 16 noma PEGI 18.
  • Imithombo: Iwebhusayithi Lesemtsetfweni Ye-PEGI, Ligunya Lekukala Imidlalo
