Everyone can contribute

Yiba yincenye ye-database yetfu yekubambisana, yamahhala nalevulekile yemikhicito levela emhlabeni wonkhe!

Emaciniso Emikhicito Levulekile yiphrojekthi lengasebenti inzuzo leyentiwe ngalokuphelele ngemavolontiya, siyakudzinga.

Add products

Sebentisa luhlelo lwe-Open Food Facts lwe- Android nobe iPhone kute uskene kalula ibhakhodi yemikhicito levela ekhaya lakho nobe etitolo tendzawo bese ulayisha titfombe temalebuli ato.

Ema-app eselula ahlanganisiwe, ngako-ke nyalo ungaskena imikhicito, kudla, timonyo, kanye nekudla kwetilwane letifuywako kusuka ku-app lefanako ye-Open Food Facts!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Tell the world

Uyatsandza yini Emaciniso Emikhicito Levulekile? Tell others about it!

Ungetfula lephrojekthi emndenini wakho nebangani bakho, ubakhombise kutsi ungayifaka njani le-app bese unikela, bhala i-blog post, wabelane ngeMaciniso Emikhicito Levulekile etindzabeni tekuchumana...

And present it live! Setfule Emaciniso Emikhicito Levulekile kulabo labahluke kakhulu etindzaweni letehluke kakhulu, futsi sihlala sitfola imphendvulo lenemdlandla kakhulu uma sikhombisa kutsi yini lengakwentiwa uma idatha yemikhicito ivulekile. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Singasebentisana kusekela kanye nekwengeta emalebuli emvelo endzaweni, emaphuzu ekulungisa, kanye nemazinga emkhicito.

Start or join a local contributors community: add local products, recruit friends, present the project in local meetups and conferences etc.

Expert help needed

Kutfutfukisa Emaciniso Emikhicito Levulekile kuphindze kudzinge lwati lolukhetsekile kanye nebungcweti etindzaweni letinyenti letehlukene:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    Sidzinga lusito lwekwakha lwati loluncono lwemsebentisi kuwebhusayithi ye-Open Products Facts kanye ne-app yeselula, kwenta ncono umklamo wabo, kwenta tintfo tekwetfula letinemtselela njll.

  • Development

    Sinemsebenti lomnyenti wekutfutfukisa lokufanele siwente: ku-backend yeMaciniso Emikhicito Levulekile (Perl ne-MongoDB), i-API (JSON), iwebhusayithi (HTML5, JS, Sisekelo), kanye ne-app yeselula. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Ngalokubaluleke kakhulu, kunetintfo letinyenti letipholile letentiwa basebentisi, labafaka sandla kanye nebasebentisi labaphindze basebentise ngeMaciniso Emikhicito Levulekile, kungaba kuhle kutsi bantfu labanyenti bati ngako. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    Kukhona ticelo letinyenti letijabulisako naletisunguliwe tedatha levulekile yemkhicito lebesingasebenta kuto ne ema-ejensi emvelo, bakhiciti, bacwaningi, emanyuvesi, tikolo, ema-NGO njll. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    Ungacoca nemmango enkundleni yetfu . Yindzawo lenhle yekubuta imibuto, kwabelana ngemibono, kanye nekubambisana kumaphrojekthi.

  • Wiki

    Siphindze futsi sibe ne- wiki lesiyisebentisako kute sibhale ngekubambisana Emaciniso Emikhicito Levulekile kanye nemaphrojekthi ayo lamancane. Siphindze futsi sisebentise i-wiki kulawula emahumusho kanye nekusebenta ku ema-taxonomies / ema-hierarchy etigaba, emalebuli njll.

Kukhona letinye tindlela letinyenti tekunikela ku-Open Products Facts, sicela usijoyine ku-Slack bese sicala ingcoco!
