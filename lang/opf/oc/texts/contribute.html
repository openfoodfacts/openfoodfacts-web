Contribuir a Open Products Facts

All you need is a scan-do attitude! :-)

Podèm rendre l'informacion sul produch mai transparenta e sostenibla? Yes we scan!

Everyone can contribute

Fasètz partida de nòstra basa de donadas collaborativa, gratuita e dobèrta de produchs del mond entièr!

Open Products Facts es un projècte sens fins lucratius fach entièrament per de volontaris, avèm besonh de vos.

Add products

Utilizatz l'aplicacion Open Food Facts per Android o iPhone per escanejar aisidament lo còde de barras dels produches de vòstre ostal o de magasins locals e mandar d'imatges de lors etiquetas.

Las aplicacions mobilas son estadas fusionadas, aital podètz ara escanejar de produches, de noiridura, de cosmetics e de noiridura per animals de companhiá dempuèi la meteissa aplicacion Open Food Facts!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Installatz l'aplicacion per contribuir a la transparéncia del produch

Tell the world

T'agradan los faches dels produches dobèrts? Tell others about it!

Podètz presentar lo projècte a vòstra familha e amics, lor mostrar cossí installar l'aplicacion e contribuir, escriure un article de blòg, partejar los faches dels produches dobèrts suls mèdias socials...

And present it live! Avèm presentat de faches dels produches dobèrts a de publics fòrça diferents dins d'encastres fòrça diferents, e obtenèm totjorn una responsa fòrça entusiasta quand mostram çò que se pòt far quand las donadas pels produches son dobèrtas. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Podèm trabalhar amassa per sosténer e apondre d'etiquetas environamentalas localas, de puntuacions de reparabilitat e de nòrmas de produch.

Start or join a local contributors community: add local products, recruit friends, present the project in local meetups and conferences etc.

Expert help needed

Desvolopar de faches de produchs dobèrts demanda tanben de coneissenças e d'expertisa especializadas dins fòrça domenis diferents:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    Avèm besonh d'ajuda per bastir una melhora experiéncia d'utilizaire sul sit web e l'aplicacion mobila Open Products Facts, per melhorar lor dessenh, per crear de materials de presentacion impactants etc.

  • Development

    Avèm fòrça trabalh de desvolopament a far: sul backend Open Products Facts (Perl e MongoDB), l'API (JSON), lo sit web (HTML5, JS, Foundation), e l'aplicacion mobila. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Mai seriosament, i a fòrça causas cool que los utilizaires, contributors e reutilizaires fan amb Open Products Facts, seriá genial de far saber mai de monde. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    I a fòrça aplicacions interessantas e originalas de donadas dobèrtas de produch que podriam trabalhar amb agéncias environamentalas, fabricants, cercaires, universitats, escòlas, ONG etc. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    Podètz discutir amb la comunautat sus nòstre forum . Es un luòc ideal per pausar de questions, partejar d'idèas, e collaborar sus de projèctes.

  • Wiki

    Avèm tanben un wiki qu'utilizam per documentar collaborativament los faches dels produches dobèrts e sos sosprojèctes. Utilizam tanben lo wiki per gerir las traduccions e per trabalhar sus las taxonomias / ierarquias de categorias, d'etiquetas etc.

I a fòrça mai de biaisses de contribuir a Open Products Facts, mercés nos rejónher sus Slack e començam la discussion !
