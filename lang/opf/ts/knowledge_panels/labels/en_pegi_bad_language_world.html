  • Ku olova (PEGI 12): Ku rhukana ko olova na/kumbe ririmi ro khunguvanyisa, ro koma na swivulavulelo swa masangu (xikombiso, "damn," "hell," "crap").
  • Strong (PEGI 16/18): Swivulavulelo swa masangu, ku sandza, kumbe ririmi ro khunguvanyisa swinene.
  • Nhlamuselo: Vukona na matimba ya ririmi ro biha swi tlakuka hi swiyimo swa le henhla swa malembe ya PEGI.
