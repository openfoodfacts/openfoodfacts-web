Expert help needed

Ku tumbuluxa Tinhla ta Swikumiwa leswi Pfulekeke swi tlhela swi lava vutivi byo hlawuleka na vutshila eka tindhawu to tala to hambana:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    Hi lava mpfuneto ku aka ntokoto wo antswa wa mutirhisi eka webusayiti ya Open Products Facts na app ya movha, ku antswisa dizayini ya vona, ku tumbuluxa switirhisiwa swa nkandziyiso leswi khumbaka na swin’wana.

  • Development

    Hi na ntirho wo tala wa nhluvukiso lowu hi faneleke ku wu endla: eka Open Products Facts backend (Perl na MongoDB), API (JSON), web site (HTML5, JS, Foundation), na app ya movha. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Lexi nga xa nkoka swinene, ku na swilo swo tala swo kufumela leswi vatirhisi, vahoxi va xandla na vatirhisi nakambe va swi endlaka hi Open Products Facts, swi nga va swinene ku endla leswaku vanhu vo tala va tiva hi swona. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    Ku na switirhisiwa swo tala swo tsakisa na swa masungulo swa datha leyi pfulekeke ya swikumiwa leswi hi nga tirhaka eka swona na tiejensi ta mbango, vaendli, valavisisi, tiyunivhesiti, swikolo, tiNGO na swin’wana. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.
