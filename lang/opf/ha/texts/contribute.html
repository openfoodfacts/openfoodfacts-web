Everyone can contribute

Kasance cikin haɗin gwiwarmu, kyauta da buɗe bayanan samfuran samfuran daga ko'ina cikin duniya!

Facts Buɗe Kayayyakin aiki ne mara riba wanda masu sa kai suka yi gaba ɗaya, muna buƙatar ku.

Add products

Yi amfani da Buɗaɗɗen Bayanan Bayanai na Abinci don Android ko iPhone don bincika lambar sirrin samfuran cikin sauƙi daga shagunan gida ko na gida da loda hotunan tamburan su.

An haɗa ƙa'idodin wayar hannu, don haka yanzu zaku iya bincika samfuran, abinci, kayan kwalliya, da abincin dabbobi daga ƙa'idar Buɗe Bayanan Abinci!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Shigar da ƙa'idar don ba da gudummawa ga bayyanar samfur

Tell the world

Kuna son Buɗe Abubuwan Gaskiya? Tell others about it!

Kuna iya gabatar da aikin ga danginku da abokanku, ku nuna musu yadda ake shigar da app ɗin kuma ku ba da gudummawa, rubuta rubutun bulogi, raba Buɗewar Abubuwan Facts akan kafofin watsa labarun...

And present it live! Mun gabatar da Bayanan Abubuwan Buɗewa ga masu sauraro daban-daban a cikin saituna daban-daban, kuma koyaushe muna samun amsa mai daɗi sosai lokacin da muka nuna abin da za a iya yi lokacin da bayanan samfuran ke buɗe. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Za mu iya aiki tare don tallafawa da ƙara alamun muhalli na gida, ƙimar gyarawa, da ƙimar samfur.

Start or join a local contributors community: add local products, recruit friends, present the project in local meetups and conferences etc.

Expert help needed

Haɓaka Bayanan Buɗaɗɗen Kayayyaki kuma yana buƙatar ilimi na musamman da ƙwarewa a fagage daban-daban:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    Muna buƙatar taimako don gina ingantacciyar ƙwarewar mai amfani akan Buɗaɗɗen Abubuwan Facts gidan yanar gizo da aikace-aikacen hannu, don inganta ƙirar su, don ƙirƙirar kayan gabatarwa masu tasiri da sauransu.

  • Development

    Muna da ayyuka da yawa na ci gaba da za mu yi: akan Buɗaɗɗen Abubuwan Facts backend (Perl da MongoDB), API (JSON), rukunin yanar gizo (HTML5, JS, Foundation), da aikace-aikacen hannu. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Mafi mahimmanci, akwai abubuwa masu daɗi da yawa waɗanda masu amfani, masu ba da gudummawa da masu sake amfani da su ke yi tare da Buɗewar Samfuran Facts, zai yi kyau a sami ƙarin mutane su sani game da shi. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    Akwai abubuwa da yawa masu ban sha'awa da na asali na bayanan buɗaɗɗen samfur waɗanda za mu iya aiki da su tare da hukumomin muhalli, masana'anta, masu bincike, jami'o'i, makarantu, ƙungiyoyin sa-kai da sauransu. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    You can discuss with the community on our forum. Yana da kyakkyawan wuri don yin tambayoyi, raba ra'ayoyi, da haɗin kai akan ayyuka.

  • Wiki

    Hakanan muna da wiki da muke amfani da shi tare don tattara bayanan Buɗaɗɗen Abubuwan Facts da ƙananan ayyukan sa. Muna kuma amfani da wiki don sarrafa fassarorin da kuma yin aiki akan haraji / matsayi na rukunoni, lakabi da sauransu.

Akwai ƙarin hanyoyi da yawa don ba da gudummawa ga Buɗaɗɗen Abubuwan Facts, da fatan za a Kasance tare da mu akan Slack kuma bari mu fara tattaunawar!
