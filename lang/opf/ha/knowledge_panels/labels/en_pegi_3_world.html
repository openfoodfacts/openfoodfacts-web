• *** Abin da yake: *** Ana ɗaukar abun ciki ya dace da kowane rukunin shekaru.
  • Halayen Maɓalli:
  • Tashin hankali: Hannun tashe-tashen hankula masu sauƙi ne kawai ake karɓa, yawanci a cikin wasan ban dariya, zane mai ban dariya, ko yanayin yara (misali, sandar mari, cutarwa mai faɗi, halayen da ba na gaskiya ba).
  • Tsoro: Babu sauti ko hotuna da zasu firgita kananan yara.
  • ** Harshe:** Kada a ji mugun harshe.
  • Sauran: Babu abun ciki na jima'i, kwayoyi, ko caca.
  • Ma'ana: Wannan ƙimar tana mayar da hankali kan tabbatar da abun ciki yana da aminci kuma ba ya dagula wa ƙarami masu sauraro.
