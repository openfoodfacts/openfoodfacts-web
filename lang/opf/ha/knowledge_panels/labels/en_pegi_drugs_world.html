  • ** Ƙarfafawa/Bayyana Amfani (PEGI 16):** Ƙarfafa yin amfani da taba ko barasa (misali, fa'idar halayen halayen) ko nunin amfani da miyagun ƙwayoyi ba bisa ka'ida ba.
  • Glamorization/Promotion (PEGI 18): Kyawawan hotuna na haramtattun kwayoyi ko fitattun tallan abubuwan sarrafawa.
  • Mafi ƙarancin ƙima: Ana samun wannan bayanin akan wasannin da aka kimanta PEGI 16 ko PEGI 18.
