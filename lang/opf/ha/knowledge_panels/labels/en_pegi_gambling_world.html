• ***Mene ne: *** Wannan bayanin yana nuna cewa wasan ya ƙunshi abubuwa masu ƙarfafawa ko koyar da caca.
  • Mai iyaka: Yana nufin kwaikwaiyon wasannin gargajiya na damammaki da aka saba samu a gidajen caca ko dakunan caca (misali, wasannin katin da aka buga don kuɗi, yin fare akan tsere).
  • Mafi ƙarancin ƙima: Wannan mai siffantawa yana fitowa ne kawai akan wasannin da aka kima PEGI 12, 16, ko 18*. Tun daga 2020, sabbin wasannin da aka ƙima tare da simintin caca na gaske koyaushe koyaushe PEGI 18.
  • *** Lura akan Akwatunan Loot:*** Kasancewar "Abubuwan Random da Aka Biya" (wanda galibi ana kiransa akwatunan ganima) wani keɓaɓɓen bayanin abun ciki ne ("Sayen-In-Game"), kuma ana iya samun ƙima a cikin wasanni tare da ƙananan shekaru (misali 7).
