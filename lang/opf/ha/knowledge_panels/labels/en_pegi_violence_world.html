• ***Mene ne: *** Wannan mai siffantawa yana nuna cewa wasan yana dauke da hotunan tashin hankali.
  • Matsakai masu tsanani:
  • ** M / zane mai ban dariya (PEGI 3/7):** Tashin hankali maras tabbas (misali, slapstick, cutarwar zane mai ban dariya, ko tashin hankali) galibi ana yin gaba da su akan haruffa masu ban sha'awa.
  • Matsakaici (PEGI 12): Ƙarin zayyana ko tashin hankali na zahiri ga haruffan ra'ayi, ko cin zarafi marar gaskiya ga halaye irin na ɗan adam (misali, ƙananan raunuka, babu jini).
  • Mai karfi (PEGI 16): Tashin hankali na hakika ga halayen ɗan adam, gami da hotunan mutuwa da rauni. Gory ko tashin hankali na jini na iya kasancewa idan a cikin salon arcade.
  • Gross (PEGI 18): Hanyoyi masu ban tsoro na kawo mutuwa ko rauni mai tsanani, gami da azabtarwa, yanke jiki, yanke jiki, ko tashin hankali mara dalili ga masu rauni/marasa laifi. Ana kuma rarraba cin zarafin jima'i da barazana a nan.
