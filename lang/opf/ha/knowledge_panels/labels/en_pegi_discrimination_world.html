• Mene ne: Wannan wasan yana nuni da cewa wasan yana kunshe da hotunan kabilanci, addini, kishin kasa, ko wasu ra'ayoyin da zasu iya karfafa kiyayya.
  • Ma'anar: Ana amfani da wannan ƙimar lokacin da wasan ya inganta ko kuma ya ɗaukaka nuna wariya ga ƙungiyoyi dangane da asali, addini, ƙasa, jinsi, jima'i, ko wasu halaye.
  • Mafi ƙarancin ƙima: Wannan mai siffantawa yawanci yana fitowa akan wasannin da aka ƙimashi PEGI 18, saboda tsananin yanayin irin wannan abun ciki.
