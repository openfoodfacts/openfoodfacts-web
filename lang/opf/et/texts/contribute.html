Open Products Facts on mittetulunduslik projekt, mille teevad täielikult vabatahtlikud, me vajame sind.

Lisa tooteid

Use the Open Food Facts app for Android or iPhone to easily scan the barcode of products from your home or local stores and upload pictures of their labels.

Mobiilirakendused on ühendatud, nii et nüüd saate tooteid, toitu, kosmeetikat ja lemmikloomatoitu skannida samast Open Food Factsi rakendusest!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Tell the world

Kas sulle meeldivad Open Products Facts? Tell others about it!

Saate projekti oma perele ja sõpradele esitleda, näidata neile, kuidas rakendust installida ja panustada, kirjutada ajaveebipostituse, jagada avatud toodete fakte sotsiaalmeedias...

And present it live! Oleme esitlenud avatud toodete fakte väga erinevatele sihtrühmadele väga erinevates keskkondades ja saame alati väga entusiastliku vastukaja, kui näitame, mida saab teha, kui toodete andmed on avatud. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Saame koostööd teha kohalike keskkonnamärgiste, remonditavushinnangute ja tootestandardite toetamiseks ja lisamiseks.

Start or join a local contributors community: add local products, recruit friends, present the project in local meetups and conferences etc.

Expert help needed

Avatud toodete faktide väljatöötamine nõuab ka erialaseid teadmisi ja kogemusi paljudes erinevates valdkondades:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    We need help to build a better user experience on the Open Products Facts web site and mobile app, to improve their design, to create impactful presentation materials etc.

  • Development

    Meil on palju arendustööd teha: Open Products Factsi tagaserveri (Perl ja MongoDB), API (JSON), veebisaidi (HTML5, JS, Foundation) ja mobiilirakenduse kallal. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Tõsisemalt öeldes on Open Products Factsi kasutajatel, kaastöölistel ja taaskasutajatel palju lahedaid asju teha, oleks tore, kui rohkem inimesi sellest teada saaks. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    There are lots of interesting and original applications of product open data that we could work on with environmental agencies, manufacturers, researchers, universities, schools, NGOs etc. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    Saate kogukonnaga arutada meie foorumis. See on suurepärane koht küsimuste esitamiseks, ideede jagamiseks ja projektide kallal koostöö tegemiseks.

  • Wiki

    Meil on ka wiki , mida me kasutame avatud toodete faktide ja selle alamprojektide ühiseks dokumenteerimiseks. We also use the wiki to manage translations and to work on the taxonomies / hierarchies of categories, labels etc.

Open Products Factsi panustamiseks on palju rohkem võimalusi, palun liitu meiega Slackis ja alustame arutelu!
