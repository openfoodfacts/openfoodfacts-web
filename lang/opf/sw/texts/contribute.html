Je, tunaweza kufanya maelezo ya bidhaa kuwa wazi zaidi na endelevu? Yes we scan!

Everyone can contribute

Kuwa sehemu ya hifadhidata yetu shirikishi, isiyolipishwa na wazi ya bidhaa kutoka kote ulimwenguni!

Open Products Facts ni mradi usio wa faida ulioundwa na watu waliojitolea kabisa, tunakuhitaji.

Add products

Tumia programu ya Open Food Facts ya Android au iPhone ili kuchanganua kwa urahisi msimbopau wa bidhaa kutoka nyumbani kwako au maduka ya karibu na kupakia picha za lebo zao.

Programu za simu za mkononi zimeunganishwa, kwa hivyo sasa unaweza kuchanganua bidhaa, vyakula, vipodozi na vyakula vipenzi kutoka kwa programu sawa ya Open Food Facts!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Sakinisha programu ili kuchangia uwazi wa bidhaa

Tell the world

Je, unapenda Ukweli wa Bidhaa Huria? Tell others about it!

Unaweza kuwasilisha mradi kwa familia yako na marafiki, kuwaonyesha jinsi ya kusakinisha programu na kuchangia, kuandika chapisho kwenye blogu, kushiriki Ukweli wa Bidhaa Huria kwenye mitandao ya kijamii...

And present it live! Tumewasilisha Ukweli wa Bidhaa Huria kwa hadhira tofauti sana katika mipangilio tofauti kabisa, na kila mara tunapata jibu la shauku tunapoonyesha kile kinachoweza kufanywa wakati data ya bidhaa imefunguliwa. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Tunaweza kufanya kazi pamoja ili kusaidia na kuongeza lebo za mazingira za ndani, alama za urekebishaji na viwango vya bidhaa.

Start or join a local contributors community: add local products, recruit friends, present the project in local meetups and conferences etc.

Expert help needed

Kutengeneza Ukweli wa Bidhaa Huria pia kunahitaji maarifa na utaalamu maalum katika maeneo mengi tofauti:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    Tunahitaji usaidizi ili kujenga hali bora ya utumiaji kwenye tovuti ya Open Products Facts na programu ya simu, ili kuboresha muundo wao, kuunda nyenzo za uwasilishaji zenye matokeo n.k.

  • Development

    Tuna kazi nyingi za kutengeneza: kwenye mandhari ya nyuma ya Ukweli wa Bidhaa Huria (Perl na MongoDB), API (JSON), tovuti (HTML5, JS, Foundation), na programu ya simu ya mkononi. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Kwa umakini zaidi, kuna mambo mengi mazuri ambayo watumiaji, wachangiaji na watumiaji tena hufanya kwa kutumia Ukweli wa Bidhaa Huria, itakuwa vyema kupata watu zaidi kujua kuyahusu. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    Kuna matumizi mengi ya kuvutia na asilia ya data wazi ya bidhaa ambayo tunaweza kufanyia kazi mashirika ya mazingira, watengenezaji, watafiti, vyuo vikuu, shule, NGOs n.k. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    Unaweza kujadiliana na jumuiya kwenye jukwaa letu. Ni mahali pazuri pa kuuliza maswali, kushiriki mawazo, na kushirikiana katika miradi.

  • Wiki

    Pia tuna wiki tunayotumia kwa ushirikiano kutayarisha Ukweli wa Bidhaa Huria na miradi yake midogo. Pia tunatumia wiki kudhibiti tafsiri na kufanyia kazi tasnifu / safu ya kategoria, lebo n.k.

Kuna njia nyingi zaidi za kuchangia Ukweli wa Bidhaa Huria, tafadhali jiunge nasi kwenye Slack na tuanze mjadala!
