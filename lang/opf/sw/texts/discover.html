Hifadhidata ya bidhaa

Open Products Facts ni hifadhidata ya bidhaa za kila siku zilizo na data ya athari za mazingira, alama za kurekebisha, nyenzo na maelezo yote tunayoweza kupata kwenye lebo za bidhaa na vifungashio.

Made by everyone

Open Products Facts ni shirika lisilo la faida la watu wa kujitolea.

Wachangiaji kama wewe wanaweza kuongeza bidhaa kutoka duniani kote kwa kutumia programu ya Open Food Facts ya Android au iPhone au kamera yao ili kuchanganua misimbopau na kupakia picha za bidhaa na lebo zao.

For everyone

Data kuhusu bidhaa ni ya manufaa ya umma na lazima iwe wazi. The complete database is published as open data and can be reused by anyone and for any use. Check-out the cool reuses or make your own!

Bidhaa za uvumbuzi na sayansi

Our data can be reused for education, new products and services, research and more!

Hifadhidata ya Ukweli wa Bidhaa Huria ni data wazi iliyochapishwa chini ya Leseni ya Hifadhidata Huria. Anyone can use it for any purpose - commercial or non-commercial - provided the source of the data is attributed and improvements to the database are shared in a similar way.

Education

Jifunze kuhusu athari za kimazingira za bidhaa za kila siku na uelewe mzunguko wa maisha wa bidhaa za walaji.

Products and services

Data ya Ukweli wa Bidhaa Huria inaweza kutumika tena katika miradi isiyo ya kibiashara na ya kibiashara, mradi uboreshaji wa hifadhidata unashirikiwa pia.

Science

Data ya Ukweli wa Bidhaa Huria inaweza kutumiwa na wanasayansi wanaosoma uendelevu, uchumi duara, athari za mazingira na tabia ya watumiaji. Saidia kuunda data kwa mustakabali endelevu zaidi!
