Everyone can contribute

Ka mid noqo xogtayada wada shaqayneed, bilaash ah oo furan ee alaabta aduunka oo dhan!

Xaqiiqooyinka Alaabooyinka Furan waa mashruuc aan faa'iido doon ahayn oo ay sameeyeen tabaruceyaal, waanu kuu baahanahay.

Add products

U isticmaal abka Xaqiiqada Cunnada Furan ee Android ama iPhone si aad si fudud ugu sawirto barcode alaabta gurigaaga ama dukaamada xaafadda una soo rartid sawirada sumadaha.

Apps-ka moobilka waa la isku daray, si aad hadda u sawirto alaabada, cuntada, waxyaalaha la isku qurxiyo, iyo cuntada xayawaanka ee isla abka Xaqiiqada Cunnada ee Furan!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Ku rakib abka si aad gacan uga geysato daahfurnaanta alaabta

Tell the world

Ma jeceshahay Xaqiiqooyinka Alaabta Furan? Tell others about it!

Waxaad u soo bandhigi kartaa mashruuca qoyskaaga iyo asxaabtaada, tus sida loo rakibo appka oo wax ku biiriyaan, ku qor boostada blog, wadaag Xaqiiqooyinka Alaabada Furan baraha bulshada...

And present it live! Waxaan u soo bandhignay Xaqiiqooyinka Alaabada Furan ee daawadayaasha aadka u kala duwan ee goobaha aad u kala duwan, iyo waxaan had iyo jeer helnaa jawaab celin aad u xamaasad leh marka aan muujino waxa la samayn karo marka xogta alaabadu furan tahay. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Waxaan ka wada shaqeyn karnaa inaan taageerno oo aan ku darno sumadaha deegaanka, buundooyinka dayactirka, iyo heerarka alaabta.

Start or join a local contributors community: add local products, recruit friends, present the project in local meetups and conferences etc.

Expert help needed

Horumarinta Xaqiiqooyinka Alaabada Furan waxay sidoo kale u baahan tahay aqoon gaar ah iyo khibrad dhinacyo badan oo kala duwan:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    Waxaan u baahanahay caawimo si aan u dhisno khibrad isticmaale oo wanaagsan oo ku saabsan shabakada Xaqiiqooyinka Alaabada Furan iyo abka moobilka, si aan u wanaajino naqshadooda, si aan u abuurno agab bandhigeed oo saamayn leh iwm.

  • Development

    Waxaan haynaa shaqooyin badan oo horumarineed oo aanu ku qabano: dhabarka Xaqiiqooyinka Alaabada Furan (Perl iyo MongoDB), API (JSON), web site (HTML5, JS, Foundation), iyo abka mobaylka. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Si ka sii daran, waxaa jira waxyaabo badan oo fiican oo isticmaalayaasha, wax ku biiriyaasha iyo dib u isticmaala ay ku sameeyaan Xaqiiqooyinka Alaabada Furan, way fiicnaan lahayd in dad badan ay ogaadaan. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    Waxa jira codsiyo badan oo xiiso leh oo asal ah oo ah xogta furan ee alaabta oo aanu kala shaqayn karno wakaaladaha deegaanka, soosaarayaasha, cilmi-baarayaasha, jaamacadaha, dugsiyada, NGO-yada iwm. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    Waxaad bulshada kala hadli kartaa golahayaga . Waa meel aad u wanaagsan in su'aalo lagu weydiiyo, fikrado lagu wadaago, lagana wada shaqeeyo mashaariicda.

  • Wiki

    Waxaan sidoo kale leennahay wiki oo aan u isticmaalno si wadajir ah inaan u diiwaangelinno Xaqiiqooyinka Alaabta Furan iyo mashruuc-hoosaadyadeeda. Waxaan sidoo kale u isticmaalnaa wiki si aan u maamulno tarjumaadaha iyo inaan ka shaqayno canshuuraha / kala sareynta qaybaha, sumadaha iwm.

Waxaa jira siyaabo badan oo badan oo wax loogu biirin karo Xaqiiqooyinka Alaabada Furan, fadlan nagu soo biir Slack oo aan bilowno dooda!
