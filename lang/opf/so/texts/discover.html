Xogta alaabta

Made by everyone

Xaqiiqooyinka Alaabooyinka Furan waa urur aan faa'iido doon ahayn oo iskaa wax u qabso ah.

Contributors like you can add products from around the world using the Open Food Facts app for Android or iPhone or their camera to scan barcodes and upload pictures of products and their labels.

For everyone

Xogta alaabta ku saabsan waa danta guud waana inay furnaataa. The complete database is published as open data and can be reused by anyone and for any use. Check-out the cool reuses or make your own!

Samee xulashooyin badan oo waara

Knowledge is power. Aqoonta badeecadu waa awood lagu isticmaalo si ka wanaagsan!

  • Saamaynta Deegaanka

    Discover the CO2 impact of products and make informed decisions about your environmental footprint. Open Products Facts helps you understand the environmental cost of the things you buy.

  • Repairability Score

    Check the repairability score of electronics and appliances. Taageer dhaqaale wareeg ah adiga oo dooranaya alaabta la hagaajin karo halkii la bedeli lahaa.

  • Find products

    Our search form includes dozens of criteria so that you can find products that match your environmental and sustainability criteria.

  • Decode labels

    Halabuurka walxaha, codes dib u warshadaynta, sumadaha tamarta... Xaqiiqooyinka Alaabta Furan waxay kaa caawinayaan inaad macno u yeelatid macluumaadka alaabta iyo sumadaha.

Alaabooyinka hal-abuurka iyo sayniska

Our data can be reused for education, new products and services, research and more!

Xogta Xaqiiqooyinka Alaabada Furan waa xog furan oo lagu daabacay shatiga Xogta Furan. Anyone can use it for any purpose - commercial or non-commercial - provided the source of the data is attributed and improvements to the database are shared in a similar way.
