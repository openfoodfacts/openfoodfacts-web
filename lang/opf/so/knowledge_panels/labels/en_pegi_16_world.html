  • Rabshadaha: Sawirada rabshadaha (ama fal galmo) oo gaara heer la mid ah sidii la filayo nolosha dhabta ah. Gacan ka hadal ama rabshad dhiig leh ayaa laga yaabaa inay joogaan haddii ay tahay qaab arcade ah.

  • ** Khamaarka:** Waxaa ku jiri kara waxyaabo dhiirigeliya ama bara khamaarka.
