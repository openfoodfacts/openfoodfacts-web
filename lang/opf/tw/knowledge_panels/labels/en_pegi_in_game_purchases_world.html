• Nea ɛyɛ: Saa nkyerɛkyerɛmu yi kyerɛ sɛ agodie no de dijitaal adetɔ a wobetumi apaw a sika ankasa wom ma.
  • Scope: Nea ɛka ho ne akwan ahodoɔ a wɔfa so tɔ wɔ agodie mu:
  • Nneɛma a wɔde yɛ ahosiesie: Skins, emotes, visual enhancements.
  • Gameplay mfaso: Power-ups, sika, boosters.
  • Ntrɛwmu/DLC: Nsɛm foforo anaa asɛm.
  • Paid Random Items (Loot Boxes): Baabi a agodifo nnim nea wɔrenya ankasa ansa na wɔatɔ (sɛ nhwɛso no, card packs, item crates).
  • Hyɛ no nsow: Saa nkyerɛkyerɛmu yi ɛnyɛ mfeɛ a wɔadi na mmom ɛyɛ nsɛm ho ahyɛnsodeɛ. Ebetumi apue wɔ agodie ahorow a PEGI mfe dodow biara ka ho, a PEGI 3 ka ho
  • Adwennwene (titiriw ma mmofra): Sɛnea ebetumi aba sɛ wɔsɛe sika dodo, nneyɛe a ɛte sɛ kyakyatow ho nkuranhyɛ a wɔde nneɛma a wɔanhyɛ da ayɛ, ne nhyɛso a wɔde bɛtɔ ade na ama wɔanya nkɔso.
  • Fibea: PEGI Ɔmanfoɔ Wɛbsaet, Awofoɔ Beaeɛ - PEGI ratings nteaseɛ
