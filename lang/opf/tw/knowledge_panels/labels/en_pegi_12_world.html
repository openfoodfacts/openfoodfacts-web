  • Su Titiriw:
  • Basabasayɛ: Basabasayɛ a ɛyɛ mfonini anaa ɛyɛ nokware kɛse wɔ nsusuwii hunu mu nnipa ho, anaa basabasayɛ a ɛnyɛ nokware a wɔyɛ wɔ nnipa a wɔte sɛ nnipa ho. Eyi betumi ayɛ nkɛntɛnso a wotumi hu kɛse, nanso mpɛn pii no ɛnyɛ gore. Wɔaka basabasayɛ a wɔkyerɛ sɛ wɔyɛ wɔ nnipa so wɔ baabi a wɔankyerɛ basabasayɛ ankasa no nso ho asɛm.
  • Ehu/Ehu: Ahunahuna a emu yɛ den, ehu, anaa mfonini a ɛfa opira ho, ne ahude a ɛkɔ so nnidiso nnidiso anaa mfonini ahorow a ɛhaw adwene a ɛkɔ so.
  • Kasa: Ntam a ɛyɛ brɛoo ne/anaasɛ kasa a ɛyɛ abufuw (nna ho nsɛm tiawa).
  • Ɔbarima ne ɔbea nna: Ɔbarima ne ɔbea nna ho nsɛm anaa nna ho nsɛm a wɔde gyina hɔ betumi aba. Adagyaw a ɛkanyan akɔnnɔ anaa nna a ɛnyɛ nea ɛda adi pefee.
  • Kyakyatow: Ebia nneɛma a ɛhyɛ kyakyatow ho nkuran anaa ɛkyerɛkyerɛ (casino agodie ho mfonini, ne nea ɛkeka ho) ka ho.
  • Nkyerɛkyerɛmu: Ɛhyɛ anammɔn titiriw a ɛkɔ soro wɔ onyin mu agyirae, a basabasayɛ a ɛyɛ nokware kɛse, nsɛmti a ɛkyerɛ biribi, ne kasafĩ a ɛnyɛ den ka ho.
