• Nea ɛyɛ: Saa nkyerɛkyerɛmu yi kyerɛ sɛ agodie no kyerɛ anaa ɛkyerɛ nnubɔne a mmara mma ho kwan, nsa, anaa tawa a wɔde di dwuma.
  • Severity Levels:
  • Nkuranhyɛ/Nneɛma a wɔde di dwuma ho mfonini (PEGI 16): Tawa anaa nsa a wɔde di dwuma ho nkuranhyɛ (sɛ nhwɛso no, suban nya mfaso) anaa nnubɔne a mmara mma ho kwan a wɔde di dwuma ho mfonini.
  • Glamourization/Promotion (PEGI 18): Nnubɔne a mmara mma ho kwan ho mfonini a ɛyɛ fɛ anaasɛ nneɛma a wɔahyɛ da ahyɛ ho nkuran a ɛda nsow.
  • Minimum Rating: Saa nkyerɛkyerɛmu yi wɔ agodie a wɔakyerɛ sɛ PEGI 16 anaa PEGI 18 nkutoo so.
  • Fibea: PEGI Ɔman Wɛbsaet, Agodie Nhwehwɛmu Tumi
