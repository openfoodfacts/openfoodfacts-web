• Nea ɛyɛ: Saa nkyerɛkyerɛmu yi kyerɛ sɛ agodie no kura mmusuakuo, nyamesom, ɔmampɛ, anaa nsɛm foforɔ a ɛyɛ katee a ɛda adi sɛ ɛbɛhyɛ nitan ho mfonini.
  • Nsɛm a ɛfa ho: Wɔde saa dodoɔ yi di dwuma berɛ a agodie no hyɛ nyiyim a wɔyɛ tia akuo a egyina mfitiaseɛ, nyamesom, ɔman, ɔbarima ne ɔbaa, nna, anaa su foforɔ so ho nkuran anaa ɛhyɛ no anuonyam.
  • Minimum Rating: Saa nkyerɛkyerɛmu yi taa pue wɔ agodie a wɔde PEGI 18 ahyɛ mu, esiane sɛnea nsɛm a ɛtete saa no mu yɛ den nti.
