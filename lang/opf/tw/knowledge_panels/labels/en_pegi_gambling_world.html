• Nea ɛyɛ: Saa nkyerɛkyerɛmu yi kyerɛ sɛ agodie no kura nneɛma a ɛhyɛ kyakyatow ho nkuran anaa ɛkyerɛkyerɛ.
  • Scope: Ɛkyerɛ atetesɛm agodie a ɛfa akwanhyia ho a wɔtaa hu wɔ kyakyatow adan anaa kyakyatow adan mu (sɛ nhwɛso no, kaad agodie a wɔbɔ de gye sika, kyakyatow wɔ mmirikatu so).
  • Minimum Rating: Saa nkyerɛkyerɛmu yi pue wɔ agodie a wɔakyerɛ sɛ PEGI 12, 16, anaa 18 nkutoo so. Ɛde besi afe 2020 no, agodie foforo a wɔahyɛ no agyirae a ɛwɔ kyakyatow ho mfonini a ɛyɛ nokware no yɛ PEGI 18 bere nyinaa.
  • Hyɛ no nsow wɔ Loot Boxes ho: "Paid Random Items" (a wɔtaa frɛ no loot boxes) a ɛwɔ hɔ no yɛ content descriptor a ɛyɛ soronko ("In-Game Purchases") na wobetumi ahu wɔ agodie a ne mfe dodow sua (sɛ nhwɛso no, PEGI 7).
  • Fibea: PEGI Ɔman Wɛbsaet, Agodie Nhwehwɛmu Tumi
