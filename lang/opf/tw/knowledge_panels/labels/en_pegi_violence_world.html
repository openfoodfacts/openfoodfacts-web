• Nea ɛyɛ: Saa nkyerɛkyerɛmu yi kyerɛ sɛ basabasayɛ ho mfonini wɔ agodie no mu.
  • Severity Levels:
  • Mild/Cartoon (PEGI 3/7): Basabasayɛ a ɛyɛ mmerɛw yiye, a ɛnyɛ nokware (sɛ nhwɛso no, akuturuku, cartoonish opira, anaa basabasayɛ a wɔkyerɛ) a wɔtaa de kyerɛ fantasy characters.
  • Moderate (PEGI 12): Basabasayɛ a ɛyɛ mfonini anaa ɛyɛ nokware kɛse a wɔyɛ wɔ nsusuwii hunu mu nnipa ho, anaa basabasayɛ a ɛnyɛ nokware a wɔyɛ wɔ nnipa a wɔte sɛ nnipa ho (sɛ nhwɛso no, opira nketenkete, mogya biara nni mu).
  • Ahoɔden (PEGI 16): Basabasayɛ ankasa a wɔyɛ wɔ nnipa suban ho, a owu ne opira ho mfonini ka ho. Basabasayɛ a mogya wom anaa mogyahwiegu betumi aba sɛ ɛyɛ agorudi kwan so a.
  • Gross (PEGI 18): Akwan a ɛyɛ hu a wɔfa so de owu anaa opira a emu yɛ den ba, a ayayade, twitwa ti, twitwa, anaa basabasayɛ a enni atirimpɔw a wɔyɛ tia nnipa a wɔyɛ mmerɛw/wɔn ho nni asɛm ka ho. Wɔakyekyɛ nna mu basabasayɛ ne ahunahuna nso mu wɔ ha.
  • Nkyerɛkyerɛmu: Dodow a PEGI mfeɛ dodoɔ a ɛbata ho no kɔ soro no, dodoɔ no ara na basabasayɛ a wɔayɛ ho mfonini no mu yɛ den na ɛyɛ nokware.
  • Fibea: PEGI Ɔman Wɛbsaet, Agodie Nhwehwɛmu Tumi
