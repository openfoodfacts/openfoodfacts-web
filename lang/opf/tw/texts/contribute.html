Boa ma Open Products Nokwasɛm ahorow

All you need is a scan-do attitude! :-)

So yebetumi ama nneɛma a wɔyɛ ho nsɛm ada adi kɛse na atra hɔ daa? Yes we scan!

Everyone can contribute

Yɛ yɛn adwumayɛkuw, a wontua hwee na ɛbue database a ɛfa nneɛma a efi wiase nyinaa ho no fã!

Open Products Facts yɛ adwuma a ɛnyɛ mfasoɔ a atuhoamafoɔ na wɔyɛeɛ nyinaa, yɛhia wo.

Add products

Fa Open Food Facts app no di dwuma ma Android anaa iPhone na ɛnyɛ den sɛ wobɛhwehwɛ nneɛma a ɛwɔ wo fie anaa wo mpɔtam hɔ sotɔɔ ahorow mu no barcode na wode wɔn nkyerɛwde ho mfonini akɔ so.

Wɔaka mobile app ahorow no abom, enti afei wubetumi ahwehwɛ nneɛma, nnuan, nnuru a wɔde yɛ ahosiesie, ne afieboa nnuan afi Open Food Facts app koro no ara so!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Fa app no hyɛ mu na aboa ma nneɛma no ada adi pefee

Tell the world

So w’ani gye Open Products Facts ho? Tell others about it!

Wubetumi de adwuma no akyerɛ w’abusua ne wo nnamfo, akyerɛ wɔn sɛnea wɔde app no bɛhyɛ mu na woaboa, akyerɛw blog post, akyɛ Open Products Facts wɔ social media so...

And present it live! Yɛde Open Products Facts akyerɛ atiefoɔ soronko koraa wɔ tebea ahodoɔ mu, na yɛnya mmuaeɛ a ɛyɛ anigyeɛ paa berɛ biara berɛ a yɛkyerɛ deɛ wɔbɛtumi ayɛ berɛ a data a ɛfa nneɛma ho no abue. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Yebetumi abom ayɛ adwuma de aboa na yɛde mpɔtam hɔ nneɛma a atwa yɛn ho ahyia ho nkyerɛwde, nkontabuo a ɛkyerɛ sɛ wɔsiesie, ne nneɛma a wɔyɛ no gyinapɛn bɛka ho.

Start or join a local contributors community: add local products, recruit friends, present the project in local meetups and conferences etc.

Expert help needed

Developing Open Products Facts nso hwehwɛ nimdeɛ ne nimdeɛ soronko wɔ nneɛma ahorow pii mu:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    Yɛhia mmoa na yɛakyekye osuahu a ɛyɛ papa wɔ Open Products Facts wɛbsaet ne mobile app no so, ama wɔn nhyehyɛɛ atu mpɔn, de ayɛ nkyerɛkyerɛmu nneɛma a ɛwɔ nkɛntɛnsoɔ ne nea ɛkeka ho.

  • Development

    Yɛwɔ nkɔsoɔ adwuma pii a ɛsɛ sɛ yɛyɛ: wɔ Open Products Facts akyi (Perl ne MongoDB), API (JSON), wɛbsaet (HTML5, JS, Foundation), ne mobile app no so. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Nea ɛyɛ aniberesɛm kɛse no, nneɛma a ɛyɛ nwini pii wɔ hɔ a wɔn a wɔde di dwuma, wɔn a wɔde mmoa ma ne wɔn a wɔsan de di dwuma de Open Products Facts yɛ, ɛbɛyɛ papa sɛ wobɛma nnipa pii ahu ho asɛm. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    Nneɛma pii wɔ hɔ a ɛyɛ anigye na ɛyɛ mfitiaseɛ dwumadie a ɛfa product open data ho a yɛbɛtumi ne nneɛma a atwa yɛn ho ahyia ho adwumayɛkuo, wɔn a wɔyɛ, nhwehwɛmufoɔ, sukuupɔn, sukuu, NGOs ne nea ɛkeka ho ayɛ adwuma wɔ so. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    Wubetumi ne mpɔtam hɔfo asusuw ho wɔ yɛn forum. Ɛyɛ beae pa a wobɛbisa nsɛm, akyɛ adwene, na woayɛ adwuma abom wɔ nnwuma mu.

  • Wiki

    Yɛwɔ wiki nso a yɛde bom kyerɛw Open Products Facts ne ne nnwuma nketewa. Yɛsan nso de wiki di dwuma de hwɛ nkyerɛaseɛ na yɛyɛ adwuma wɔ taxonomies / hierarchies a ɛwɔ categories, labels ne nea ɛkeka ho.

Akwan pii wɔ hɔ a wobɛfa so aboa Open Products Facts, yɛsrɛ wo kɔka yɛn ho wɔ Slack na momma yɛnhyɛ nkɔmmɔbɔ no ase!
