Cuir ri Fiosrachadh mu Thoraidhean Fosgailte

All you need is a scan-do attitude! :-)

An urrainn dhuinn fiosrachadh mu thoraidhean a dhèanamh nas soilleire agus nas seasmhaiche? Yes we scan!

Everyone can contribute

Bi nad phàirt den stòr-dàta co-obrachail, an-asgaidh agus fosgailte againn de thoraidhean bho air feadh an t-saoghail!

’S e pròiseact neo-phrothaideach a th’ ann an Open Products Facts a chaidh a dhèanamh gu tur le saor-thoilich, agus tha feum againn ort.

Add products

Cleachd an aplacaid Open Food Facts airson Android no iPhone gus còd-barra thoraidhean bhon dachaigh agad no bho stòran ionadail a sganadh gu furasta agus dealbhan de na bileagan aca a luchdachadh suas.

Chaidh na h-aplacaidean gluasadach a chur còmhla, agus mar sin faodaidh tu a-nis toraidhean, biadh, cungaidhean maise, agus biadh pheataichean a sganadh bhon aon aplacaid Open Food Facts!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Stàlaich an aplacaid gus cur ri follaiseachd toraidh

Tell the world

A bheil Fiosrachadh mu Thoraidhean Fosgailte a’ còrdadh riut? Tell others about it!

Faodaidh tu am pròiseact a thaisbeanadh do do theaghlach is do charaidean, sealltainn dhaibh mar a stàlaicheas iad an aplacaid agus cur ris, post blog a sgrìobhadh, Open Products Facts a cho-roinn air na meadhanan sòisealta...

And present it live! Tha sinn air Fiosrachadh mu Thoraidhean Fosgailte a thaisbeanadh do luchd-èisteachd gu math eadar-dhealaichte ann an suidheachaidhean gu math eadar-dhealaichte, agus bidh sinn an-còmhnaidh a’ faighinn freagairt glè dhealasach nuair a sheallas sinn dè a ghabhas dèanamh nuair a bhios an dàta airson thoraidhean fosgailte. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Is urrainn dhuinn obrachadh còmhla gus taic a thoirt do leubail àrainneachdail ionadail, sgòran càraidh, agus inbhean toraidh agus an cur ris.

Start or join a local contributors community: add local products, recruit friends, present the project in local meetups and conferences etc.

Expert help needed

Feumaidh eòlas agus eòlas sònraichte ann an iomadh raon eadar-dhealaichte a bhith ann cuideachd airson Fiosrachadh mu Thoraidhean Fosgailte a leasachadh:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    We need help to build a better user experience on the Open Products Facts web site and mobile app, to improve their design, to create impactful presentation materials etc.

  • Development

    Tha tòrr obair leasachaidh againn ri dhèanamh: air cùl-raon Open Products Facts (Perl agus MongoDB), API (JSON), làrach-lìn (HTML5, JS, Foundation), agus an aplacaid gluasadach. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Nas cudromaiche buileach, tha tòrr rudan inntinneach ann a bhios luchd-cleachdaidh, luchd-tabhartais agus ath-chleachdaichean a’ dèanamh le Open Products Facts, bhiodh e math barrachd dhaoine fhaighinn a-mach mu dheidhinn. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    There are lots of interesting and original applications of product open data that we could work on with environmental agencies, manufacturers, researchers, universities, schools, NGOs etc. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    Faodaidh tu bruidhinn ris a’ choimhearsnachd air an fhòram againn. ’S e àite math a th’ ann airson ceistean fhaighneachd, beachdan a cho-roinn, agus co-obrachadh air pròiseactan.

  • Wiki

    Tha wiki againn cuideachd a bhios sinn a’ cleachdadh gus Open Products Facts agus na fo-phròiseactan aige a chlàradh gu co-obrachail. We also use the wiki to manage translations and to work on the taxonomies / hierarchies of categories, labels etc.

There are many more ways to contribute to Open Products Facts, please join us on Slack and let's start the discussion!
