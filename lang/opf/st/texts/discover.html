Sebaka sa polokelo ea lihlahisoa

Open Products Facts ke pokello ea lintlha tsa lihlahisoa tsa letsatsi le letsatsi tse nang le lintlha tsa phello ea tikoloho, lintlha tse ka lokisoang, lisebelisoa, le lintlha tsohle tseo re ka li fumanang ho lileibole le liphutheloana tsa lihlahisoa.

Lihlahisoa tsa boqapi le mahlale

Our data can be reused for education, new products and services, research and more!

Sebaka sa polokelo ea lintlha tsa Open Products ke lintlha tse bulehileng tse hatisitsoeng tlas'a Laesense ea Open Database. Anyone can use it for any purpose - commercial or non-commercial - provided the source of the data is attributed and improvements to the database are shared in a similar way.

Education
