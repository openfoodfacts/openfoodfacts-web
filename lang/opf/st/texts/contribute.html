Everyone can contribute

Eba karolo ea tšebelisano-'moho, ea mahala le e bulehileng ea lihlahisoa tse tsoang lefats'eng ka bophara!

Open Products Facts ke projeke e sa etseng phaello e entsoeng ke baithaopi, rea u hloka.

Add products

Sebelisa sesebelisoa sa Open Food Facts bakeng sa Android kapa iPhone ho sheba habonolo barcode ea lihlahisoa tsa hae kapa mabenkeleng a lehae le ho kenya linepe tsa lileibole tsa tsona.

Lisebelisoa tsa mehala li kopantsoe, kahoo joale u ka khona ho hlahloba lihlahisoa, lijo, litlolo le lijo tsa lapeng ho tsoa ho sesebelisoa se tšoanang sa Open Food Facts!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Kenya sesebelisoa ho kenya letsoho ponaletsong ea sehlahisoa

Tell the world

A na u rata Open Products Facts? Tell others about it!

O ka hlahisa projeke ho ba lelapa le metsoalle ea hau, oa ba bontša mokhoa oa ho kenya sesebelisoa le ho kenya letsoho, ngola poso ea blog, arolelana lintlha tsa Open Products mecheng ea litaba tsa sechaba...

And present it live! Re hlahisitse lintlha tsa Open Products ho bamameli ba fapaneng haholo ka maemo a fapaneng haholo, 'me re lula re fumana karabelo e chesehang haholo ha re bonts'a se ka etsoang ha data ea lihlahisoa e bulehile. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Re ka sebetsa 'moho ho ts'ehetsa le ho kenya lileibole tsa tikoloho ea lehae, lintlha tsa ho lokisoa, le maemo a lihlahisoa.

Start or join a local contributors community: add local products, recruit friends, present the project in local meetups and conferences etc.

Expert help needed

Ho theha Lintlha tsa Open Products ho boetse ho hloka tsebo e khethehileng le boitseanape libakeng tse ngata tse fapaneng:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    Re hloka thuso ho aha boiphihlelo bo betere ba mosebelisi ho webosaete ea Open Products Facts le sesebelisoa sa mohala, ho ntlafatsa moralo oa bona, ho theha lisebelisoa tsa tlhahiso tse nang le tšusumetso jj.

  • Development

    Re na le mosebetsi o mongata oa ntlafatso oo re lokelang ho o etsa: ho Open Products Facts backend (Perl le MongoDB), API (JSON), sebaka sa marang-rang (HTML5, JS, Foundation), le app mobile. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Habohlokoa le ho feta, ho na le lintho tse ngata tse monate tseo basebelisi, batšehetsi le basebelisi ba li etsang ka Open Products Facts, ho ka ba monate ho etsa hore batho ba bangata ba tsebe ka eona. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    Ho na le lits'ebetso tse ngata tse khahlisang le tsa mantlha tsa data e bulehileng ea sehlahisoa eo re ka sebetsanang le eona mekhatlo ea tikoloho, bahlahisi, bafuputsi, liunivesithi, likolo, mekhatlo e ikemetseng jj. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    U ka buisana le sechaba ho rona forum. Ke sebaka se setle sa ho botsa lipotso, ho arolelana maikutlo, le ho sebelisana 'moho mererong.

  • Wiki

    Hape re na le wiki eo re e sebelisang ho ngola ka kopanelo lintlha tsa Open Products le merero ea eona. Hape re sebelisa wiki ho laola liphetolelo le ho sebetsa ho taxonomies / hierarchies ea lihlopha, li-label jj.

Ho na le mekhoa e meng e mengata ea ho kenya letsoho ho Open Products Facts, ka kopo ikopanye le rona ho Slack 'me re qale puisano!
