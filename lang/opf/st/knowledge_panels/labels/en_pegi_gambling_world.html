• Seo eleng sona: Tlhaloso ena e supa hore papali e na le lintlha tse khothaletsang kapa tse rutang papali ea chelete.
  • Scope: E bua ka papiso ea lipapali tsa khale tsa t≈°ohanyetso tse fumanehang lik'hasinong kapa liholong tsa ho becha (mohlala, lipapali tsa likarete tse bapaloang chelete, ho becha mabelo).
  • Bonyane Boemo: Tlhaloso ena e hlaha feela lipapaling tse fuoeng lintlha tse PEGI 12, 16, kapa 18. Ho tloha ka 2020, lipapali tse sa tsoa fuoa lintlha tse nang le papiso ea sebele ea papali ea chelete li lula li le PEGI 18.
  • Tlhokomeliso ka Mabokose a Loot: Boteng ba "Lintho tse Lefshoang tse sa Lebelloang" (hangata li bitsoa mabokose a thepa) ke tlhaloso e arohaneng ea litaba ("In-Game Purchases") le Lipapali tse tlase tsa GI (7) li ka fumanoa ho PE).
  • Mehloli: Webosaete ea Semmuso ea PEGI, Bolaoli ba Kelo ea Lipapali
