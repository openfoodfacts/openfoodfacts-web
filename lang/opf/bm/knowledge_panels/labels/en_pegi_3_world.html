• A ye min ye: Kɔnɔkow bɛ jate ko u bɛnnen don san hakɛ bɛɛ ma.
  • Jɛkulu jɔnjɔnw:
  • Fariya: Fariya suguya nɔgɔmanw dɔrɔn de bɛ sɔn, a ka c’a la, nisɔndiyako, dessin animé, walima denmisɛnninw ka yɔrɔ la (misali la, sɔgɔsɔgɔninjɛ, tiɲɛni min bɛ fɔ a ma, wale minnu tɛ tiɲɛ ye).
  • Siran: Mankan walima ja si tɛ yen min bɛ se ka denmisɛn fitininw lasiran.
  • Kan: Kan jugu si man kan ka mɛn.
  • Wɛrɛw: Cɛnimusoko, dɔrɔgu, walima wasa tɛ kɛ.
  • A kɔrɔ: Nin jatebɔ in sinsinnen bɛ a kan ko kɔnɔkow ka kɛ lakana ye ani ko u tɛ lamɛnnikɛla fitininw degun.
