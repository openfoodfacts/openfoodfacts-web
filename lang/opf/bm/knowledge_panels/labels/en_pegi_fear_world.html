• A ye min ye: Nin ɲɛfɔli in b’a jira ko fɛn dɔw bɛ tulon in na minnu bɛ se ka siran walima ka siran.
  • Gɛlɛya hakɛw:
  • Siranfɛn (PEGI 7): Ja walima mankan minnu ka teli ka siran walima ka siran denmisɛn fitininw ɲɛ (misali la, dɔnkili minnu bɛ mɔgɔ kɔnɔnafili, sigida minnu bɛ siran, kɔkan kulekan). A man kan ka kɛ ko fariyako tilennenw sen bɛ o la.
  • Siranfɛn (PEGI 12+): Bagabagali barikama, siran, walima joginni kunnafoni jalenw, ani siranfɛnw sigiyɔrɔma walima ja jɔrɔnanko minnu bɛ mɛn.
  • A kɔrɔ: A bɛ sinsin hakili ta fan fɛ nɔ bilali kan ani kɔnɔkow kan minnu bɛ mɔgɔ jɔrɔ, sanni ka kɛ fariyako jɛlen ye.
  • Sɔrɔyɔrɔw: PEGI ka siti ofisiyali, Tulonkɛlaw ka jatebɔ ɲɛmɔgɔso
