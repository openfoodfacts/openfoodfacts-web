• A ye min ye: Kɔnɔkow bɛnnen don mɔgɔ kelen-kelen bɛɛ ma minnu si bɛ san 12 la ka tɛmɛ o kan.
  • Jɛkulu jɔnjɔnw:
  • Fariya: Fariyako min bɛ kɛ ni ja ye walima min bɛ kɛ tiɲɛ na ka ɲɛsin mɔgɔ minnu bɛ miirili kɛ, walima fariyako min tɛ tiɲɛ ye ka ɲɛsin mɔgɔ minnu bɛ i n’a fɔ hadamadenw. O bɛ se ka kɛ ni nɔ ye min bɛ ye ka caya, nka a ka ca a la, gore tɛ a la. Fariyako min bɛ kɛ hadamadenw na, fariyako yɛrɛ ma jira yɔrɔ minnu na, o fana bɛ ɲɛfɔ.
  • Siran/Siran: Bagabagali barikama, siran, walima joginni kunnafoni jalenw, ani siranɲɛ sinsinni sabatilenw walima ja jɔrɔnankow.
  • Kan: Kalili nɔgɔman ani/walima kan min bɛ mɔgɔ tɔɔrɔ (cɛnimusoya kumasen surun).
  • Cɛnimusoko: Cɛnimusoko jiracogo walima cɛnimusoya jɔcogo bɛ se ka kɛ yen. Farikoloɲɛnajɛ min bɛ kɛ ni cɛnimusoya ye walima cɛnimusoya min tɛ kɛ cogo jɛlen na.
  • Wasa: A bɛ se ka kɛ fɛnw ye minnu bɛ dusu don walima ka wasako kalan (kasino tulonw simulatigɛ, a ɲɔgɔnnaw).
  • A kɔrɔ: A bɛ senna-tɛgɛrɛba jira kɔgɔli la, i n’a fɔ fariyako min bɛ kɛ tiɲɛ na kosɛbɛ, barokun minnu bɛ hakilina di, ani kumajugu nɔgɔlenw.
