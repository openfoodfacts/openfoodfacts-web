• A ye min ye: Nin ɲɛfɔli in b’a jira ko tulon in bɛ sanni kɛ nizɛrikan na ni wari lakika ye.
  • Scope: Tulon kɔnɔ sanni suguya caman bɛ a la:
  • Fɛn minnu bɛ mɔgɔ ɲɛnajɛ: Fari, dusukunnataw, ɲɛnayelifɛnw.
  • Tulonkɛ nafaw: Setigiya, wari, boosters.
  • Expansions/DLC: Kɔnɔkow walima maana wɛrɛw.
  • Paid Random Items (Loot Boxes): Yɔrɔ minnu na tulonkɛlaw t’a dɔn tigitigi u bɛ min sɔrɔ sani u ka sanni kɛ (misali la, karti pakew, fɛnw kɛsu).
  • Kɔlɔsili: Nin ɲɛfɔli in ye tɛ san hakɛ jate ye nka kunnafoni taamasiyɛn ye. A bɛ se ka bɔ tulonw kan minnu si hakɛ bɛ se ka kɛ PEGI ye, PEGI 3 fana sen bɛ o la.
  • Jɔrɔnankow (kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenya la denmisɛnw ta): Se ka musaka kɛ kojugu, ka dusu don u kɔnɔ ka kɛwalew kɛ minnu bɛ i n’a fɔ wasa ni fɛnw ye minnu kɛra k’a sɔrɔ u ma kɛ cogo si la, ani degun ka sanni kɛ walasa ka taa ɲɛ.
  • Sɔrɔyɔrɔw: PEGI ka siti ofisiyali, Bangebagaw ka yɔrɔ - PEGI ka jatebɔw faamuyali
