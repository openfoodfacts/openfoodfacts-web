• A ye min ye: Kɔnɔkow bɛnnen don denmisɛnw ma minnu si bɛ san 7 la ka tɛmɛ o kan.
  • Jɛkulu jɔnjɔnw:
  • Fariya: Fariya sugu minnu ka nɔgɔn kosɛbɛ, olu bɛ sɔn. O bɛ se ka kɛ fariyako ye min bɛ kɛ ni hakilina ye, min tɛ ɲɛfɔli kɛ, walima min tɛ tiɲɛ ye, tuma caman na, o bɛ ɲɛsin mɔgɔ minnu ma minnu ye miiriyajuguw ye. Fariyako nɔgɔmanba min tɛ tiɲɛ ye, o fana bɛ se ka jira hadamadenw bɔɲɔgɔnko mɔgɔw la.
  • Siran: Tulon minnu bɛ kɛ ni ɲɛjiraliw walima mankanw ye minnu bɛ se ka siran denmisɛn fitininw ɲɛ (misali la, fiɲɛ lafiyabali, mankan walima ja dɔw minnu bɛ siran) olu bɛ don o kulu kɔnɔ.
  • Kan: Kan jugu tɛ, walima kalili nɔgɔman dɔrɔn, min tɛ cɛnimusoya ye.
  • Wɛrɛw: Hali bi cɛnimusoya, dɔrɔgu, walima wasako ɲɛfɔli jɛlen si tɛ yen.
  • A kɔrɔ: A bɛ fɛnw jira minnu bɛ se ka juguya kojugu denmisɛnnin fitininw ma, kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenya la siran siratigɛ la ani fariyako min bɛ bɔ dɔɔnin dɔɔnin.
