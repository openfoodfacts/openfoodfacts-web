Aw ye aw dɛmɛ ka Open Products Facts (Fɛn Dabɔlenw Tiɲɛw) Kɛ

All you need is a scan-do attitude! :-)

Yala an bɛ se ka fɛn dilannenw kunnafoniw kɛ kɛnɛ kan ani ka sabati wa? Yes we scan!

Everyone can contribute

Aw ka kɛ an ka jɛ-ka-baara, fu ani da wulilen kunnafonidilan kɔnɔ, fɛn minnu bɛ bɔ diɲɛ fan bɛɛ la!

Open Products Facts ye porozɛ ye min tɛ tɔnɔ ɲini, a bɛɛ kɛra bolomafaraw fɛ, an mago bɛ aw la.

Add products

Baara kɛ ni Open Food Facts app ye Android walima iPhone walasa ka fɛnw ka barɔdi sɛgɛsɛgɛ nɔgɔya la ka bɔ i ka so walima i ka sigida maganw na ani k’u tɔgɔw jaw bila.

Mobili porogaramuw farala ɲɔgɔn kan, o la sisan i bɛ se ka fɛnw, dumuniw, ɲɛgɛnw, ani sokɔnɔbaganw ka dumuniw sɛgɛsɛgɛ ka bɔ Open Food Facts porogaramu kelen na!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Application in sigi walasa ka dɛmɛ don fɛn dilannenw ka kɛnɛya la

Tell the world

Yala Open Products Facts ka di i ye wa? Tell others about it!

Aw bɛ se ka porozɛ in jira aw somɔgɔw n’aw teriw la, ka appli in sigicogo jira u la ani ka dɛmɛ don, ka bulɔgu sɛbɛn, ka Open Products Facts tila sosiyete mediaw kan.

And present it live! An ye Open Products Facts jira lamɛnnikɛlaw la minnu tɛ kelen ye kosɛbɛ sigida wɛrɛw la, wa an bɛ jaabi di tuma bɛɛ ni dusu ye kosɛbɛ n’an y’a jira ko min bɛ se ka kɛ ni fɛn dilannenw ka kunnafoniw dabɔra. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

An bɛ se ka baara kɛ ɲɔgɔn fɛ walasa ka sigida lamini sɛbɛnw, dilancogo jatew, ani fɛn dilannenw sariyaw dɛmɛ ani k’u fara u kan.

Start or join a local contributors community: add local products, recruit friends, present the project in local meetups and conferences etc.

Expert help needed

Fɛn dafalenw tiɲɛniw yiriwali fana bɛ dɔnniya kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenw ni dɔnniya de wajibiya yɔrɔ caman na:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    An mago bɛ dɛmɛ na walasa ka baarakɛlaw ka baarakɛcogo ɲuman jɔ Open Products Facts siti kan ani mobili app kan, walasa ka u dilanni ɲɛ, ka ɲɛjiralifɛnw dilan minnu bɛ nɔ bila mɔgɔw la a ɲɔgɔnnaw.

  • Development

    An bɛ yiriwali baara caman kɛ : Open Products Facts kɔkanna (Perl ni MongoDB), API (JSON), ɛntɛrinɛti yɔrɔ (HTML5, JS, Foundation), ani mobili app kan. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Min ka jugu kosɛbɛ, fɛn duman caman bɛ yen baarakɛlaw, dɛmɛbagaw ani baarakɛlaw seginbagaw bɛ minnu kɛ ni Open Products Facts ye, a bɛna diya kosɛbɛ ka mɔgɔ caman bila k’o dɔn. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    Fɛn dilannenw da wulilenw baarakɛcogo nafama ni fɔlɔ caman bɛ yen an tun bɛ se ka baara kɛ minnu kan ni sigida lamini baaradaw, fɛn dilannikɛlaw, ɲininikɛlaw, iniwɛrisitew, lakɔlisow, ONGw wdfl. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    aw bɛ se ka baro kɛ ni sigidamɔgɔw ye an ka forobaciyɛnkan . O ye yɔrɔ ɲuman ye ka ɲininkaliw kɛ, ka hakilinaw falenfalen, ani ka jɛ ka baara kɛ porozɛw kan.

  • Wiki

    An fana bɛ wiki min kɛ ka jɛkafɔ kɛ ka Open Products Facts n’a porozɛ fitininw sɛbɛn. An bɛ baara kɛ ni wiki fana ye walasa ka bamanankan bamanankan-falen ɲɛnabɔ ani ka baara kɛ taxonomies / hierarchies kan, minnu ye kuluw, labeliw wdfl ye.

Fɛɛrɛ caman wɛrɛ bɛ yen walasa ka dɛmɛ don Open Products Facts la, aw ka fara an kan Slack kan ani an ka baro daminɛ!
