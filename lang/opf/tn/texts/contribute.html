Everyone can contribute

E ba karolo ya polokelotshedimosetso ya rona ya tirisanommogo, ya mahala le e e bulegileng ya dikumo go tswa kwa lefatsheng lotlhe!

Open Products Facts ke porojeke e e sa direng dipoelo e e dirilweng gotlhelele ke baithaopi, re a go tlhoka.

Add products

Dirisa app ya Open Food Facts ya Android kgotsa iPhone go sekaseka bonolo khoutu ya dikumo go tswa kwa legaeng la gago kgotsa kwa mabenkeleng a selegae le go laisa ditshwantsho tsa dileibole tsa tsone.

Ditiriso tsa selulafouno di kopantswe, ka jalo jaanong o ka kgona go sekaseka dikumo, dijo, ditlolo, le dijo tsa diruiwa tsa mo gae go tswa mo tirisong e le nngwe ya Open Food Facts!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Tell the world

A o rata Dintlha tsa Dikumo tse di Buletsweng? Tell others about it!

O ka tlhagisetsa ba lelapa la gago le ditsala porojeke, wa ba bontsha kafa o ka tsenyang tiriswa ka gone le go tsenya letsogo, kwala poso ya blog, abelana ka Dintlha tsa Dikumo tse di Butsweng mo metsweding ya dikgang...

And present it live! Re tlhagisitse Dintlha tsa Dikumo tse di Buletsweng go bareetsi ba ba farologaneng thata mo maemong a a farologaneng thata, mme ka metlha re bona karabo e e matlhagatlhaga thata fa re bontsha se se ka dirwang fa tshedimosetso ya dikumo e bulegile. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Re ka sebetsa mmoho ho tshehetsa le ho eketsa dileibole tsa tikoloho ya lehae, dintlha tsa ho lokisa, le maemo a sehlahiswa.

Start or join a local contributors community: add local products, recruit friends, present the project in local meetups and conferences etc.

Expert help needed

Go tlhama Dintlha tsa Dikumo tse di Buletsweng gape go tlhoka kitso e e kgethegileng le boitseanape mo dikarolong tse dintsi tse di farologaneng:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    Re tlhoka thuso ya go aga maitemogelo a a botoka a modirisi mo webosaeteng ya Open Products Facts le mo app ya selulafouno, go tokafatsa moakanyetso wa tsone, go tlhama didirisiwa tsa tlhagiso tse di nang le tlhotlheletso jj.

  • Development

    Re na le tiro e ntsi ya tlhabololo e re tshwanetseng go e dira: mo backend ya Dintlha tsa Dikumo tse di Buletsweng (Perl le MongoDB), API (JSON), webosaete ya (HTML5, JS, Motheo), le app ya selulafouno. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Se se masisi le go feta, go na le dilo tse dintsi tse di monate tse badirisi, ba ba nang le seabe le ba ba dirisang gape ba di dirang ka Dintlha tsa Dikumo tse di Buletsweng, go ka nna molemo go dira gore batho ba le bantsi ba itse ka gone. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    Go na le ditiriso tse dintsi tse di kgatlhang le tsa ntlha tsa tshedimosetso e e bulegileng ya setlhagiswa tse re ka dirang mo go tsone le ditheo tsa tikologo, batlhagisi, babatlisisi, diyunibesithi, dikolo, diNGO jj. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    O ka buisana le baagi mo foramong ya rona ya . Ke lefelo le le siameng la go botsa dipotso, go abelana dikgopolo, le go dirisana mmogo mo diporojekeng.

  • Wiki

    Gape re na le wiki e re e dirisang go kwala ka tirisanommogo Dintlha tsa Dikumo tse di Buletsweng le diporojekepotlana tsa yone. Gape re dirisa wiki go laola dithanolo tsa le go dira ka ditlhopha / tatelano ya maemo tsa ditlhopha, dileibole jj.
