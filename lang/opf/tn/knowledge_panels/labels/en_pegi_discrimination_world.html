• Se e leng sone: Tlhaloso e e supa gore motshameko o na le ditshwantsho tsa lotso, bodumedi, bosetšhaba, kgotsa dikakanyo tse dingwe tse di ka rotloetsang letlhoo.
  • Bokao: Tekanyetso e e dirisiwa fa motshameko o rotloetsa kgotsa o galaletsa go tlhaola ditlhopha go ikaegilwe ka tshimologo, bodumedi, bosetšhaba, bong, thobalano, kgotsa diponagalo tse dingwe.
  • Tekanyetso e e kwa tlase: Tlhaloso eno gantsi e tlhagelela mo metshamekong e e lekanyeditsweng PEGI 18, ka ntlha ya go nna masisi ga diteng tse di ntseng jalo.
  • Metswedi: Webosaete ya Semmuso ya PEGI, Bothati jwa go Lekanya Metshameko
