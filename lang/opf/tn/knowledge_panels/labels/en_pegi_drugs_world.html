• Se e leng sone: Tlhaloso e e supa gore motshameko o kaya kgotsa o tshwantsha tiriso ya diritibatsi tse di seng ka fa molaong, bojalwa, kgotsa motsoko.
  • Maemo a Bokete:
  • Thotloetso/Tshwantsho ya Tiriso (PEGI 16): Thotloetso ya tiriso ya motsoko kgotsa bojalwa (sekao, moanelwa o bona mosola) kgotsa ditshwantsho tsa tiriso e e seng ka fa molaong ya diritibatsi.
  • Go kgatlha/Go rotloetsa (PEGI 18): Ditshwantsho tse di kgatlhang tsa diritibatsi tse di seng ka fa molaong kgotsa go rotloetsa go go bonalang ga diokobatsi tse di laolwang.
  • Tekanyetso e e kwa tlase: Tlhaloso eno e fitlhelwa fela mo metshamekong e e lekanyeditsweng PEGI 16 kgotsa PEGI 18.
