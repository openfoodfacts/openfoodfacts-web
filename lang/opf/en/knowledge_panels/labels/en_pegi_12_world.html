• What it is: Content is suitable for individuals aged 12 and over.
  • Key Characteristics:
    • Violence: More graphic or realistic violence towards fantasy characters, or non-realistic violence towards human-like characters. This can include more visible impact, but generally without gore. Implied violence to humans where the actual violence is not shown is also covered.
    • Fear/Horror: Stronger threat, dread, or graphic injury details, and more sustained horror sequences or disturbing images.
    • Language: Mild swearing and/or offensive language (short of sexual expletives).
    • Sex: Sexual innuendo or sexual posturing can be present. Erotic nudity or non-explicit sexual intercourse.
    • Gambling: May include elements that encourage or teach gambling (simulations of casino games, etc.).
  • Implication: Marks a significant step up in maturity, including more realistic violence, suggestive themes, and mild profanity.
  • Sources: PEGI Official Website, Uswitch - Parent's Guide to PEGI Ratings
