• What it is: Content is considered suitable for all age groups.
  • Key Characteristics:
    • Violence: Only very mild forms of violence are acceptable, usually in a comical, cartoonish, or child-like setting (e.g., slapstick, implied harm, non-realistic reactions).
    • Fear: No sounds or pictures that are likely to frighten young children.
    • Language: No bad language should be heard.
    • Other: No sexual content, drugs, or gambling.
  • Implication: This rating focuses on ensuring the content is safe and non-disturbing for the youngest audiences.
  • Sources: PEGI Official Website, Uswitch - Parent's Guide to PEGI Ratings
