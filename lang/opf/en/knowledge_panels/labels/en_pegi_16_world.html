• What it is: Content is suitable for individuals aged 16 and over.
  • Key Characteristics:
  • Violence: Depictions of violence (or sexual activity) that reach a stage that looks the same as would be expected in real life. This can include realistic violence towards human characters, often with depictions of death and injury. Gory or bloody violence may be present if in an arcade style.
  • Language: More extreme bad language, including sexual expletives or blasphemy.
  • Drugs: May include encouragement of tobacco or alcohol use, or depictions of illegal drug use.
  • Sex: Erotic nudity or sexual intercourse without visible genitals.
  • Gambling: May include elements that encourage or teach gambling.
  • Implication: Content at this level is considerably more mature and often includes realistic and potentially disturbing themes.
  • Sources: PEGI Official Website, Uswitch - Parent's Guide to PEGI Ratings
