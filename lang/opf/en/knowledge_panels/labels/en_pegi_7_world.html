• What it is: Content is suitable for children aged 7 and over.
  • Key Characteristics:
  • Violence: Very mild forms of violence are acceptable. This can be implied, non-detailed, or non-realistic violence, often directed towards fantasy characters. Very mild non-realistic violence to human-like characters may also feature.
  • Fear: Games with scenes or sounds that can possibly be frightening to younger children (e.g., unsettling atmosphere, some scary sounds or images) fall into this category.
  • Language: No bad language, or only very mild, non-sexual swearing.
  • Other: Still no explicit sexual content, drugs, or gambling.
  • Implication: Introduces elements that might be too intense for very young children, particularly in terms of fear and slightly more pronounced fantasy violence.
  • Sources: PEGI Official Website, Uswitch - Parent's Guide to PEGI Ratings
