• What it is: Content is suitable only for adults.
  • Key Characteristics:
  • Violence: Applied when the level of violence becomes "gross violence." This includes horrific methods of bringing death or severe injury, torture, decapitation, dismemberment, motiveless killing, or violence towards defenceless characters. Sexual violence and threat are also classified here.
  • Drugs: Glamorized portrayals of illegal drugs or prominent promotion of controlled substances.
  • Sex: Detailed or explicit sexual activity, including visible genitalia.
  • Gambling: Realistic gambling simulations.
  • Discrimination: May contain elements that encourage or teach discrimination.
  • Implication: This is the highest age rating and indicates content that is strictly for mature audiences due to its graphic and potentially disturbing nature.
  • Sources: PEGI Official Website, Uswitch - Parent's Guide to PEGI Ratings
