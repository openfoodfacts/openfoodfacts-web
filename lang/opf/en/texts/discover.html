A products database

Open Products Facts is a database of everyday products with environmental impact data, repairability scores, materials, and all the information we can find on product labels and packaging.

Made by everyone

Open Products Facts is a non-profit association of volunteers.

Contributors like you can add products from around the world using the Open Food Facts app for Android or iPhone or their camera to scan barcodes and upload pictures of products and their labels.

Get the app to start scanning products

For everyone

Data about products is of public interest and has to be open. The complete database is published as open data and can be reused by anyone and for any use. Check-out the cool reuses or make your own!

Make more sustainable product choices

Knowledge is power. Product knowledge is power to consume better!

  • Environmental Impact

    Discover the CO2 impact of products and make informed decisions about your environmental footprint. Open Products Facts helps you understand the environmental cost of the things you buy.

  • Repairability Score

    Check the repairability score of electronics and appliances. Support a circular economy by choosing products that can be repaired rather than replaced.

  • Find products

    Our search form includes dozens of criteria so that you can find products that match your environmental and sustainability criteria.

  • Decode labels

    Material composition, recycling codes, energy labels... Open Products Facts helps you to make sense of product information and labels.

Products for innovation and science

Our data can be reused for education, new products and services, research and more!

The Open Products Facts database is open data published under the Open Database Licence. Anyone can use it for any purpose - commercial or non-commercial - provided the source of the data is attributed and improvements to the database are shared in a similar way.

Education

Learn about the environmental impact of everyday products and understand the lifecycle of consumer goods.

Products and services

Open Products Facts data can be reused in both non-commercial and commercial projects, as long as improvements to the database are shared as well.

Science

Open Products Facts data can be used by scientists studying sustainability, circular economy, environmental impact, and consumer behavior. Help build the data for a more sustainable future!
