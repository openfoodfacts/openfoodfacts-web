Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Kuska llamk'ayta atiykuman yanapakuypaq chaymanta yapaypaq kiti pachamama etiquetakuna, allichaypaq puntuacionkuna chaymanta ruru kamachiykuna.

Start or join a local contributors community: add local products, recruit friends, present the project in local meetups and conferences etc.

Expert help needed

Kichasqa Rurukuna Chiqakuna ruwaypas achka imaymana ruwaykunapi sapaq yachaykunata chaymanta yachaykunata munan:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    Yanapakuyta necesitayku huk aswan allin user experiencia ruwanapaq Open Products Facts web kitipi chaymanta movil app kaqpi, allinchaypaq ruwayninku, ruwanapaq impactoyuq rikuchiy materialkuna etc.

  • Development

    Askha wiñachiy llamk'ay ruwanayku tiyan: Kichasqa Rurukuna Chiqakuna qhipa kaqpi (Perl chaymanta MongoDB), API (JSON), web kitipi (HTML5, JS, Fundación), chaymanta kuyuchina ruwanapi. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Aswan sinchi, achka q'uñi ruwaykuna kanku mayqinkunatachus ruwaqkuna, yanapaqkuna chaymanta wakmanta llamk'achiqkuna Kichasqa Rurukuna Chiqap kaqwan ruwanku, ancha allinmi kanman aswan runakuna chaymanta yachanankupaq. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    Kanmi achka munay chaymanta original ruwanakuna ruru kichasqa willakuykunamanta mayqinkunapichus llamk'ayta atiykuman pachamamamanta agenciakunawan, ruwaqkunawan, maskaqkunawan, hatun yachaywasikunawan, yachaywasikunawan, ONGkunawan hukkunawanpas. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    Aylluwan rimayta atinki foronisqapi. Allinmi tapukuy ruwanapaq, yuyaykunata rakinapaq, hinaspa proyectokunapi yanapanakunapaq.

  • Wiki

    Hinallataq huk wiki kaqniyuq kayku, chayta llamk'achiyku Kichasqa Rurukuna Chiqapkunata chaymanta huch'uy llamk'ayninkunata kuska qillqanaykupaq. Hinallataqmi wiki nisqawan t'ikraykunata kamachinapaq, hinallataq taxonomías / jerarquías nisqapi llamk'anapaq, categoría nisqakuna, etiquetakuna hukkunapas.

Aswan achka ñankuna kan Kichasqa Rurukuna Chiqap kaqpi yanapakuypaq, ama hina kaspa Slack kaqpi ñuqaykuwan kuskachakuy hinaspa rimanakuyta qallarisun!
