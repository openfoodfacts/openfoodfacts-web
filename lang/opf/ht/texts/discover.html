For everyone

Done sou pwodwi yo se nan enterè piblik la epi yo dwe ouvè a tout moun. The complete database is published as open data and can be reused by anyone and for any use. Check-out the cool reuses or make your own!

Fè chwa pwodwi ki pi dirab

Knowledge is power. Konesans pwodwi se pouvwa pou konsome pi byen!

  • Enpak sou anviwònman an

    Discover the CO2 impact of products and make informed decisions about your environmental footprint. Open Products Facts helps you understand the environmental cost of the things you buy.

  • Repairability Score

    Check the repairability score of electronics and appliances. Sipòte yon ekonomi sikilè lè w chwazi pwodwi ki ka repare olye pou yo ranplase.

  • Find products

    Fòm rechèch nou an gen ladan l plizyè douzèn kritè pou ou ka jwenn pwodwi ki koresponn ak kritè anviwònman ak dirabilite ou yo.

  • Decode labels

    Konpozisyon materyèl, kòd resiklaj, etikèt enèji... Open Products Facts ede ou konprann enfòmasyon ak etikèt pwodwi yo.

Pwodwi pou inovasyon ak syans

Our data can be reused for education, new products and services, research and more!

Baz done Open Products Facts la se done ouvè ki pibliye anba Lisans Open Database la. Anyone can use it for any purpose - commercial or non-commercial - provided the source of the data is attributed and improvements to the database are shared in a similar way.
