Everyone can contribute

Vin fè pati baz done kolaboratif, gratis ak ouvè nou an ki gen pwodwi atravè lemond!

Open Products Facts se yon pwojè san bi likratif ki fèt antyèman pa volontè, nou vrèman bezwen ou.

Add products

Sèvi ak aplikasyon Open Food Facts pou Android oubyen iPhone pou w ka fasilman eskane kòd bar pwodwi ki lakay ou oswa nan magazen lokal yo epi telechaje foto etikèt yo.

Aplikasyon mobil yo te fusionné, kidonk kounye a ou ka eskane pwodwi, manje, pwodui kosmetik ak manje bèt kay nan menm aplikasyon Open Food Facts la!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Enstale aplikasyon an pou kontribye nan transparans pwodwi yo

Di mond lan

Èske ou renmen Enfòmasyon sou Pwodwi Ouvè yo? Di lòt moun sou sa!

Ou ka prezante pwojè a bay fanmi w ak zanmi w, montre yo kijan pou yo enstale aplikasyon an epi kontribye, ekri yon pòs blog, pataje Open Products Facts sou rezo sosyal yo...

And present it live! Nou prezante Open Products Facts bay odyans trè diferan nan anviwònman trè diferan, epi nou toujou jwenn yon repons trè chofe lè nou montre sa ki ka fèt lè done pou pwodwi yo ouvè. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Fè li lokal

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Nou ka travay ansanm pou sipòte epi ajoute etikèt anviwònman lokal yo, nòt reparabilite, ak estanda pwodwi yo.

Start or join a local contributors community: add local products, recruit friends, present the project in local meetups and conferences etc.

Expert help needed

Devlope Open Products Facts mande tou konesans espesyalize ak ekspètiz nan plizyè domèn diferan:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    Nou bezwen èd pou nou bati yon pi bon eksperyans itilizatè sou sit entènèt Open Products Facts la ak aplikasyon mobil lan, pou amelyore konsepsyon yo, pou kreye materyèl prezantasyon ki gen enpak, eksetera.

  • Development

    Nou gen anpil travay devlopman pou nou fè: sou backend Open Products Facts la (Perl ak MongoDB), API a (JSON), sit entènèt la (HTML5, JS, Foundation), ak aplikasyon mobil lan. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Èske w se youn nan yo?

  • Kominikasyon

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Pi seryezman, gen anpil bagay enteresan ke itilizatè yo, kontribitè yo ak moun ki reyitilize yo fè ak Open Products Facts, li ta bon pou fè plis moun konnen sa. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    Gen anpil aplikasyon enteresan ak orijinal nan done ouvè pwodwi ke nou ta ka travay sou yo ak ajans anviwònman, manifaktirè, chèchè, inivèsite, lekòl, ONG elatriye. Petèt ou deja gen yon lide? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Ann pale!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. Li konplètman chanje fason nou travay ansanm. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Fowòm

    Ou ka diskite ak kominote a sou fowòm nou an. Se yon bon kote pou poze kesyon, pataje lide, epi kolabore sou pwojè.

  • Wiki

    Nou genyen tou yon wiki ke nou itilize pou dokimante Open Products Facts ak sou-pwojè li yo an kolaborasyon. Nou itilize wiki la tou pou jere tradiksyon yo epi pou travay sou taksonomi / yerachi kategori yo, etikèt yo, elatriye.

Gen anpil lòt fason pou kontribye nan Open Products Facts, tanpri rantre nan nou sou Slack epi an nou kòmanse diskisyon an!
