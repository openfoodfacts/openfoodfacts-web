• Kuyini: Okuqukethwe kulungele izingane ezineminyaka engu-7 nangaphezulu.
  • Izici Ezibalulekile:
  • Udlame: Izinhlobo zodlame ezithambile ziyamukeleka. Lokhu kungasho udlame olungenamininingwane, noma olungelona iqiniso, ngokuvamile oluqondiswe kubalingiswa abayinganekwane. Udlame olungenangqondo oluncane kakhulu lwabalingiswa abafana nomuntu lungase lufakwe.
  • Ukwesaba: Imidlalo enezigcawu noma imisindo engase yethuse izingane ezincane (isb., umoya ongaphatheki kahle, imisindo ethile esabekayo noma izithombe) iwela kulesi sigaba.
  • Ulimi: Alukho ulimi olubi, noma inhlamba emnene kuphela, engahlangene nocansi.
  • Okunye: Namanje akukho okuqukethwe kocansi okuyingcaca, izidakamizwa, noma ukugembula.
  • Okushiwo: Yethula izici ezingase ziqine kakhulu ezinganeni ezincane kakhulu, ikakhulukazi mayelana nokwesaba kanye nodlame lwenganekwane olugqame kakhudlwana.
