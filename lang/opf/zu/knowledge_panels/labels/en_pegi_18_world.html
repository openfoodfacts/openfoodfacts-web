• Kuyini: Okuqukethwe kufanelekile abantu abadala kuphela.
  • Izici Ezibalulekile:
  • Udlame: Lusetshenziswa uma izinga lodlame liba "udlame olubi." Lokhu kuhlanganisa izindlela ezisabekayo zokuletha ukufa noma ukulimala okubi kakhulu, ukuhlushwa, ukunqunywa ikhanda, ukunqunywa, ukubulawa okungenanjongo, noma udlame olubhekiswe kubalingiswa abangenakuzivikela. Udlame locansi nokusongelwa nakho kuhlukaniswa lapha.
  • Izidakamizwa: Ukuvezwa okukhangayo kwezidakamizwa ezingekho emthethweni noma ukuphromotha okuvelele kwezinto ezilawulwayo.
  • Ucansi: Imisebenzi yocansi enemininingwane noma ebekela ingcaca, kuhlanganisa nezitho zangasese ezibonakalayo.
  • Ukugembula: Imifanekiso yokugembula engokoqobo.
  • Ukucwasa: Kungase kuqukathe izici ezikhuthaza noma ezifundisa ukucwasa.
  • Okushiwo: Lesi isilinganiso esiphezulu seminyaka yobudala futhi sibonisa okuqukethwe okwenzelwe izethameli ezikhulile impela ngenxa yengcaca yakho kanye nemvelo engase iphazamise.
