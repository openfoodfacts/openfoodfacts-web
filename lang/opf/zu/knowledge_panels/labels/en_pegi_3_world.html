• Kuyini: Okuqukethwe kuthathwa njengokufanelekile kuwo wonke amaqembu obudala.
  • Izici Eziyinhloko:
  • Udlame: Izinhlobo zodlame ezithambile kuphela ezamukelekayo, ngokuvamile endaweni ehlekisayo, ekhathuni, noma efana neyengane (isb., i-slapstick, ukulimala okushiwo, ukusabela okungekona okwangempela).
  • Ukwesaba: Ayikho imisindo noma izithombe ezingathusa izingane ezincane.
  • Ulimi: Alukho ulimi olubi okufanele luzwakale.
  • Okunye: Akukho okuqukethwe kwezocansi, izidakamizwa, noma ukugembula.
  • Okushiwo: Lesi silinganiso sigxile ekuqinisekiseni ukuthi okuqukethwe kuphephile futhi akuphazamisi izethameli ezincane kakhulu.
