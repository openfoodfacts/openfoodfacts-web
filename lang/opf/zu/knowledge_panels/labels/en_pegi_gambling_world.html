• Kuyini: Lesi sichazi sikhombisa ukuthi umdlalo uqukethe izinto ezikhuthaza noma ezifundisa ukugembula.
  • Scope: Ibhekisela ekufanisweni kwemidlalo yenhlanhla evamile evame ukutholakala emakhasino noma emahholo okugembula (isb., imidlalo yamakhadi edlelwa imali, ukubheja emijahweni).
  • Isilinganiso Esincane: Lesi sichazi sivela kuphela emidlalweni elinganiselwe PEGI 12, 16, noma 18. Kusukela ngo-2020, imidlalo emisha elinganiselwe enokulingiswa kokugembula okungokoqobo ihlezi i-PEGI 18.
  • Inothi kuma-Loot Boxes: Ukuba khona "Kwezinto Ezikhokhelwayo Ezingahleliwe" (okuvame ukubizwa ngokuthi amabhokisi okuphanga) kuyincazelo yokuqukethwe ehlukile ("I-In-Game Purchases") kanye nemidlalo etholakalayo ye-GI (i-GI ephansi ingatholakala) kanye nemidlalo ye-GI ephansi.
  • Imithombo: Iwebhusayithi Esemthethweni Ye-PEGI, Igunya Lokulinganisa Imidlalo
