• Kuyini: Lesi sichazi sibonisa ukuthi umdlalo unikeza ukuthenga okudijithali ngemali yangempela.
  • Scope: Ihlanganisa izinhlobo ezahlukene zokuthenga okuphakathi kwegeyimu:
  • Izinto zezimonyo: Izikhumba, imizwa, izithuthukisi ezibonakalayo.
  • Izinzuzo zegeyimu: Amandla aphezulu, uhlobo lwemali, izithuthukisi.
  • Izandiso/I-DLC: Okuqukethwe okwengeziwe noma indaba.
  • Izinto Ezikhokhelwa Ngokungahleliwe (Amabhokisi Okuphanga): Lapho abadlali bengazi kahle ukuthi batholani ngaphambi kokuthenga (isb., amaphakethe amakhadi, amakhreyithi ezinto).
  • Qaphela: Lesi sichazi akusi isilinganiso sobudala kodwa siyisithonjana solwazi. Ingavela kumageyimu ananoma yisiphi isilinganiso sobudala se-PEGI, okuhlanganisa i-PEGI 3.
  • Okukhathazayo (ikakhulukazi izingane): Amathuba okusebenzisa imali ngokweqile, ukukhuthazwa kokuziphatha okufana nokugembula ngezinto ezingahleliwe, kanye nengcindezi yokuthenga ukuze uthuthuke.
  • Imithombo: Iwebhusayithi Esemthethweni Ye-PEGI, Indawo Yomzali - Ukuqonda izilinganiso ze-PEGI
