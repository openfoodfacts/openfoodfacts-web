• Kuyini: Lesi sichazi sikhombisa ukuthi umdlalo uqukethe imifanekiso yodlame.
  • Amazinga Okuqina:
  • Okumaphakathi/Opopayi (PEGI 3/7): Udlame oluthambile kakhulu, olungelona olwangempela (isb., i-slapstick, ukulimala kwekhathuni, noma udlame oluhlotshaniswayo) ngokuvamile oluqondiswe kubalingiswa benganekwane.
  • Okumaphakathi (PEGI 12): Udlame oluningi oluyingcaca noma lwangempela olubhekiswe kubalingiswa benganekwane, noma udlame olungenangqondo olubhekiswe kubalingiswa abafana nabantu (isb., ukulimala okuncane, ukungagazi).
  • Oluqinile (PEGI 16): Udlame lwangempela olubhekiswe kubalingiswa abangabantu, okuhlanganisa ukuvezwa kokufa nokulimala. Udlame olubi noma oluchitha igazi lungase lube khona uma kuyisitayela se-arcade.
  • Okubi (PEGI 18): Izindlela ezishaqisayo zokuletha ukufa noma ukulimala kanzima, okuhlanganisa ukuhlushwa, ukunqunywa ikhanda, ukunqunywa, noma udlame olungenanjongo olubhekiswe kwabalingiswa abasengozini/abangenacala. Udlame locansi nokusongelwa nakho kuhlukaniswa lapha.
  • Okushiwo: Uma ukuphakama kwesilinganiso sobudala se-PEGI esihlotshaniswayo, kulapho udlame oluboniswayo lushubile futhi lungokoqobo.
