• Kuyini: Lesi sichazi sikhombisa ukuthi umdlalo uqukethe izinto ezingasabisa noma zethuke.
  • Amazinga Okuqina:
  • Okwethusayo (I-PEGI 7): Izithombe noma imisindo engase yethuke noma yethuse ezinganeni ezincane (isb., umculo oshisayo, indawo esabekayo, ukukhala kwangemuva). Akubandakanyi udlame oluqondile.
  • Okwethusayo (I-PEGI 12+): Ukusongela okuqinile, ukwesaba, noma imininingwane yokulimala eyingcaca, nokulandelana okusabisayo okuqhubekayo noma izithombe eziphazamisayo.
  • Okushiwo: Igxila kumthelela wengqondo nokuqukethwe okuphazamisayo, kunodlame oluyingcaca.
