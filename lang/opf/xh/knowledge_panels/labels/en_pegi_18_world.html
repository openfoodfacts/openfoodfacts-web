  • IiMpawu eziPhambili:
  • Ubundlobongela: Busetyenziswa xa inqanaba lobundlobongela liba "nobundlobongela obugqithisileyo." Oku kuquka iindlela ezimanyumnyezi zokuzisa ukufa okanye ukwenzakala okumanyumnyezi, intuthumbo, ukunqunyulwa kwentloko, ukunqunyulwa, ukubulala okungenanjongo, okanye ubundlobongela kubalinganiswa abangakwazi kuzikhusela. Ubundlobongela obuphathelele kwezesondo kunye nezoyikiso nazo zicalulwa apha.
  • Iziyobisi: Imifanekiso encomekayo yeziyobisi ezingekho mthethweni okanye ukukhuthazwa okubalaseleyo kwezinto ezilawulwayo.
  • Ukwabelana ngesondo: Izenzo zesondo ezineenkcukacha okanye ezicacileyo, kuquka amalungu obuni abonakalayo.
  • Ukungcakaza: Ukulinganisa okuyinyaniso kongcakazo.
  • Ucalucalulo: Isenokuba nezinto ezikhuthaza okanye ezifundisa ucalucalulo.
  • Intsingiselo: Eli lolona mlinganiselo liphezulu lobudala kwaye libonisa umxholo ojoliswe ngokungqongqo kubaphulaphuli abaqolileyo ngenxa yomzobo kunye nendalo enokubanokuphazamisa.
