• Iyintoni: Esi sichazi sibonisa ukuba umdlalo uqulathe izinto ezinokoyikisa okanye zoyike.
  • Amanqanaba oBungqongqo:
  • Eyothusayo (PEGI 7): Imifanekiso okanye izandi ezinokuthi zoyike okanye zoyike kubantwana abancinci (umz., umculo okhathazayo, indawo eyoyikisayo, izikhalo zangasemva). Ayibandakanyi ubundlobongela obuthe ngqo.
  • Isoyikiso (PEGI 12+): Isisongelo esinamandla, ukunkwantya, okanye iinkcukacha zokwenzakala okumzobo, kunye nolandelelwano olothusayo oluqhubekayo okanye imifanekiso ephazamisayo.
  • Intsingiselo: Igxile kwimpembelelo yengqondo kunye nomxholo ophazamisayo, kunobundlobongela obucacileyo.
