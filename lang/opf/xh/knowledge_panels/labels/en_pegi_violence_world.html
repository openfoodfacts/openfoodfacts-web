• Iyintoni: Esi sichazi sibonisa ukuba umdlalo uqulethe imiboniso yobundlobongela.
  • Amanqanaba oBungqongqo:
  • Iphakathi/Ikhathuni (PEGI 3/7): Ubumnene kakhulu, ubundlobongela obungenanyani (umzekelo, intonga yempama, ukwenzakala kwekhathuni, okanye ubundlobongela obucingelwayo) obusoloko bujoliswe kubalinganiswa bentelekelelo.
  • Phakathi (PEGI 12): Ubundlobongela obungaphaya bomzobo okanye obuyinyani obubhekiselele kubalinganiswa bentelekelelo, okanye ubundlobongela obungekho ngqiqweni kubalinganiswa abafana nabantu (umzekelo, ukonzakala okungephi, akukho gazi).
  • Oluqilima (PEGI 16): Ubundlobongela obuyinyani kubalinganiswa abangabantu, kuquka nemifanekiso yokufa nokwenzakala. Ubundlobongela obunoburhalarhume okanye olunegazi lunokubakho ukuba lukwindlela ye-arcade.
  • Gross (PEGI 18): Iindlela ezoyikekayo zokuzisa ukufa okanye ukwenzakala okumandundu, kuquka intuthumbo, ukunqunyulwa intloko, ukunqunyulwa, okanye ubundlobongela obungenanjongo ngokuchasene nabalinganiswa abasesichengeni/abamsulwa. Ubundlobongela obuphathelele kwezesondo kunye nezoyikiso nazo zicalulwa apha.
  • Intsingiselo: Okukhona iphezulu i-PEGI yomlinganiso wobudala onxulumeneyo, kokukhona ubundlobongela obuboniswayo buqina kwaye buyinyani.
  • Imithombo: IWebhsayithi esemthethweni ye-PEGI, IGunya lokulinganisa iMidlalo
