• Iyintoni: Umxholo ulungele abantwana abaneminyaka esi-7 nangaphezulu.
  • IiMpawu eziPhambili:
  • Ubundlobongela: Iindlela ezingeyomfuneko kakhulu zobundlobongela zamkelekile. Oku kungachazwa, okungachazwanga, okanye ubundlobongela obungeyonyani, obusoloko bujoliswe kubalinganiswa bentelekelelo. Ubundlobongela obungeyonyani obuphakathi kakhulu kubalinganiswa abafana nabantu busenokubonakala.
  • Uloyiko: Imidlalo enemiboniso okanye izandi ezinokuboyikisa abantwana abancinci (umzekelo, umoya ongazinzisiyo, izandi ezoyikisayo okanye imifanekiso) iwela kolu luhlu.
  • Ulwimi: Akukho lwimi lubi, okanye izithuko ezithambileyo nezingabhekiseli ngokwesondo.
  • Okunye: Akukho mxholo wesondo ocacileyo, iziyobisi, okanye ungcakazo.
  • Intsingiselo: Yazisa izinto ezinokuba qatha kakhulu kubantwana abancinci kakhulu, ngakumbi malunga noloyiko kunye nobundlobongela obuvakala ngakumbi bephupha.
