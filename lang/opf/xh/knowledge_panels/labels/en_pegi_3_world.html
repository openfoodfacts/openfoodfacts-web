• Iyintoni: Umxholo uthathwa njengofanelekile kuwo onke amaqela obudala.
  • IiMpawu eziPhambili:
  • Ubundlobongela: Kuphela ziintlobo zobundlobongela ezibuthathaka kakhulu ezamkelekileyo, ngokuqhelekileyo kwimeko ehlekisayo, eyoopopayi, okanye efana nomntwana (umz., i-slapstick, ukwenzakala okuchazwayo, ukusabela okungeyonyani).
  • Uloyiko: Akukho zandi okanye imifanekiso enokoyikisa abantwana abancinci.
  • Ulwimi: Akukho lwimi lubi kufuneka luviwe.
  • Okunye: Akukho mxholo wesondo, iziyobisi, okanye ungcakazo.
  • Intsingiselo: Olu thelekelelo lujolise ekuqinisekiseni ukuba umxholo ukhuselekile kwaye awuphazamisi kubaphulaphuli abancinci.
