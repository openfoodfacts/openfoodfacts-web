• Iyintoni: Esi sichazi sibonisa ukuba umdlalo uqulethe izinto ezikhuthaza okanye ezifundisa ungcakazo.
  • Scope: Ibhekiselele kufaniso lwemidlalo yamathuba yakudala edla ngokufumaneka kwiikhasino okanye kwiholo yokungcakaza (umz., imidlalo yamakhadi edlalwa ngemali, ukubheja kugqatso).
  • Umlinganiselo ophantsi: Esi sichazi sivela kuphela kwimidlalo enikwe umlinganiselo PEGI 12, 16, okanye 18. Ukusukela ngo-2020, imidlalo esanda ukulinganiswa kunye nemilinganiselo yongcakazo eyinyani isoloko i-PEGI 18.
  • Qaphela kwiiBhokisi zokuLawula: Ubukho bezinto "eziHlawulweyo ezingaQekanga" (ezihlala zibizwa ngokuba ziibhokisi zokuphanga) yinkcazo yomxholo eyahlukileyo ("I-In-Game Purchase) kunye nemidlalo ephantsi ye-PE inokufunyanwa (i-7 yemidlalo enokufunyanwa).
