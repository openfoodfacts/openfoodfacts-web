• Iyintoni: Umxholo ulungele abantu abaneminyaka eyi-12 nangaphezulu.
  • IiNgcaciso eziPhambili:
  • Ubundlobongela: Ubundlobongela obungaphaya bomzobo okanye obuyinyani obubhekiselele kubalinganiswa bentelekelelo, okanye ubundlobongela obungeyonyani kubalinganiswa abafana nabantu. Oku kunokubandakanya impembelelo ebonakala ngakumbi, kodwa ngokubanzi ngaphandle kokudubula. Ubundlobongela obuchazwayo ebantwini apho olona bundlobongela bungaboniswanga nalo luyabandakanywa.
  • Uloyiko/Ukoyikeka: Isisongelo esinamandla, ukoyikwa, okanye iinkcukacha zokwenzakala okumzobo, kunye nolandelelwano oloyikekayo oluqhubekayo okanye imifanekiso ephazamisayo.
  • Ulwimi: Ukuthuka kunye/okanye ulwimi olukhubekisayo (olufutshane lwezithuko zesini).
  • Ukwabelana ngesondo: Iintetho zesondo okanye ukuthunyelwa ngokwesondo kunokubakho. Ubunqunu obuvuselela inkanuko okanye ukwabelana ngesondo ngokungafihlisiyo.
  • Ukungcakaza: Kusenokuquka izinto ezikhuthaza okanye ezifundisa ungcakazo (ukulinganisa imidlalo yekhasino, njl. njl.).
  • Intsingiselo: Iphawula inyathelo elibalulekileyo lokukhula, kuquka ubundlobongela obuyinyani, imixholo ecekisayo, kunye nezinyeliso ezingephi.
