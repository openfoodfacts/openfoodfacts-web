• Iyintoni: Esi sichazi sichaza ukuba umdlalo ubonelela ngokuthenga ngedijithali ngemali yokwenyani.
  • Scope: Uquka iindlela ezahlukeneyo zokuthenga ngaphakathi komdlalo:
  • Izinto zokuthambisa: Izikhumba, iimvakalelo, izincedisi ezibonakalayo.
  • Iinzuzo zomdlalo: Amandla aphezulu, imali, izincedisi.
  • ** Ukwandiswa/i-DLC:** Umxholo okanye ibali elongezelelweyo.
  • Izinto eziHlawulweyo ngokungaQekanga (Iibhokisi zokuphanga): Apho abadlali bangazi kakuhle ukuba bafumana ntoni na phambi kokuthenga (umzekelo, iipakethi zamakhadi, iikhreyithi zezinto).
  • Qaphela: Esi sichazi ayisiyo isikali sobudala kodwa luphawu lolwazi. Inokuvela kwimidlalo nangaliphi na izinga le-PEGI lobudala, ukuquka i-PEGI 3.
  • Iinkxalabo (ingakumbi ebantwaneni): Enokubakho kwinkcitho egqithisileyo, inkuthazo yokuziphatha okufana nokungcakaza ngezinto ezingacwangciswanga, kunye noxinzelelo lokuthenga ukuqhubela phambili.
  • Imithombo: Iwebhusayithi ye-PEGI esemthethweni, INdawo yoMzali - Ukuqonda iireyithingi zePEGI
