• Iyintoni: Umxholo ulungele abantu abaneminyaka eyi-16 nangaphezulu.
  • IiMpawu eziPhambili:
  • Ubundlobongela: Imiboniso yobundlobongela (okanye izenzo zesondo) ezifikelela kwinqanaba elijongeka ngokufanayo neliya kulindeleka kubomi bokwenyani. Oku kunokubandakanya ubundlobongela obuyinyani kubalinganiswa babantu, rhoqo ngemifanekiso yokufa nokwenzakala. Ubundlobongela obunoburhalarhume okanye olunegazi lunokubakho ukuba lukwindlela ye-arcade.
  • Ulwimi: Ulwimi olubi ngokugqithisileyo, kuquka izithuko zesini okanye ukunyelisa.
  • Iziyobisi: Asenokuquka inkuthazo yokusetyenziswa kwecuba okanye utywala, okanye imiboniso yokusetyenziswa kweziyobisi ezingekho mthethweni.
  • Isondo: Ubunqunu obuvuselela inkanuko okanye ukwabelana ngesondo ngaphandle kwamalungu esini abonakalayo.
  • Ukungcakaza: Kusenokuquka izinto ezikhuthaza okanye ezifundisa ungcakazo.
  • Intsingiselo: Umxholo kweli nqanaba ukhule kakhulu kwaye uhlala ubandakanya imixholo eyinyani kunye nenokuthi iphazamise.
