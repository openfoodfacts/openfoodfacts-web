Everyone can contribute

Yiba yinxalenye yogcino lwethu lwentsebenziswano, lwasimahla noluvulekileyo lweemveliso ezivela kwihlabathi liphela!

Vula iiMveliso zeeMveliso yiprojekthi engenzi nzuzo eyenziwe ngokupheleleyo ngamavolontiya, siyakudinga.

Add products

Sebenzisa i-Open Food Facts ye- Android okanye iPhone ukujonga ngokulula ibhakhowudi yeemveliso ezisuka ekhayeni lakho okanye kwiivenkile zasekuhlaleni kwaye ulayishe imifanekiso yeeleyibhile zazo.

Ii-apps eziphathwayo zidityanisiwe, ke ngoku unokuskena iimveliso, ukutya, izithambiso, kunye nokutya kwesilwanyana sasekhaya kwi-app efanayo ye-Open Food Facts!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Tell the world

Ngaba uyathanda iiMveliso eziVulekileyo zeeMveliso? Tell others about it!

Ungabonisa iprojekthi kusapho kunye nabahlobo bakho, ubabonise indlela yokufaka i-app kunye negalelo, bhala iposti yebhlog, wabelane ngeeMveliso eziVulelekileyo kwimidiya yoluntu...

And present it live! Sibonise iiMveliso eziVulekileyo zeeMveliso kubaphulaphuli abahluke kakhulu kwiisetingi ezahlukeneyo, kwaye sihlala sifumana impendulo enomdla kakhulu xa sibonisa into enokwenziwa xa idatha yeemveliso ivuliwe. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Singasebenza kunye ukuxhasa kunye nokongeza iilebhile zendalo esingqongileyo, amanqaku okulungisa, kunye nemigangatho yemveliso.

Start or join a local contributors community: add local products, recruit friends, present the project in local meetups and conferences etc.

Expert help needed

Ukuphuhlisa iiMveliso eziVulelekileyo zeeMveliso kufuna ulwazi olukhethekileyo kunye nobuchule kwiindawo ezininzi ezahlukeneyo:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    Sidinga uncedo lokwakha amava omsebenzisi angcono kwiwebhusayithi yeeMveliso zeeMveliso ezivulekileyo kunye ne-app yeselula, ukuphucula uyilo lwabo, ukudala imathiriyeli yokubonisa impembelelo njl.

  • Development

    Sinomsebenzi omningi wokuphuhlisa: kwi-Open Products Facts backend (Perl kunye ne-MongoDB), i-API (JSON), indawo yewebhu (HTML5, JS, Foundation), kunye ne-app yeselula. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Okubaluleke ngakumbi, zininzi izinto ezipholileyo ezenziwa ngabasebenzisi, abaxhasi kunye nabasebenzisi kwakhona ngeeMveliso zeeMveliso eziVulekileyo, kuya kuba kuhle ukwenza abantu abaninzi bazi ngayo. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    Zininzi izinto ezinomdla kunye nezicelo zokuqala zedatha evuliweyo esinokusebenza kuyo neearhente zokusingqongileyo, abavelisi, abaphandi, iiyunivesithi, izikolo, ii-NGOs njl.njl. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    Unokuxoxa noluntu kwiforum yethu . Yindawo entle yokubuza imibuzo, ukwabelana ngezimvo, kunye nokusebenzisana kwiiprojekthi.

  • Wiki

    Sikwanayo ne- wiki esiyisebenzisayo ukuze sibhale ngentsebenziswano iiMveliso zeeMveliso zeeNyaniso kunye neeprojekthi eziphantsi kwayo. Sikwasebenzisa i-wiki ukulawula iinguqulelo kunye nokusebenza kwi i-taxonomies / hierarchies yeendidi, iilebhile njl.

Kukho iindlela ezininzi zokufaka isandla kwiiMveliso eziVulelekileyo, nceda usijoyine kwi-Slack kwaye masiqale ingxoxo!
