Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Mir kënnen zesumme schaffen, fir lokal Ëmweltlabelen, Reparaturbarkeets-Scores a Produktnormen z'ënnerstëtzen an derbäizesetzen.

Start or join a local contributors community: add local products, recruit friends, present the project in local meetups and conferences etc.

Expert help needed

D'Entwécklung vun Open Products Facts erfuerdert och spezialiséiert Wëssen an Expertise a ville verschiddene Beräicher:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    Mir brauchen Hëllef fir eng besser Benotzererfarung op der Open Products Facts Websäit an der mobiler App opzebauen, fir hiren Design vun ze verbesseren, fir beandrockend Presentatiounsmaterialien ze kreéieren etc.

  • Development

    Mir hunn nach vill Entwécklungsaarbecht ze maachen: um Open Products Facts Backend (Perl a MongoDB), der API (JSON), der Websäit (HTML5, JS, Foundation) an der mobiler App. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Méi eescht, et gi vill cool Saachen, déi Benotzer, Mataarbechter a Wiederverwender mat Open Products Facts maachen, et wier super, méi Leit doriwwer Bescheed ze soen. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    Et gi vill interessant an originell Uwendungen vun oppene Produktdaten, un deenen mir mat Ëmweltagenturen, Hiersteller, Fuerscher, Universitéiten, Schoulen, ONGen etc. zesummeschaffe kéinten. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    Dir kënnt mat der Communautéit op eisem Forumdiskutéieren. Et ass eng gutt Plaz fir Froen ze stellen, Iddien ze deelen a Projeten zesummenzeschaffen.

  • Wiki

    Mir hunn och e Wiki , deen mir benotze fir Open Products Facts an seng Ënnerprojeten zesummen ze dokumentéieren. Mir benotzen och de Wiki fir Iwwersetzungen ze verwalten an un den Taxonomien / Hierarchien vu Kategorien, Etiketten etc. ze schaffen.

Et gëtt vill méi Méiglechkeeten, fir zu Open Products Facts bäizedroen, maacht mat bei eis op Slack a loosst eis d'Diskussioun ufänken!
