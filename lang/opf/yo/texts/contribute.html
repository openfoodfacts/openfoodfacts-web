Add products

Lo ohun elo Awọn Otitọ Ounjẹ Ṣii fun Android > tabi iPhone lati ṣawari awọn koodu iwọle ti awọn ọja lati ile tabi awọn ile itaja agbegbe ati gbejade awọn aworan ti awọn aami wọn.

Awọn ohun elo alagbeka naa ti dapọ, nitorinaa o le ṣe ọlọjẹ awọn ọja, ounjẹ, awọn ohun ikunra, ati ounjẹ ọsin lati inu ohun elo Awọn Otitọ Ounjẹ Ṣiṣii kanna!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Tell the world

Ṣe o fẹran Awọn Otitọ Awọn ọja Ṣii? Tell others about it!

O le ṣafihan iṣẹ akanṣe naa si ẹbi ati awọn ọrẹ rẹ, fihan wọn bi wọn ṣe le fi app sori ẹrọ ati ṣe alabapin, kọ ifiweranṣẹ bulọọgi kan, pin Awọn Otitọ Awọn ọja Ṣii sori media awujọ…

And present it live! A ti ṣafihan Awọn Otitọ Awọn ọja Ṣii si awọn olugbo ti o yatọ pupọ ni awọn eto oriṣiriṣi pupọ, ati a nigbagbogbo gba esi itara pupọ nigbati a ba ṣafihan kini ohun ti o le ṣee ṣe nigbati data fun awọn ọja ba ṣii. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

A le ṣiṣẹ papọ lati ṣe atilẹyin ati ṣafikun awọn aami agbegbe agbegbe, awọn iṣiro atunṣe, ati awọn iṣedede ọja.

Start or join a local contributors community: add local products, recruit friends, present the project in local meetups and conferences etc.

Expert help needed

Idagbasoke Awọn Otitọ Awọn ọja Ṣii tun nilo imọ amọja ati oye ni ọpọlọpọ awọn agbegbe oriṣiriṣi:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    A nilo iranlọwọ lati kọ iriri olumulo to dara julọ lori oju opo wẹẹbu Awọn Otitọ Awọn ọja Ṣii ati ohun elo alagbeka, lati mu ilọsiwaju apẹrẹ wọn, lati ṣẹda awọn ohun elo igbejade ti o ni ipa ati bẹbẹ lọ.

  • Development

    A ni ọpọlọpọ awọn iṣẹ idagbasoke lati ṣe: lori Open Products Facts backend (Perl ati MongoDB), API (JSON), oju opo wẹẹbu (HTML5, JS, Foundation), ati ohun elo alagbeka. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Ni pataki diẹ sii, ọpọlọpọ awọn ohun tutu lo wa ti awọn olumulo, awọn oluranlọwọ ati awọn atunlo ṣe pẹlu Awọn Otitọ Awọn ọja Ṣii, yoo jẹ ohun nla lati jẹ ki eniyan diẹ sii mọ nipa rẹ. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    Ọpọlọpọ awọn ohun elo ti o nifẹ ati atilẹba ti data ṣiṣi ọja ti a le ṣiṣẹ lori pẹlu awọn ile-iṣẹ ayika, awọn aṣelọpọ, awọn oniwadi, awọn ile-ẹkọ giga, awọn ile-iwe, awọn NGO ati bẹbẹ lọ. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    O le jiroro pẹlu agbegbe lori apejọ wa. O jẹ aaye nla lati beere awọn ibeere, pin awọn imọran, ati ifowosowopo lori awọn iṣẹ akanṣe.

  • Wiki

    A tun ni wiki ti a lo lati ṣe iwe-ifowosowopo Awọn Otitọ Awọn ọja Ṣii ati awọn iṣẹ akanṣe rẹ. A tun lo wiki lati ṣakoso awọn itumọ ati lati ṣiṣẹ lori awọn owo-ori / awọn ipo giga ti awọn ẹka, awọn aami abbl.

Ọpọlọpọ awọn ọna miiran lo wa lati ṣe alabapin si Awọn Otitọ Awọn ọja Ṣii, jọwọ darapọ mọ wa lori Slack ki a bẹrẹ ijiroro naa!
