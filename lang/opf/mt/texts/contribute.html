Nistgħu nagħmlu l-informazzjoni dwar il-prodott aktar trasparenti u sostenibbli? Yes we scan!

Everyone can contribute

Kun parti mid-database kollaborattiva, bla ħlas u miftuħa tagħna ta' prodotti minn madwar id-dinja!

Open Products Facts huwa proġett mingħajr skop ta' qligħ magħmul kompletament minn voluntiera, għandna bżonnok.

Add products

Uża l-app Open Food Facts għal Android jew iPhone biex tiskennja faċilment il-barcode tal-prodotti mid-dar tiegħek jew minn ħwienet lokali u ttella’ ritratti tat-tikketti tagħhom.

L-apps mobbli ngħaqdu flimkien, sabiex issa tista' tiskennja prodotti, ikel, kożmetiċi, u ikel għall-annimali domestiċi mill-istess app Open Food Facts!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Installa l-app biex tikkontribwixxi għat-trasparenza tal-prodott

Tell the world

Tħobb il-Fatti dwar Prodotti Miftuħa? Tell others about it!

Tista' tippreżenta l-proġett lill-familja u l-ħbieb tiegħek, turihom kif jinstallaw l-app u tikkontribwixxi, tikteb post fuq blog, taqsam Fatti dwar Prodotti Miftuħa fuq il-midja soċjali...

And present it live! Ippreżentajna Open Products Facts lil udjenzi differenti ħafna f'ambjenti differenti ħafna, u dejjem niksbu rispons entużjastiku ħafna meta nuru x'jista' jsir meta d-dejta għall-prodotti tkun miftuħa. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Nistgħu naħdmu flimkien biex nappoġġjaw u nżidu tikketti ambjentali lokali, punteġġi ta' riparabbiltà, u standards tal-prodott.

Start or join a local contributors community: add local products, recruit friends, present the project in local meetups and conferences etc.

Expert help needed

L-iżvilupp ta' Open Products Facts jeħtieġ ukoll għarfien speċjalizzat u kompetenza f'ħafna oqsma differenti:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    Neħtieġu l-għajnuna biex nibnu esperjenza aħjar għall-utent fuq is-sit web u l-app mobbli ta' Open Products Facts, biex intejbu id-disinn tagħhom, biex noħolqu materjali ta' preżentazzjoni li jħallu impatt eċċ.

  • Development

    Għandna ħafna xogħol ta' żvilupp xi nagħmlu: fuq il-backend tal-Open Products Facts (Perl u MongoDB), l-API (JSON), is-sit web (HTML5, JS, Foundation), u l-app mobbli. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Aktar serjament, hemm ħafna affarijiet interessanti li l-utenti, il-kontributuri u dawk li jużaw mill-ġdid jagħmlu b'Open Products Facts, ikun tajjeb li aktar nies ikunu jafu dwaru. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    Hemm ħafna applikazzjonijiet interessanti u oriġinali tad-dejta miftuħa tal-prodott li nistgħu naħdmu fuqhom ma' aġenziji ambjentali, manifatturi, riċerkaturi, universitajiet, skejjel, NGOs eċċ. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    Tista' tiddiskuti mal-komunità fuq il- forumtagħna. Huwa post tajjeb ħafna biex tistaqsi mistoqsijiet, taqsam ideat, u tikkollabora fuq proġetti.

  • Wiki

    Għandna wkoll wiki li nużaw biex niddokumentaw b'mod kollaborattiv Open Products Facts u s-sottoproġetti tagħha. We also use the wiki to manage translations and to work on the taxonomies / hierarchies of categories, labels etc.

Hemm ħafna aktar modi kif tista' tikkontribwixxi għal Open Products Facts, jekk jogħġbok ingħaqad magħna fuq Slack u ejja nibdew id-diskussjoni!
