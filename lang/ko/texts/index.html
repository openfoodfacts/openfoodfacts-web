기여하기

Open Food Facts는 전 세계 수천 명의 자원 봉사자가 개발한 비영리 프로젝트입니다. You can start contributing by adding a petfood product, and we have lots of exciting projects you can contribute to in many different ways.

You can use it to make better choices about pet food, and as it is open data, anyone can re-use it for any purpose.
