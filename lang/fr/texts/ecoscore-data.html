Collecter les nombreuses données nécessaires au calcul de l'Eco-Score

Packaging codes

There are many traceability codes available on the food packaging. Those usually indicate the plant location which is helpful to compute the distance the product has traveled. For instance, we do a map of all European plants called Made Near Me.

Environmental labels

Using our contributors and our artificial intelligence, we've also been collecting environmental labels for the 8 past years.

Origins of ingredients

We build on the powerful technology that enables us to detect additives, compute the ultra-processing levels or detect allergens.
