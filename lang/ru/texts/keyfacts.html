Open Food Facts - это уникальная в мире пищевая база данных:

  • collaborative : comparable to Wikipedia, it has 60,000 contributors
  • international and no limit to size and products: born in France, it now contains 3,000,000 products in more than 180 countries - 16 countries have more than 1,000 products, 8 more than 8,000
  • freely reusable (open data): dozens of companies, researchers, innovators freely reuse the data for their own use
  • strictly independent of agribusiness, it is run by an association under French law, law of 1901.

With 300,000 users/month, Open Food Facts has already become, in 11 years only, the reference for food information in several countries. Оно поддерживает исследования и государственную политику направленную на здоровую диету.

5 основных историй успеха Open Food Facts?

Создание первой бесплатной глобальной базы данных пищевых продуктов

Более 700,000 продуктов проверенных более чем 16,000 участников. Данные Open Food Facts также публикуются в официальном французском портале data.gouv.fr.

Информирование широкой общественности и специалистов

Потребительское приложение установлено на более чем 750,000 телефонов или планшетов. Сайтом и приложением пользуются 100,000 пользователей в день и 6 миллионов пользователей в год.

Promoting nutritional quality indicators

For 3 years, Open Food Facts has been supporting the roll out of Nutri-Score in 5 European countries. In France, Open Food Facts has been displaying the Nutri-Score since 2015, before its officialization. В Испании и Бельгии Open Food Facts помогает принятию Nutri-Score в этих странах. Open Food Facts also helped bring ultra-processing in the limelight by computing the NOVA classification, and made ecological labelling a reality by computing the Eco-Score at scale.

Creating and maintaining an ecosystem of uses and research

Since the Open Food Facts data is freely reusable, several information sites, start-ups, research laboratories, teachers, etc., have started their activity and / or use the Open Food Facts data daily: Yuka, Foodvisor, Scaneat, Ya Quoi Dedans (What’s inside) (Super U), Buy Or Not, nopalm.org, My Nutritional Info… Since its creation, Open Food Facts has developed a real ecosystem of reuse of its data.

Изменить практику производителей

For 2 years, Open Food Facts has helped to change the practices of the food industry; they know and rely on Open Food Facts to compare and improve their products. Many companies spontaneously communicate the data of their products on the platform: Fleury Michon, Super U, Carrefour, Casino, etc.

Кто использует данные Open Food Facts?

It would be too long to name all the organizations that build on the Open Food Facts platform.

  • More than 100 applications for mobile phones that meet the needs of various audiences: diabetes (VeryDiab), allergies, food balance via artificial intelligence (FoodVisor), disabilities, and more. Наша база данных также способствовала созданию Yuka.
  • Множество сайтов: HowMuchSugar.in, MadeNear.me…
  • Журналисты данных: Le Monde's Decoders, BFMTV
  • Исследователи почти в 100 научных статьях
  • Food banks and solidarity stores: Data For Good project in the continuity of a pilot project initiated by the French Red Cross, integration into the cash management of a small food cooperative.
  • Established companies: Terraillon to bring its connected kitchen scales to life.
  • Школы программирования (Simplon, Open Classrooms) включили Open Food Facts в качестве мотивирующего проекта в свою учебную программу.
