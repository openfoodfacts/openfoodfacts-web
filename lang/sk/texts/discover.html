Databáza potravinárskych výrobkov

Open Food Facts is a database of food products with ingredients, allergens, nutrition facts and all the tidbits of information we can find on product labels.

Made by everyone

Open Food Facts je neziskové združenie dobrovoľníkov.

15000+ contributors like you have added 1 000 000+ products from 150 countries using our Android,iPhone or Windows Phone app or their camera to scan barcodes and upload pictures of products and their labels.

For everyone

Údaje o potravinách sú predmetom verejného záujmu a musia byť voľne dostupné. The complete database is published as open data and can be reused by anyone and for any use. Check-out the cool reuses or make your own!

Urobte si lepší výber potravín

Knowledge is power. Znalosť jedla je sila lepšie jesť!

  • Decode labels

    Potravinárske prísady E-čísla, alergény, kódy obalov... Open Food Facts helps you to make sense of the fine print on products labels.

    Tiež hodnotíme aj nutričnú kvalitu každého produktu.

  • Find products

    Our search form includes dozens of criteria so that you can find products that match your criteria.

  • Compare and change

    Viete, ktoré raňajkové cereálie majú menej tuku a cukru? In 3 clicks you can create an interactive graph to show it. So you can change for healthier cereals, and by comparing products from different brands, you encourage producers to make them better.

  • Explore and discover

    Zaujíma vás jedlo? Explore products by facets like brands, categories, labels, origins of ingredients etc. Facets can be combined to discover new insights, for instance to map the origins of ingredients that bear a fair trade label.

Potrava na premýšľanie, inovácie a vedu

Our data can be reused for education, new products and services, research and more!

Databáza Open Food Facts sú otvorené údaje publikované pod licenciou Open Database License. Anyone can use it for any purpose - commercial or non-commercial - provided the source of the data is attributed and improvements to the database are shared in a similar way.

Science

Údaje Open Food Facts sú používané vedcami v rôznych oblastiach (Výživa, Biológia, Linked Open Data...). And you don't need a PhD to take part in citizen science!

Nutrition grades

The French National Nutrition and Health Program uses Open Food Facts to validate the formula of its nutrition quality score and nutrition grades.
