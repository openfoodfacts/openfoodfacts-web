Sesiapa sahaja boleh memberi sumbangan

Be part of our collaborative, free and open source database of food products from around the world!

Open Food Facts merupakan sebuah project tidak berasaskan keuntungan yang sepenuhnya dibangunkan oleh para sukarelawan, oleh itu kami amat memerlukan penglibatan anda.

Tambah produk

Use our Android, iPhone or Windows app to easily scan the barcode of products from your home or local stores and upload pictures of their label.

Tiada telefon pintar? Tiada masalah: anda masih boleh menggunakan kamera anda untuk menambah produk terus ke dalam tapak sesawang kami.

Produk lengkap

Kami perlu kategorikan produk-produk dan ekstrak senarai ramuan dan fakta nutrisi untuk menganalisis kualiti nutrisinya, supaya kami dapat tentukan adalah produk-produk ini sesuai untuk para vegan dan vegatarian dan banyak lagi!

You can help us complete products by selecting and cropping photos and filling in information.

Bantuan pakar diperlukan

Pembangunan Open Food Facts juga perlukan pengetahuan dan kepakaran khusus dalam pelbagai bidang:

  • Pengurusan projek

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Reka bentuk

    We need help to build a better user experience on the Open Food Facts web site and mobile app, to improve their design, to create impactful presentation materials etc.

    Join our design effort

  • Pembangunan

    Kami masih ada banyak kerja-kerja pembangunan yang perlu dibuat. On the Open Food Facts backend (Perl and MongoDB), API (JSON), web site (templatized HTML5, JS, Foundation), our Flutter mobile app for iOS and Android, but also to build new cool reuses etc. We have SDK projects in many programming languages to ensure anyone can reuse and contribute to Open Food Facts, in any language. Kami juga membangunkan usaha Kecerdasan Buatan untuk memudahkan kerja-kerja sumbangan. Kod kami di Git-Hub. Explore the list of projects on GitHub

  • Pembinaan komuniti

    Kita perlu membina komuniti-komuniti setempat di seluruh negara dan pada masa yang sama dapat menyatukan mereka secara sejagat.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Adakah anda salah seorang daripada mereka?

  • Komunikasi

    Kami tidak pasti apa yang perlu ditulis di sini, anda boleh membantu?

    More seriously, there are lot of cool things that users, contributors and reusers do with Open Food Facts, it would be great to get more people to know about it. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Projek khas

    There are lots of interesting and original applications of food open data that we could work on with government food agencies, food producers, researchers, universities, schools, NGOs etc. Anda ada idea bernas? Jika ada, bantulah kami dengan mengutarakan idea tersebut.

    Join the Open Food Facts chat server

  • Spread the word on social media

    We have many social media accounts and groups for Open Food Facts contributors in many languages. Joining them is a good way to get news about the project and to share announcements to a wider audience.
