Data Quality

Se database ine vanhu vakawanda, mhando yedata inogona kusiyana. Kana uchishandisa Open Food Facts pakutsvaga:

  • Funga nezvekusefa uchishandisa mamakisi ekukwana kana zviratidzo zvemhando yedata
  • Simbisa mapoinzi edata akakosha kana kunyatsojeka kuchikosha
  • Zivai nezvenzvimbo uye nguva dzatinosangana nadzo mumashoko edu
  • Tibate kana uchida rubatsiro nekuongorora mhando yedata pakutsvaga kwako

Batana Nesu
