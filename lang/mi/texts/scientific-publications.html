Ngā Whakaputanga Pūtaiao

Ngā Tūemi Kai Tuwhera i roto i te rangahau: ngā whakaputanga, ngā tohutoro, me ngā rauemi mā ngā kairangahau

Mai i te tau 2012, kua noho a Open Food Facts hei rauemi whai hua mā ngā kairangahau o te ao katoa e whakamahi ana i ā mātou raraunga kai mō ngā rangahau mō te kai totika, te taiao, te hauora tūmatanui, me te maha atu o ngā mara. Kua whakahuatia tā mātou pātengi raraunga tuwhera i roto i ngā rau o ngā tuhinga pūtaiao.

Mō ngā Kairangahau

Kei te whakamahi koe, kei te whakaaro rānei koe ki te whakamahi i ngā Open Food Facts hei wāhanga o tētahi kaupapa rangahau?

Tena koa whakamōhio mai ki a mātou i te contact@openfoodfacts.org

E harikoa ana mātou ki te āwhina me te mahi tahi me ngā tīma rangahau kia kite ai me pēhea e taea ai e mātou te tautoko i tā koutou mahi.

Ngā Wāhanga Rangahau Matua

Ka tautokohia e ā mātou raraunga ngā momo mara rangahau

Kai me te Hauora Tūmatanui

Ka whakamahia e ngā kairangahau tā mātou pātengi raraunga hei ako i te kounga kai, te hanganga o ngā kai, me ngā wawaotanga hauora tūmatanui. He mea nui ngā raraunga o Open Food Facts ki te rangahau me te aromatawai i a Nutri-Score.

Te Painga Taiao

Ka whai hua mai i tā mātou pātengi raraunga whānui o ngā mōhiohio hua, ngā rangahau mō te tapuwae waro, te Eco-Score, me te oranga tonutanga taiao o ngā hua kai.

Te Tukatuka Kai

Ko te rangahau mō ngā kai tino tukatukahia mā te whakamahi i te whakarōpūtanga NOVA, e whakawhirinaki ana ki ā mātou rārangi kai me ngā mōhiohio tukatuka taipitopito.

Pūtaiao Rorohiko me te AI

Ka whakamahia ā mātou huinga raraunga tuwhera me ngā whakaahua e te ako mīhini, te mōhio ki ngā whakaahua, te tukatuka reo tūturu, me ngā kaupapa pūtaiao raraunga.

Whanoke Kaihoko

Ko ngā rangahau e tirotiro ana i ngā kōwhiringa kai, ngā pānga o te tapanga, me te māramatanga i roto i te umanga kai e whakamahi ana i ngā raraunga Open Food Facts.

Kaupapahere Kai

Ka tātarihia e ngā kaihanga kaupapa here me ngā kairangahau ngā ture kai, ngā whakaritenga tapanga, me ngā mahi ahumahi mā te whakamahi i tā mātou pātengi raraunga mārama.

Te Uru atu ki ngā Raraunga

He maha ngā huarahi e tukuna ana e Open Food Facts hei uru atu ki ā mātou raraunga:

  • Ngā kaweake pātengi raraunga: Tikiake i ngā putunga pātengi raraunga katoa i roto i ngā whakatakotoranga rerekē. Tirohia tā mātou whārangi raraunga mō ētahi atu kōrero.
  • API: Uiui i tā mātou pātengi raraunga mā te whakamahi i tā mātou API whānui. Ngā tuhinga API
  • Atanga rapu: Tūhurahia ngā hua i runga i tā mātou paetukutuku me ngā āheinga rapu me te tātari matatau.

Te Whakahua i ngā Mōhiohio Kai Tuwhera

Mena kei te whakamahi koe i ngā Open Food Facts i roto i tāu rangahau, tēnā whakahuahia i roto i ngā kupu e tika ana. Anei tētahi hōputu tohutoro kua tūtohutia:

Tohutoro paetukutuku:

Tohutoro huinga raraunga:

Data Quality

I te mea he pātengi raraunga i kohikohia e te marea, ka rerekē pea te kounga o ngā raraunga. Ina whakamahia ngā Open Food Facts mō ngā rangahau:

  • Whakaarohia te tātari mā te kaute whakaoti, mā te tohu kounga raraunga rānei
  • Whakamanahia ngā tohu raraunga matua mēnā he mea nui te tika
  • Kia tūpato ki ngā whakaaro ā-whenua me ā-wā i roto i tā mātou kapinga raraunga
  • Whakapā mai ki a mātou mēnā e hiahia ana koe ki te āwhina mō te aromatawai kounga raraunga mō tāu rangahau.

Mahi tahi me mātou

E mihi ana mātou ki te mahi tahi me ngā tīma rangahau:

  • Ngā hiahia raraunga: Mēnā e hiahia ana koe ki ngā raraunga motuhake, ki ngā whakapainga rānei ki te hanganga o tā mātou pātengi raraunga, whakamōhio mai ki a mātou
  • Ngā rōpū matawai: Whakaritea ngā kaupapa kohikohi raraunga kua tohua mō tō rohe rangahau, mō tō kāwai hua rānei
  • Te tiritiri i ngā hua: Tohaina ō kitenga ki tō mātou hapori, ā, āwhina ki te whakatairanga i te mātauranga
  • Te whakawhanake i ngā taputapu: Te mahi tahi ki ngā taputapu hei painga mō ngā kairangahau me ngā kaihoko

Whakapā mai ki a mātou i contact@openfoodfacts.org hei matapaki i ngā whai wāhitanga mahi tahi.

Pānga Rangahau

He nui ngā takoha a Open Food Facts ki te rangahau pūtaiao me te hauora tūmatanui:

  • Neke atu i te 600 ngā tuhinga pūtaiao kua whakahua i tā mātou pātengi raraunga
  • Kua whakamahia e ngā tīma rangahau nui tae atu ki a EREN (France), USP (Brazil), me Te Whare Wānanga o Liège (Belgium) ā mātou raraunga.
  • Kua tautokohia e tā mātou pātengi raraunga ngā rangahau i arahi ai ki ngā huringa kaupapa here me ngā taunakitanga hauora tūmatanui.
  • Kua whakahohea e mātou ngā whakatairite puta noa i te whenua me ngā rangahau ā-ao mō te kounga kai me te oranga totika.
