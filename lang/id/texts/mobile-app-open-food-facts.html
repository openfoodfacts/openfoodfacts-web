Instal aplikasi seluler Open Food Facts

Untuk memindai produk - produk makanan, dan mengetahui Nutri-Score, Eco-Score, tingkat pemrosesan makanannya lewat grup NOVA (Nova 4 untuk makanan olahan ultra), dan untuk mengartikan zat - zat aditif makanan, instal aplikasi Open Food Facts secara gratis!



Anda juga dapat menambahkan produk - produk baru ke Open Food Facts dan membangun kebaikan bersama untuk meningkatkan nutrisi dan kesehatan bagi semua orang. Terima kasih!

Lihat fitur terbaru

June 2022: Completely redesigned app for Android and iPhone: more personalized, more pedagogical and more intuitive

January 2021: The apps get the Eco-Score, initially in France (announcement in French)

September 2020: Dark mode, artificial intelligence, vegetarian, vegan and palm oil detection, and offline contributions (announcement in French)

June 2019: New Open Food Facts mobile apps for iPhone and Android
New features: continuous scan, offline mode, product comparison, lists, unlimited history and more !

2012: Launch of the Open Food Facts mobile app, the very first collaborative scanning app for food.
