Nyansahu mu Nhoma ahorow

Open Food Facts wɔ nhwehwɛmu mu: nhoma ahorow, nsɛm a wɔafa aka, ne nneɛma a wɔde ma nhwehwɛmufo

Efi afe 2012 no, Open Food Facts abɛyɛ ade a ɛsom bo ma nhwehwɛmufo a wɔwɔ wiase nyinaa a wɔde yɛn aduan ho nsɛm di dwuma de yɛ nhwehwɛmu a ɛfa aduannuru, nneɛma a atwa yɛn ho ahyia, ɔmanfo akwahosan, ne nneɛma afoforo pii ho. Wɔatwe adwene asi yɛn database a wɔabue so no ho asɛm wɔ nyansahu nkrataa ɔhaha pii mu.

Aduandi & Ɔmanfo Akwahosan

Nhwehwɛmufo de yɛn database no di dwuma de sua aduannuru pa, aduan a ɛwom, ne ɔmanfo akwahosan ho nneɛma a wɔde wɔn ho hyɛ mu ho ade. Open Food Facts data aboa kɛse wɔ Nutri-Score nhwehwɛmu ne nhwehwɛmu mu.

Nkɛntɛnso a Ɛwɔ Nneɛma a Atwa Yɛn Ho Ahyia So

Nhwehwɛmu a wɔyɛ wɔ carbon footprint, Eco-Score, ne nneɛma a atwa yɛn ho ahyia a ɛbɛkɔ so atra hɔ daa a ɛfa nnuan ho no nya mfaso fi yɛn nneɛma a wɔyɛ ho nsɛm a yɛde asie a ɛkɔ akyiri no mu.

Aduan a Wɔyɛ

Nhwehwɛmu a wɔyɛ wɔ nnuan a wɔayɛ no yiye a wɔde NOVA nkyekyɛmu di dwuma ho no gyina yɛn nneɛma a wɔde yɛ aduan a wɔahyehyɛ no kɔ akyiri ne nsɛm a ɛfa sɛnea wɔyɛ ho no so.

Kɔmputa Nyansahu & AI

Mfiri adesua, mfonini a wohu, abɔde mu kasa ho dwumadie, ne data nyansahu nnwuma de yɛn dataset ne mfonini a wɔabue ano no di dwuma.

Adetɔfo Suban

Nhwehwɛmu ahorow a ɛhwehwɛ nnuan a wɔpaw, nkɛntɛnso a ɛwɔ nkyerɛwde so, ne nea ɛda adi pefee wɔ aduan adwumayɛ mu no de Open Food Facts data di dwuma.

Aduan Ho Nhyehyɛe

Wɔn a wɔyɛ nhyehyɛe ne nhwehwɛmufo de yɛn databea a ɛda adi pefee no hwehwɛ nnuan ho mmara, ahwehwɛde ahorow a ɛfa nneɛma a wɔde kyerɛw nneɛma ho, ne nnwumayɛbea nneyɛe mu.

Nneɛma a Wɔde Ma Nhwehwɛmufo

Sɛnea wɔde Open Food Facts bedi dwuma na wɔatwe adwene asi so

Data no a Wobɛkɔ

Open Food Facts ma akwan pii a yɛbɛfa so anya yɛn data:

  • Database exports: Twe database dumps a edi mũ wɔ format ahorow mu. Hwɛ yɛn Data krataafa ma nsɛm no mu nsɛm.
  • API: Bisa yɛn database no wɔ nhyehyɛe kwan so denam yɛn API a edi mũ no so. API Nwoma
  • Hwehwɛ interface: Hwehwɛ nneɛma mu tẽẽ wɔ yɛn wɛbsaet no so a wowɔ hwehwɛ ne filtering tumi a ɛkɔ akyiri.

Aduan Ho Nokwasɛm Ahorow a Wɔabue Mu a Wɔfa Ka

Sɛ wode Open Food Facts di dwuma wɔ wo nhwehwɛmu no mu a, yɛsrɛ sɛ fa asɛm ka sɛnea ɛfata. Sɛnea wɔahyɛ ho nyansa sɛ wɔmfa nsɛm nhyɛ mu ni:

Wɛbsaet no so nsɛm a wɔafa aka:

Aduan Ho Nokwasɛm a Wɔabue Mu. (Afe). Open Aduan Ho Nokwasɛm - Wiase. Wɔfaa fii https://world.openfoodfacts.org


Dataset a wɔafa aka:

Aduan Ho Nokwasɛm a Wɔabue Mu. (Afe). Bue Aduan Ho Nokwasɛm Database [Data set]. Wɔfaa fii https://world.openfoodfacts.org/data

Tumi krataa a wɔde ma

Yɛn data no abue na wotumi de di dwuma bio kwa:

  • Database no wɔ Open Database License (ODbL)ase
  • Database mu nsɛm wɔ Database mu nsɛm Tumi krataa (DbCL)ase
  • Aguadeɛ mfonini wɔ Creative Commons Attribution ShareAlike (CC BY-SA 3.0)ase

Hwɛ yɛn Mmara ne nhyehyɛe a yɛde di dwuma na yɛsan de di dwuma ma nsɛm a edi mũ.

Data Quality

Sɛ́ database a nnipadɔm de di dwuma no, data no su betumi ayɛ soronko. Sɛ wode Open Food Facts redi dwuma de ayɛ nhwehwɛmu a:

  • Susuw ho sɛ wobɛfa nkontabuo a ɛdi mũ anaa data su ho nsɛnkyerɛnneɛ so ayɛ yie
  • Di data nsɛntitiriw a ɛho hia no mu den sɛ pɛpɛɛpɛyɛ ho hia a
  • Hu asase ne bere mu animhwɛ wɔ yɛn data a yɛde di dwuma no mu
  • Sɛ wo hia mmoa wɔ data quality assessment ma wo nhwehwɛmu no a, di yɛn ho nkɔmmɔ

Wo ne Yɛn nyɛ adwuma

Yɛma yɛn ani gye sɛ yɛne nhwehwɛmu akuw bɛyɛ biako:

  • Data hia: Sɛ wo hia data pɔtee anaa nkɔsoɔ wɔ yɛn database nhyehyɛɛ mu a, ma yɛnhu
  • Scan parties: Hyehyɛ data a wɔboaboa ano ɔsatuo a wɔde asi w’ani so ma wo nhwehwɛmu mantam anaa afiri kuo
  • Nea efi mu ba a wɔkyɛ: Fa nea woahu no kyerɛ yɛn mpɔtam hɔfo na boa ma nimdeɛ kɔ anim
  • Nnwinnadeɛ nkɔsoɔ: Yɛ adwuma bom wɔ nnwinnadeɛ a ɛboa nhwehwɛmufoɔ ne adetɔfoɔ nyinaa

Di yɛn ho nkɔmmɔ wɔ contact@openfoodfacts.org na susuw adwumayɛ hokwan ahorow ho.

Nhwehwɛmu Nkɛntɛnso

Open Food Facts aboa kɛse wɔ nyansahu mu nhwehwɛmu ne ɔmanfo akwahosan mu:

  • Nyansahu ho nhoma bɛboro 600 atwe adwene asi yɛn database no so
  • Nhwehwɛmu akuw akɛse a EREN (France), USP (Brazil), ne Liège Sukuupɔn (Belgium) ka ho de yɛn nsɛm no adi dwuma
  • Yɛn database no aboa nhwehwɛmu a ɛde nhyehyɛe mu nsakrae ne ɔmanfo akwahosan ho nyansahyɛ ahorow ba
  • Yɛama aman ntam ntotoho ne wiase nyinaa nhwehwɛmu a ɛfa aduan pa ne aduannuru ho atumi ayɛ yiye
