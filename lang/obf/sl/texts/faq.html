How can I contact the Open Beauty Facts team, ask questions or make suggestions?

You can ask questions or make suggestions on the Idea Forum or contact us by e-mail: contact@openbeautyfacts.org

Ali so informacije in podatki o izdelkih preverjeni?

The information and data is submitted by the Open Beauty Facts contributors. The contributors also send pictures of the product, its labels, ingredients lists and health and beauty claims. Če ste v dvomih, lahko obiskovalci sami preverte točnost podatkov in če pride do napake, jo lahko popravite na kraju samem.

Da bi lažje zaznali morebitne napake, bomo postopoma dodali samodejne preglede. e.g. if the ingredients of a product are very different from products of the same category, it may be an error.

Ali lahko dodam slike ali podatke o izdelku s strani proizvajalca, nakupovalnih mest ali drugih spletnih mest?

Almost all other sites forbid reproduction and reuse of their data and images, and that's actually the reason why we are creating Open Beauty Facts: to make all this data available to all and for all uses.

Da bi se izognili kakršnim koli pravnim problemom, prosimo sodelavce, da dodajo samo slike, ki so jih sami posneli, in samo podatke, ki prihajajo iz embalaže in etikete izdelka.

I am a cosmetics product manufacturer, can I add my own products?

Da! Edini pogoj je sprejeti dejstvo, da so podatki in slike na voljo v okviru odprte licence. (see the Terms of contribution)

Če imate veliko število izdelkov, ki jih želite dodati (več kot 50), lahko poskusimo najti način za samodejno dodajanje izdelkov in njihovih podatkov. Contact-us to discuss how we could proceed: contact@openbeautyfacts.org

What is the difference with other web sites, services and mobile applications that already allow to view cosmetics products information?

Glavna razlika je za nas kritičnega pomena: naši podatki so prosto dostopni vsem in za vse vrste uporabe. To je tisto, kar se imenuje odprti podatki.

Skoraj vse druge strani, storitve in aplikacije drugim prepovedujejo reproduciranje in ponovno uporabo svojih podatkov. Ravno nasprotno, ljubosumno ga hranijo zase. In almost all cases, their terms of service explicitly forbid any non-personal use and any extraction of all the data or parts of the data.

We consider that cosmetics products information is too important and useful to keep it locked in a safe. So we decided to do exactly the opposite: not only we allow use and reuse of our database, freely and without fee, to everyone and for all uses (including commercial), but we also encourage it!

Omogočiti javno dostopnost podatkov (kar je znano kot odprti podatki) omogoča posameznikom, združenjem, podjetjem, raziskovalcem itd. from all around the world to think up and develop applications for the data that we certainly would never have thought about.

What is Open Beauty facts’ economical model

Sodulejoči so prostovoljci. Njihovi prispevki so zbrani v odprti bazi podatkov, ki jo lahko uporabljajo vsi in za vse namene. (see the Terms of reuse)

Everyone (including but not limited to Open Beauty Facts contributors and creator) can thus redistribute and/or reuse the data to build web sites, services, software, mobile applications, or to write articles and studies. So svobodni pri zagotavljanju, da je nastalo delo prosto dostopno, ali da ga prodajajo ali ovrednotijo (npr. Z oglasi), če spoštujejo pogoje ponovne uporabe.
