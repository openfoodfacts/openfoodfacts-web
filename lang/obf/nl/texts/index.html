Ontdek

Open Beauty Facts is a cosmetics products database made by everyone, for everyone. You can use it to make better personal care choices, and as it is open data, anyone can re-use it for any purpose.

Bijdragen

Open Beauty Facts is een non-profitproject ontwikkeld door duizenden vrijwilligers uit de hele wereld. You can start contributing by adding a product from your bathroom with our app for iPhone or Android, and we have lots of exciting projects you can contribute to in many different ways.
