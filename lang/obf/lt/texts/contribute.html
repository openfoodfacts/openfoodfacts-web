Contribute to Open Beauty Facts

All you need is a scan-do attitude! :-)

Can we make the cosmetics industry more open and transparent? Yes we scan! In barely a week, already 30 Open Beauty Facts contributors and growing !

Kiekvienas gali prisidėti

Be part of our collaborative, free and open database of cosmetic products from around the world!

Open Beauty Facts is a non-profit project made entirely by volunteers, we do need you.

Pridėk produktus

Use the Open Food Facts app for Android or iPhone to easily scan the barcode of cosmetic products from your home or local stores and upload pictures of their label.

The mobile apps have been merged, so you can now scan cosmetics, pet food, and other products from the same Open Food Facts app!

Neturi išmaniojo? Ne problema: gali naudotis kamera, kad pridėtum produktus tiesiogiai per interneto svetainę.

Pranešk pasauliui

Do you like Open Beauty Facts? Pranešk kitiems apie tai!

You can present the project to your family and friends, show them how to install the app and contribute, write a blog post, share Open Beauty Facts on social media...

And present it live! We have presented Open Beauty Facts to very different audiences in very different settings, and we always get a very enthusiastic response when we show what can be done when the data for products is open. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Lokalizacija

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

We can work together to support and add logos for local labels, to decode local packager codes so that we can map products etc.

Start or join a local contributors community: add local products, recruit friends, present the project in local meetups and conferences etc.

Reikalinga specialistų pagalba

Developing Open Beauty Facts also requires specialized knowledge and expertise in many different areas:

  • Projektų valdymas

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Dizainas

    We need help to build a better user experience on the Open Beauty Facts web site and mobile app, to improve their design, to create impactful presentation materials etc.

  • Programavimas

    We have a lot of development work to do: on the Open Beauty Facts backend (Perl and MongoDB), API (JSON), web site (HTML5, JS, Foundation), iOS and Android apps (currently Apache Cordova / Phonegap, with a native Android version almost ready and much work to do on iOS), to build new cool reuses etc. Our code is on GitHub (Android, iOS, Cordova.)

  • Vietinės bendruomenės

    Turime kurti vietines bendruomenes įvairiose šalyse ir kartu jas vienyti globaliai.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Ar tu vienas iš jų?

  • Susisiekimas

    Nežinome, ką čia rašyti, ar galėtumėte padėti?

    More seriously, there are lot of cool things that users, contributors and reusers do with Open Beauty Facts, it would be great to get more people to know about it. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Specialūs projektai

    There are lots of interesting and original applications of cosmetic open data that we could work on with government cosmetic agencies, cosmetic producers, researchers, universities, schools, NGOs etc. Galbūt jau turi idėjų? Jei taip, padėkite mums jas pastūmėti į priekį.

Pasikalbėkime!

Mūsų savanorių bendruomenė turi daug idėjų, energijos ir entuziazmo, prisijunkite prie mūsų!

Štai keletas vietų, kur galite susitikti su kitais savanoriais, pasikalbėti ir dirbti su jais:

  • Slack

    „Slack“ yra geriausias būdas bendrauti ir bendradarbiauti su kitais bendraautoriais, kūrėjais ir pakartotiniais naudotojais. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. Tai visiškai pakeitė tai, kaip dirbame kartu. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Facebook grupės

    We have Facebook groups in many languages for Open Beauty Facts contributors. Joining them is a good way to get news about the project and to share announcements to a wider audience.

  • Wiki

    We also have a wiki that we use to collaboratively document Open Beauty Facts and its sub-projects. We also use the wiki to manage translations and to work on the taxonomies / hierarchies of categories, labels etc.

There are many more ways to contribute to Open Beauty Facts, please join us on Slack (in the #openbeautyfacts channel) and let's start the discussion!
