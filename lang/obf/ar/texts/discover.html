A cosmetics products database

Open Beauty Facts is a database of cosmetics products with ingredients, allergens, indicia and all the tidbits of information we can find on product labels.

Made by everyone

Open Beauty Facts is a non-profit association of volunteers.

1800+ contributors like you have added 13,000+ products from 134 countries using our Android or iPhone app or their camera to scan barcodes and upload pictures of products and their labels.

For everyone

Data about cosmetics is of public interest and has to be open. The complete database is published as open data and can be reused by anyone and for any use. Check-out the cool reuses or make your own!

Make better cosmetics choices

Knowledge is power. Cosmetics knowledge is power to pamper yourself better!

  • Decode labels

    Cosmetics additives, F. I. L. codes, CI-numbers, allergens, packaging codes... Open Beauty Facts helps you to make sense of the fine print on products labels.

  • Find products

    لدينا نموذج بحث يحتوي على العشرات من المعايير التي تمكنك من تخصيص البحث ومقارنة المنتجات.

  • Compare and change

    Do you know which shampoos have the less chemicals? In 3 clicks you can create an interactive graph to show it. So you can change for healthier shampoos, and by comparing products from different brands, you encourage producers to make them better.

  • Explore and discover

    Curious about cosmetics? Explore products by facets like brands, categories, labels, origins of ingredients etc. Facets can be combined to discover new insights, for instance to map the origins of ingredients that bear a fair trade label.
