Bemærk: Open Beauty Facts er under opbygning.

Det er baseret på Open Food Facts og lavet af samme team. Vi er i færd med at foretage ændringer for at tage hensyn til de specifikke aspekter indenfor kosmetik. Deltag på vores Slack i #openbeautyfacts-kanalen for at diskutere og kreere Open Beauty Facts!

Opdag

Open Beauty Facts is a cosmetics products database made by everyone, for everyone. You can use it to make better personal care choices, and as it is open data, anyone can re-use it for any purpose.

Bidrag

Open Beauty Facts er et nonprofitprojekt, der er udviklet af tusindvis af frivillige fra hele verden. You can start contributing by adding a product from your bathroom with our app for iPhone or Android, and we have lots of exciting projects you can contribute to in many different ways.
