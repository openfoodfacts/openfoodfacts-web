• What it is: Tartrazine, a synthetic yellow azo dye. Also known as FD&C Yellow 5 or E102.
  • Why it's used: Provides yellow color to cosmetic and personal care products.
  • Concerns:
  • Can cause allergic reactions in some individuals, particularly those with aspirin sensitivity.
  • May cause skin irritation or hives in sensitive people.
  • Approved for use in cosmetics in the EU and US at specified concentrations.
  • Some concerns about hyperactivity in children when used in food (not directly applicable to cosmetics).
  • Sources: FDA, European Commission
