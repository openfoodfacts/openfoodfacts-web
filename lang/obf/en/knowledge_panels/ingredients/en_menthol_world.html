• What it is: A cyclic terpene alcohol obtained from peppermint or other mint oils, or produced synthetically. Provides a cooling sensation.
  • Why it's used: Creates a cooling, refreshing sensation on skin. Used in oral care, pain relief products, and skincare for its soothing properties.
  • Concerns:
  • Generally safe when used at appropriate concentrations.
  • Can cause skin irritation at high concentrations.
  • May trigger allergic reactions in sensitive individuals.
  • Should be used with caution on broken or damaged skin.
