• What it is: An ester of ethylene glycol and stearic acid. Appears as pearlescent flakes.
  • Why it's used: Adds pearlescent or opaque appearance to shampoos and body washes. Also acts as an emulsifier and thickening agent.
  • Concerns:
  • Generally considered safe for cosmetic use.
  • Non-irritating and non-sensitizing for most people.
  • Well-tolerated in rinse-off products.
