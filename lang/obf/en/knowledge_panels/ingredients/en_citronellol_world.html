• What it is: A naturally occurring fragrance component found in rose oil, geranium oil, and citronella oil. Has a fresh, rose-like scent.
  • Why it's used: Used as a fragrance ingredient to provide floral notes in perfumes and scented products.
  • Concerns:
  • One of the 26 allergens that must be listed separately in EU cosmetics if present above certain thresholds.
  • Can cause skin sensitization and allergic reactions in susceptible individuals.
  • May oxidize over time, becoming more allergenic.
  • Sources: European Commission, Contact Dermatitis Journal
