• What it is: A glycol derived from corn sugar (bio-based) or petroleum. An alternative to propylene glycol.
  • Why it's used: Acts as a humectant, solvent, and viscosity modifier. Helps ingredients penetrate skin and improves product feel.
  • Concerns:
  • Generally safe for cosmetic use.
  • Better tolerated than propylene glycol for sensitive skin.
  • Bio-based versions available for eco-conscious formulations.
  • Low risk of irritation.
