• What it is: A quaternary ammonium compound derived from guar gum. A cationic conditioning polymer.
  • Why it's used: Provides conditioning benefits to hair and skin. Reduces static, improves combability, and enhances product texture.
  • Concerns:
  • Generally considered safe for cosmetic use.
  • Well-tolerated in hair care and skin care products.
  • Derived from natural guar gum but chemically modified.
  • Non-irritating at typical use concentrations.
  • Sources: Cosmetic Ingredient Review
