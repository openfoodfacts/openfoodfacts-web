• What it is: Extract from the aloe vera plant (Aloe barbadensis). Contains polysaccharides, vitamins, and minerals.
  • Why it's used: Provides soothing, anti-inflammatory, and moisturizing properties. Popular in after-sun and sensitive skin products.
  • Concerns:
  • Generally safe and well-tolerated.
  • Rarely may cause allergic reactions.
  • Quality and concentration vary widely between products.
  • Look for products with high aloe content for best results.
