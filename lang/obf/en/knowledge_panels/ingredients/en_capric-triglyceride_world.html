• What it is: A mixed triester of glycerin and caprylic/capric fatty acids, typically derived from coconut oil.
  • Why it's used: Acts as an emollient, providing smooth skin feel. Helps dissolve other ingredients and improves product spreadability.
  • Concerns:
  • Generally recognized as safe for cosmetic use.
  • Non-comedogenic (doesn't clog pores).
  • Well-tolerated by most skin types.
  • Lightweight and easily absorbed.
