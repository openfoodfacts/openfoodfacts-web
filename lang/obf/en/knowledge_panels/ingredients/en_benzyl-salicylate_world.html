• What it is: A synthetic ester of salicylic acid and benzyl alcohol. Has a faint balsamic, slightly floral scent.
  • Why it's used: Used as a fragrance ingredient and UV absorber in cosmetic products.
  • Concerns:
  • One of the 26 allergens that must be listed separately in EU cosmetics if present above certain thresholds.
  • Can cause allergic contact dermatitis in sensitive individuals.
  • May act as a weak endocrine disruptor according to some studies.
  • Sources: European Commission, Contact Dermatitis Journal
