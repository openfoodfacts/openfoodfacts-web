• What it is: A synthetic alcohol derived from caprylic acid. Acts as both a skin conditioning agent and preservative booster.
  • Why it's used: Provides moisturizing benefits, enhances skin feel, and boosts the effectiveness of other preservatives in formulations.
  • Concerns:
  • Generally considered safe for cosmetic use.
  • Well-tolerated by most skin types.
  • Low risk of irritation or sensitization.
