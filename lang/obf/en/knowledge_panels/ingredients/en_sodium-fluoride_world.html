• What it is: An inorganic chemical compound. The sodium salt of fluoride, commonly used in oral care products.
  • Why it's used: Primary active ingredient in toothpaste for preventing tooth decay. Helps remineralize tooth enamel and prevent cavities.
  • Concerns:
  • Safe and effective when used as directed in oral care products.
  • Should not be swallowed; rinse-off products only.
  • Excessive fluoride ingestion can lead to dental fluorosis in children.
  • Keep out of reach of children.
  • Sources: FDA, ADA
