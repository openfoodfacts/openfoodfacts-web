• What it is: An aromatic alcohol that occurs naturally in some fruits and teas. Can also be synthetically produced.
  • Why it's used: Acts as a preservative, solvent, and fragrance ingredient. Helps prevent microbial growth in cosmetic products.
  • Concerns:
  • Generally safe at concentrations used in cosmetics (typically <1%).
  • Can cause mild irritation in sensitive individuals.
  • May trigger allergic reactions in some people.
  • Considered safer than many traditional preservatives like parabens.
