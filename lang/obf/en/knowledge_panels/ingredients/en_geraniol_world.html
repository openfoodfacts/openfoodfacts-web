• What it is: A naturally occurring alcohol found in many essential oils, particularly rose and geranium oils. Has a sweet, floral scent.
  • Why it's used: Used as a fragrance ingredient and flavoring agent. Also has antimicrobial properties.
  • Concerns:
  • One of the 26 allergens that must be listed separately in EU cosmetics if present above certain thresholds.
  • Can cause allergic reactions and skin sensitization.
  • May oxidize when exposed to air, becoming more allergenic.
  • Sources: European Commission, Contact Dermatitis Journal
