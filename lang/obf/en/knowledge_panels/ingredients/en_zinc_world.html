• What it is: An essential mineral used in various forms in cosmetics (zinc oxide, zinc sulfate, etc.). Important for skin health.
  • Why it's used: Provides UV protection (as zinc oxide), helps with healing, has antimicrobial properties, and supports skin barrier function.
  • Concerns:
  • Generally safe for topical use.
  • Zinc oxide is reef-safe in appropriate formulations.
  • Nanoparticle forms of zinc oxide have some debate about safety.
  • Excessive zinc can interfere with copper absorption when ingested.
  • Sources: FDA, Cosmetic Ingredient Review
