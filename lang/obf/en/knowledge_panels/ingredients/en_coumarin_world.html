• What it is: A naturally occurring fragrance compound found in tonka beans, lavender, and cinnamon. Has a sweet, vanilla-like scent.
  • Why it's used: Used as a fragrance ingredient to provide sweet, warm notes in perfumes and scented products.
  • Concerns:
  • One of the 26 allergens that must be listed separately in EU cosmetics if present above certain thresholds.
  • Can cause allergic contact dermatitis and photosensitivity.
  • Restricted in cosmetics in the EU; maximum concentration allowed is 0.1% in leave-on products.
  • Sources: European Commission, Contact Dermatitis Journal
