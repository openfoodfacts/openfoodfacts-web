• What it is: A synthetic fragrance ingredient with a floral, violet-like scent.
  • Why it's used: Used as a fragrance component in perfumes and scented cosmetic products.
  • Concerns:
  • One of the 26 allergens that must be listed separately in EU cosmetics if present above certain thresholds.
  • Can cause allergic contact dermatitis and skin sensitization.
  • Relatively common cause of fragrance allergy.
  • Sources: European Commission, Contact Dermatitis Journal
