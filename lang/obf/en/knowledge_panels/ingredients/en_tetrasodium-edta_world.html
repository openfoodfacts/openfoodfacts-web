• What it is: A synthetic chelating agent that binds to metal ions. The tetrasodium salt of ethylenediaminetetraacetic acid.
  • Why it's used: Binds metal ions to improve product stability, enhance preservative effectiveness, and prevent product degradation.
  • Concerns:
  • Generally considered safe in cosmetics at typical concentrations (usually <0.5%).
  • Does not penetrate intact skin well.
  • Environmental concerns as it's not readily biodegradable.
  • Can mobilize heavy metals in wastewater treatment.
  • Sources: Cosmetic Ingredient Review
