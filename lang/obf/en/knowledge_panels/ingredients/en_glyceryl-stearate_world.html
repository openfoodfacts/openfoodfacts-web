• What it is: An ester of glycerin and stearic acid. Can be plant-derived or synthetic.
  • Why it's used: Acts as an emulsifier to blend oil and water phases, and provides a soft, smooth texture to creams and lotions.
  • Concerns:
  • Generally recognized as safe for cosmetic use.
  • Non-irritating and non-sensitizing for most people.
  • Well-tolerated by all skin types.
