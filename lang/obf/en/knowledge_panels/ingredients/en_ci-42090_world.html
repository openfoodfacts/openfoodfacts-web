• What it is: Brilliant Blue FCF, a synthetic blue dye. Also known as FD&C Blue 1 or E133.
  • Why it's used: Provides blue color to cosmetic products and can be combined with other dyes to create various shades.
  • Concerns:
  • Generally considered safe for cosmetic use.
  • Can cause allergic reactions in rare cases.
  • Approved for use in cosmetics and food in most countries.
  • May stain skin temporarily.
  • Sources: FDA, European Commission
