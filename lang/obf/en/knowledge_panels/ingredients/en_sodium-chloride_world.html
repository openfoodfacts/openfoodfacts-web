• What it is: Common table salt (NaCl). A mineral compound used in cosmetics for various purposes.
  • Why it's used: Acts as a viscosity adjuster, helps thicken formulas, provides scrubbing action in exfoliants, and can enhance product preservation.
  • Concerns:
  • Generally recognized as safe for cosmetic use.
  • Can be drying at high concentrations, especially for dry or sensitive skin.
  • May cause irritation if used in high concentrations in leave-on products.
  • Sources: Cosmetic Ingredient Review
