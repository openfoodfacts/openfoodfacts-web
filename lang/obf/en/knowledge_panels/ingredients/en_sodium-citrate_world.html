• What it is: The sodium salt of citric acid. A buffering agent and chelating agent.
  • Why it's used: Adjusts and maintains pH levels, acts as a preservative, and chelates metal ions to improve product stability.
  • Concerns:
  • Generally recognized as safe for cosmetic and food use.
  • Non-irritating and non-sensitizing.
  • Well-tolerated by all skin types.
  • Sources: Cosmetic Ingredient Review, FDA
