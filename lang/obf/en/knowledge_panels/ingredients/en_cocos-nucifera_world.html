• What it is: Coconut-derived ingredient. Can refer to coconut oil, coconut water, or other coconut extracts depending on the specific form listed.
  • Why it's used: Provides moisturizing, emollient properties. Rich in fatty acids that nourish skin and hair.
  • Concerns:
  • Generally safe for cosmetic use.
  • Can be comedogenic (pore-clogging) for some individuals, particularly in facial products.
  • May cause allergic reactions in people with coconut allergies (rare).
  • Sustainability varies by source; look for sustainably sourced options.
  • Sources: Cosmetic Ingredient Review
