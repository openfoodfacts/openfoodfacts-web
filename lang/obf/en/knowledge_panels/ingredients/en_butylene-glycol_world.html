• What it is: A small organic alcohol used as a humectant and solvent. Derived from petroleum or can be plant-based.
  • Why it's used: Acts as a humectant to retain moisture, helps ingredients penetrate skin, and improves product texture and spreadability.
  • Concerns:
  • Generally safe for cosmetic use.
  • Well-tolerated by most skin types.
  • Lower risk of irritation compared to propylene glycol.
  • May cause mild irritation in very sensitive skin.
