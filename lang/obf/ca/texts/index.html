Nota: Open Beauty Facts està en construcció.

Basat en Open Food Facts i creat pel mateix equip. Estem en procés de fer canvis per tenir en compte els aspectes específics de la cosmètica. Uneix-te a nosaltres al nostre Slack, al canal #openbeautyfacts per discutir i inventar Open Beauty Facts!

Descobrir

Open Beauty Facts is a cosmetics products database made by everyone, for everyone. You can use it to make better personal care choices, and as it is open data, anyone can re-use it for any purpose.

Contribuir

Open Beauty Facts és un projecte sense ànim de lucre desenvolupat per milers de voluntaris d'arreu del món. You can start contributing by adding a product from your bathroom with our app for iPhone or Android, and we have lots of exciting projects you can contribute to in many different ways.
