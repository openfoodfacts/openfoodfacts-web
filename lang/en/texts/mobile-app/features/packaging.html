Discover and weigh in on the impact of packaging Discover the impact of packaging on the planet; although it represents only a small part of the environmental impact of food, packaging is – together with a more plant-based diet – one of the two major levers on which we can all act. The new app offers a comprehensive deciphering of the topic.
If you have a precision kitchen scale, you can also join the effort to weigh the amount of plastic packaging in your favorite foods !
