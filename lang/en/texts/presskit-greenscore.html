Green-Score: Environmental Impact Information for All Products

Our goal, mirroring the success of Nutri-Score, is not just mobile scanning, but transforming the food industry by making environmental impact information transparent and directly accessible on shelves and online.

The Green-Score (formerly Eco-Score) is an experimental A+ to F rating indicating the environmental impact of food products. Based on Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) data covering factors like greenhouse gas emissions, resource depletion, and pollution, it empowers consumers to make more sustainable choices and encourages producers to adopt greener practices.

Another Pioneering Step by Open Food Facts

From launching the first food scanning app in 2013 and championing Nutri-Score, to analyzing ultra-processing with NOVA in 2018, Open Food Facts now pioneers large-scale environmental scoring with the Green-Score, starting in 2021 (as Eco-Score).

Open Food Facts continues its mission of food transparency by integrating the Green-Score, developed collaboratively and reviewed by experts. This initiative aims to help reduce the environmental footprint of our food consumption and contribute to global sustainability goals.

Green-Score Now Live in the Open Food Facts App

Available today for Android and iOS across hundreds of thousands of products. Free, no registration required, and respects your privacy.

Scan products or search the database to instantly see their Green-Score alongside nutritional information, helping you make informed decisions right in the supermarket aisle or at home.

Explore Green-Score on the Open Food Facts Website

Compare, sort, and filter products by Green-Score and other criteria, without compromising your privacy.
