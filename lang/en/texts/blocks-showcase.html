Meet the Eco Score

The Eco Score is an environmental score from A to E which makes it easy to compare the impact of food products on the environment.

Why Eco Score matters ?

Food system activities, including production, transport & packaging contribute to climate change. The Eco Score reflects the global impact of Food to guide you towards more responsible consumption. 

Start scanning products now

Start scanning a Barcode with the Open Food Facts App to instantly get the Eco Score of your food and limit your impact on the planet.
A new version of the app is coming soon. Stay tuned ! 

Beyond Eco Score

Open Food Facts will give you comprehensive information on more than 2 million food products such as Nutri Score, food processing (NOVA) and vegan options. You can customize the app easily to fit your profile.
