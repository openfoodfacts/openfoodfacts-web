Obtaining the carbon impact from ADEME data

Based on the product category, we start by looking for the closest category in the agribalyse database. We mostly made exact matches and in some cases extended the match  when the products have an extremely similar ecological profile. The complete list of associations is available at this address.


Agribalyse official website

This recovers a carbon impact for the product. This carbon impact counts for X percent of the Eco-Score. 

The rating then works with a system of positive or negative points on various factors related to the environment.
