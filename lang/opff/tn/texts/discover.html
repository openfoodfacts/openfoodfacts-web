Bona tiriswa go simolola go sekaseka dijo tsa diruiwa tsa mo gae

For everyone

Tshedimosetso ka ga dijo tsa diruiwa tsa mo gae ke ya kgatlhego ya setšhaba mme e tshwanetse go bulega. The complete database is published as open data and can be reused by anyone and for any use. Check-out the cool reuses or make your own!

Dira ditlhopho tse di botoka tsa dijo tsa diruiwa tsa mo gae

Knowledge is power. Kitso ya dijo tsa diruiwa tsa mo gae ke maatla a go fepa diruiwa tsa gago tsa mo gae botoka!

  • Decode labels

    Metswako ya dijo tsa diruiwa tsa mo gae, ditlaleletsi, boleng jwa dikotla... Open Pet Food Facts e go thusa go tlhaloganya mokwalo o mosesane o o mo dileibole tsa dikumo.

  • Find products

    Foromo ya rona ya patlo ya e akaretsa ditlhokego di le dintsintsi gore o kgone go bona dikumo tsa dijo tsa diruiwa tse di tsamaelanang le ditlhoko tsa diruiwa tsa gago.

  • Bapisa mme o tlhophe

    A o itse gore ke dijo dife tsa dintša tse di nang le poroteine e ntsi? Ke dijo dife tsa dikatse tse di senang dithoro? O ka bapisa dihlahiswa ho tswa ho matshwaokgwebo a fapaneng mme wa etsa diqeto tse nang le tsebo bakeng sa bophelo bo botle ba diruuwa tsa hao tsa lapeng.

  • Explore and discover

    A o batla go itse ka dijo tsa diruiwa tsa mo gae? Batlisisa dikumo ka dikarolo tse di tshwanang le matshwaokgwebo, ditlhopha, dileibole, mefuta (dintša, dikatse, jalo le jalo), le metswako. Ribolola gore tota ke eng se se mo dijong tsa phologotswana ya gago.

Dijo tsa diruiwa tsa mo gae tsa boitshimololedi le tsa saense

Our data can be reused for education, new products and services, research and more!

Polokelotshedimosetso ya Dintlha tsa Dijo tsa Diruiwa tsa mo Gae tse di Buletsweng ke tshedimosetso e e bulegileng e e gatisitsweng ka fa tlase ga Laesense ya Polokelotshedimosetso e e Buletsweng. Anyone can use it for any purpose - commercial or non-commercial - provided the source of the data is attributed and improvements to the database are shared in a similar way.

Education

Ithute ka phepo ya diruiwa tsa mo gae mme o tlhaloganye gore ke eng se se dirang gore ntša ya gago, katse kgotsa diruiwa tse dingwe tsa mo gae di nne le dijo tse di itekanetseng. Tshedimosetso e e bulegileng e thusa beng ba diruiwa tsa mo gae go dira ditshwetso tse di nang le kitso.

Products and services

Tshedimosetso ya Open Pet Food Facts e ka dirisiwa gape mo diporojekeng tse e seng tsa kgwebo le tsa kgwebo, fa fela ditokafatso tsa polokelotshedimosetso di abelanwa gape.

Science

Tshedimosetso ya Open Pet Food Facts e ka dirisiwa ke dingaka tsa diphologolo, baitseanape ba phepo ya diphologolo, le babatlisisi ba ba ithutang phepo ya diphologolo le boitekanelo. Thusa go nna le seabe mo saenseng e e botoka ya dijo tsa diruiwa tsa mo gae!
