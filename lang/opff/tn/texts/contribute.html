Add products

Dirisa tiriswa ya Open Food Facts ya Android kgotsa iPhone go sekaseka bonolo khoutu ya bara ya dikumo tsa dijo tsa diruiwa tsa mo gae go tswa kwa legaeng la gago kgotsa kwa mabenkeleng a diruiwa tsa mo gae a lefelo leo le go laisa ditshwantsho tsa dileibole tsa tsone.

Ditiriso tsa selulafouno di kopantswe, ka jalo jaanong o ka sekaseka dijo tsa diruiwa tsa mo gae, dijo tsa batho, ditlolo, le dikumo tse dingwe go tswa mo tirisong e le nngwe ya Open Food Facts!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Expert help needed

Go tlhama Dintlha tsa Dijo tsa Diruiwa tsa mo Gae tse di Buletsweng gape go tlhoka kitso e e kgethegileng le boitseanape mo dikarolong tse dintsi tse di farologaneng:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    Re tlhoka thuso go aga maitemogelo a a botoka a modirisi mo webosaeteng ya Open Pet Food Facts le app ya selulafouno, go tokafatsa moakanyetso wa tsone, go tlhama didirisiwa tsa tlhagiso tse di nang le tlhotlheletso jj.

  • Development

    Re na le tiro e ntsi ya tlhabololo e re tshwanetseng go e dira: mo backend ya Dintlha tsa Dijo tsa Diruiwa tse di Buletsweng (Perl le MongoDB), API (JSON), webosaete ya (HTML5, JS, Motheo), le app ya selulafouno. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Se se masisi le go feta, go na le dilo tse dintsi tse di monate tse badirisi, ba ba nang le seabe le ba ba dirisang gape ba di dirang ka Open Pet Food Facts, go ka nna molemo go dira gore batho ba le bantsi ba itse ka gone. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    Go na le ditiriso tse dintsi tse di kgatlhang le tsa ntlha tsa tshedimosetso e e bulegileng ya dijo tsa diruiwa tsa mo gae tse re ka dirang mo go tsone le dingaka tsa diphologolo, baitseanape ba phepo ya diruiwa tsa mo gae, batlhagisi ba dijo tsa diruiwa tsa mo gae, babatlisisi, diyunibesithi, dikolo, diNGO tsa katlaatleloloago ya diphologolo jj. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.
