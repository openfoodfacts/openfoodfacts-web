Contribue ad Apertas Factas de Cibo Animalium Domesticorum

All you need is a scan-do attitude! :-)

Possumusne industriam ciborum animalium domesticorum apertam et perspicuam reddere? Yes we scan!

Everyone can contribute

Particeps esto nostrae collaborativae, gratuitae et apertae collectionis ciborum animalium ex toto orbe terrarum!

"Open Pet Food Facts" est proiectum sine lucro totum a voluntariis factum; te sane desideramus.

Add products

Utere applicatione "Open Food Facts" pro Android vel iPhone ad facile codicem linearem ciborum animalium domesticorum vel tabernarum localium perscrutandum et imagines inscriptionum eorum imponendum.

Applicationes mobiles coniunctae sunt, ut nunc cibum animalium domesticorum, cibum humanum, cosmetica, et alia producta ex eadem applicatione "Open Food Facts" legere possis!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Applicationem institue ut perspicuitati cibi animalium conferas.

Tell the world

Placentne tibi "Open Pet Food Facts"? Tell others about it!

Proiectum familiae amicisque tuis praesentare, eis demonstrare quomodo applicationem instituant et contribuant, scribere articulum in diario electronico, "Open Pet Food Facts" in instrumentis socialibus communicare potes...

And present it live! "Open Pet Food Facts" auditoribus valde diversis in condicionibus valde diversis praesentavimus, et semper responsum valde enthusiasticum accipimus cum demonstramus quid fieri possit cum data de cibis animalium aperta sunt. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Una operari possumus ut insignia et inscriptiones ciborum animalium localium sustineamus et addamus.

Communitatem localem contributorum incipe vel ei intersis: cibos locales animalium domesticorum adde, amicos adscribe, proiectum in conventibus localibus et conventibus etc. praesenta.

Expert help needed

Elaboratio "Open Pet Food Facts" etiam requirit scientiam specialem et peritiam in multis diversis campis:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    Auxilio nobis opus est ut meliorem experientiam usoris in situ interretiali et applicatione mobili "Open Pet Food Facts" construamus, ut designum eorum emendemus, ut materiam praesentationis efficacem creemus, et cetera.

  • Development

    Multum laboris progressionis nobis faciendum est: in systemate administrativo "Open Pet Food Facts" (Perl et MongoDB), API (JSON), situ interretiali (HTML5, JS, Foundation), et applicatione mobili. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Serius autem, multa sunt res egregiae quas usores, contributores et qui iterum utuntur cum Open Pet Food Facts faciunt, optimum esset plures homines de hoc cognoscere. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    Multae sunt applicationes interessantes et originales notitiarum apertarum de cibo animalium domesticorum, in quibus cum veterinariis, nutritoribus animalium domesticorum, fabricatoribus ciborum animalium domesticorum, investigatoribus, universitatibus, scholis, NGOs pro cura animalium, et cetera, laborare possemus. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    Cum communitate in foro nostro disputare potes. Locus optimus est ad quaestiones ponendas, ideas de nutritione animalium communicandas, et in proiectis collaborandas.

  • Wiki

    Habemus etiam wiki quo utimur ad collaborative documentandum "Open Pet Food Facts" et eius sub-proiecta. Utimur etiam wiki ad administrandas interpretationes et ad elaborandas taxonomias / hierarchias categoriarum, inscriptionum etc.

Multae aliae rationes sunt ad Open Pet Food Facts contribuendum, quaeso nobiscum in Slack (in canali #openpetfoodfacts) iunge et disputationem incipiamus!
