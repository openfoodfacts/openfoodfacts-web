Qallariy utaq huk llaqta yanapakuq aylluman yaykuy: llaqta uywa mikhuy rurukunata yapay, masikunata mink'ay, proyectota llaqta huñunakuykunapi chaymanta hatun huñunakuykunapi hukkunapas rikuchiy.

Expert help needed

Kichasqa Uywa Mikhuy Chiqakunata wiñachinapaqqa, achka imaymana ruwaykunapi yachaykunata, yachaykunata ima necesitan:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    Yanapakuyta necesitayku huk aswan allin user experienciata ruwanapaq Open Pet Food Facts web kitipi chaymanta movil app kaqpi, allinchaypaq diseñonku, ruwanapaq impactoyuq rikuchiy materialkuna etc.

  • Development

    Askha wiñariy llamk'ay ruwanayku tiyan: Open Pet Food Facts backend kaqpi (Perl chaymanta MongoDB), API (JSON), web kitipi (HTML5, JS, Foundation), chaymanta kuyuchina app kaqpi. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Aswan serio, kan achka q'uñi ruwaykuna mayqinkunachus ruwaqkuna, yanapaqkuna chaymanta wakmanta llamk'achiqkuna ruwanku Open Pet Food Facts kaqwan, ancha allin kanman aswan runakuna chaymanta yachanankupaq. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    Kanmi achka munay hinaspa original aplicacionkuna uywakuna mikuy kichasqa willakuykunamanta mayqinkunapim llamkasunman uywa hampiqkunawan, uywa mikuna ruwaqkunawan, uywa mikuy ruwaqkunawan, investigadorkunawan, hatun yachaywasikunawan, yachaywasikunawan, uywakuna allin kananpaq ONGkunawan hukkunawanpas. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    Aylluwan rimayta atinki foronisqapi. Allinmi tapukuy ruwanapaq, uywakuna mikhuymanta yuyaykunata rakinapaq, chaymanta proyectokunapi yanapanakuypaq.

  • Wiki

    Hinallataqmi huk wiki nisqayuq kayku, chaywanmi kuska qillqayku Open Pet Food Facts nisqakunata, chaymanta subproyectos nisqakunatapas. Hinallataqmi wiki nisqawan t'ikraykunata kamachinapaq, hinallataq taxonomías / jerarquías nisqapi llamk'anapaq, categoría nisqakuna, etiquetakuna hukkunapas.

Aswan achka imaynakuna kanmi Kichasqa Uywa Mikhuy Chiqakunapi yanapakuypaq, ama hina kaspa Slack (#openpetfoodfacts kanalpi) ñuqaykuwan huñunakuy hinaspa rimayta qallarisun!
