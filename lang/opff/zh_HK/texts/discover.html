A pet food products database

Open Pet Food Facts is a database of pet food products with ingredients, nutritional values, and all the tidbits of information we can find on product labels.

Made by everyone

Open Pet Food Facts is a non-profit association of volunteers.

Pet owners like you can add products from around the world using the Open Food Facts app for Android or iPhone or their camera to scan barcodes and upload pictures of products and their labels.

Get the app to start scanning pet food

For everyone

Data about pet food is of public interest and has to be open. The complete database is published as open data and can be reused by anyone and for any use. Check-out the cool reuses or make your own!

  • Decode labels

    Pet food ingredients, additives, nutritional values... Open Pet Food Facts helps you to make sense of the fine print on product labels.

  • Find products

Pet food for innovation and science

Our data can be reused for education, new products and services, research and more!

The Open Pet Food Facts database is open data published under the Open Database Licence. Anyone can use it for any purpose - commercial or non-commercial - provided the source of the data is attributed and improvements to the database are shared in a similar way.

Education

Learn about pet nutrition and understand what makes a healthy diet for your dog, cat, or other pets. Open data helps pet owners make informed decisions.

Products and services

Open Pet Food Facts data can be reused in both non-commercial and commercial projects, as long as improvements to the database are shared as well.

Science

Open Pet Food Facts data can be used by veterinarians, pet nutritionists, and researchers studying animal nutrition and health. Help contribute to better pet food science!
