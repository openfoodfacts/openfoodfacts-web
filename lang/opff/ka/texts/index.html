Open Pet Food Facts

Discover

Open Pet Food Facts is a pet food database made by everyone, for everyone. You can use it to make better choices for your pet, and as it is open data, anyone can re-use it for any purpose.

Contribute

Open Pet Food Facts is a non-profit project developed by thousands of volunteers from around the world. You can start contributing by adding a pet food product with our app for iPhone or Android, and we have lots of exciting projects you can contribute to in many different ways.
