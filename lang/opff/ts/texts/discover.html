Our data can be reused for education, new products and services, research and more!

Dathabeyisi ya Tinhla ta Swakudya swa Swifuwo swa le Ndlwini leyi Pfulekeke i datha leyi pfulekeke leyi kandziyisiweke ehansi ka Layisense ya Dathabeyisi leyi Pfulekeke. Anyone can use it for any purpose - commercial or non-commercial - provided the source of the data is attributed and improvements to the database are shared in a similar way.
