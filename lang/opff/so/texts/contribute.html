Ma ka dhigi karnaa warshadaha cuntada xayawaanka kuwo furfuran oo hufan? Yes we scan!

Everyone can contribute

Ka mid noqo xogtayada wada shaqayneed, bilaash ah oo furan ee alaabta cuntada xayawaanka ee adduunka oo dhan!

Xaqiiqooyinka Cuntada Xayawaanka Furan waa mashruuc aan faa'iido doon ahayn oo ay si buuxda u sameeyeen tabaruceyaal, waanu kuu baahanahay.

Add products

U isticmaal abka Xaqiiqada Cunnada Furan ee Android ama iPhone si aad si fudud ugu sawirto barcode-ka alaabta cuntada xayawaanka gurigaaga ama dukaamada xayawaanka ee deegaanka una soo rareyso sawirada sumadaha.

Apps-ka moobilka waa la isku daray, si aad hadda u sawirto cuntada xayawaanka, cuntada dadka, waxyaalaha la isku qurxiyo, iyo alaabada kale ee isla app-ka Xaqiiqada Cunnada ee Furan!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Ku rakib abka si aad gacan uga geysato hufnaanta cuntada xayawaanka

Tell the world

Ma jeceshahay Xaqiiqooyinka Cuntada Xayawaanka Furan? Tell others about it!

Waxaad u soo bandhigi kartaa mashruuca qoyskaaga iyo asxaabtaada, tus sida loo rakibo abka oo wax ku biiriyaan, ku qor boostada blog, la wadaag Xaqiiqooyinka Cunnada Xayawaanka Furan ee baraha bulshada...

And present it live! Waxaan u soo bandhignay Xaqiiqooyinka Cunnada Xayawaanka Furan ee dhegeystayaal aad u kala duwan meelo aad u kala duwan, iyo waxaan had iyo jeer helnaa jawaab celin aad u xamaasad leh marka aan muujinno waxa la samayn karo marka xogta alaabta cuntada xayawaanku furan tahay. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Waxaan ka wada shaqeyn karnaa inaan taageerno oo aan ku darno calaamado loogu talagalay noocyada cuntada xayawaanka ee maxalliga ah iyo sumadaha.

Bilow ama ku biir bulshada wax ku biirinta maxalliga ah: ku dar alaabada cuntada xayawaanka rabaayada ah, qor saaxiibo, soo bandhig mashruuca shirarka maxaliga ah iyo shirarka iwm.

Expert help needed

Horumarinta Xaqiiqooyinka Cunnada Xayawaanka Furan waxay sidoo kale u baahan tahay aqoon gaar ah iyo khibrad meelo badan oo kala duwan:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    Waxaan u baahanahay caawimo si aan u dhisno khibrad isticmaale oo wanaagsan oo ku saabsan shabakada Xaqiiqada Cunnada Xayawaanka Furan iyo abka moobilka, si aan u wanaajino naqshadooda, si loo abuuro agab bandhigeed oo saameyn leh iwm.

  • Development

    Waxaan haynaa shaqooyin badan oo horumarineed oo aanu ku qabano: xaga dambe ee Xaqiiqooyinka Cunnada Xayawaanka Furan (Perl iyo MongoDB), API (JSON), web site (HTML5, JS, Foundation), iyo abka mobaylka. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Si ka sii daran, waxaa jira waxyaabo badan oo fiican oo isticmaalayaasha, wax ku biiriyaasha iyo dib-u-isticmaalka ay ku sameeyaan Xaqiiqooyinka Cunnada Xayawaanka Furan, way fiicnaan lahayd in dad badan ay ogaadaan. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    Waxaa jira codsiyo badan oo xiiso leh oo asal ah oo ah xogta furan ee cuntada xayawaanka ah oo aan ka shaqeyn karno dhakhaatiirta xoolaha, nafaqeeyayaasha xayawaanka, soosaarayaasha cuntada xayawaanka, cilmi-baarayaasha, jaamacadaha, dugsiyada, NGO-yada daryeelka xoolaha iwm. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    Waxaad bulshada kala hadli kartaa golahayaga . Waa meel aad u fiican in su'aalo lagu weydiiyo, lagu wadaago fikradaha ku saabsan nafaqeynta xayawaanka, lagana wada shaqeeyo mashaariicda.

  • Wiki

    Waxaan sidoo kale leennahay wiki oo aan u isticmaalno inaan si wada jir ah u diiwaangelinno Xaqiiqooyinka Cuntada Furan ee Xayawaanka iyo mashruucyadeeda. Waxaan sidoo kale u isticmaalnaa wiki si aan u maamulno tarjumaadaha iyo inaan ka shaqayno canshuuraha / kala sareynta qaybaha, sumadaha iwm.

Waxaa jira siyaabo badan oo badan oo wax loogu biirin karo Xaqiiqooyinka Cuntada Furan ee Xayawaanka, fadlan nagu soo biir Slack (ee kanaalka #openpetfoodfacts) oo aan bilowno dooda!
