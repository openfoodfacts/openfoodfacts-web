Xaqiiqooyinka Cunnada Xayawaanka ee Furan waa kaydka alaabta cuntada ee xayawaanka laga sameeyo maaddooyinka, qiyamka nafaqada, iyo dhammaan xogta macluumaadka aan ka heli karno calaamadaha alaabta.

Made by everyone

Xaqiiqooyinka Cunnada Xayawaanka Furan waa urur aan faa'iido doon ahayn oo iskaa wax u qabso ah.

Pet owners like you can add products from around the world using the Open Food Facts app for Android or iPhone or their camera to scan barcodes and upload pictures of products and their labels.

For everyone

Xogta ku saabsan cuntada xayawaanku waa danta guud waana inay furnaataa. The complete database is published as open data and can be reused by anyone and for any use. Check-out the cool reuses or make your own!

Samee doorashooyin cunto xayawaan oo wanaagsan

Knowledge is power. Aqoonta cuntada xayawaanku waa awood ay ku quudiso xayawaankaaga si ka wanaagsan!

  • Decode labels

    Maaddooyinka cuntada xayawaanka, waxyaabaha lagu daro, qiimaha nafaqada... Xaqiiqooyinka Cunnada Xayawaanka ee Furan waxay kaa caawineysaa inaad macno sameyso daabacaadda wanaagsan ee calaamadaha alaabta.

  • Find products

    Our search form includes dozens of criteria so that you can find pet food products that match your pet's needs.

  • Is barbar dhig oo dooro

    Ma garanaysaa cunnooyinka eyda leh borotiinka ugu badan? Cuntooyinka bisadaha ee aan hadhuudh lahayn? Waxaad is barbardhigi kartaa alaabooyinka noocyo kala duwan leh oo waxaad samayn kartaa go'aamo xog ogaal ah oo ku saabsan caafimaadka xayawaankaaga.

  • Explore and discover

    Ma jeceshahay cuntada xayawaanka? Sahami alaabooyinka wajiyada sida sumadaha, qaybaha, sumadaha, noocyada (eyyada, bisadaha, iwm.), iyo maaddooyinka. Soo ogow waxa runtii ku jira cuntada xayawaankaaga.

Cunto xayawaan ah oo loogu talagalay hal-abuurka iyo sayniska

Our data can be reused for education, new products and services, research and more!

Xogta Xaqiiqooyinka Cunnada Xayawaanka Furan waa xog furan oo lagu daabacay shatiga Xogta Furan. Anyone can use it for any purpose - commercial or non-commercial - provided the source of the data is attributed and improvements to the database are shared in a similar way.

Education

Wax ka baro nafaqada xayawaanka oo fahan waxa cunto caafimaad u leh eygaaga, bisaddaada, ama xayawaanka kale ee rabaayada ah. Xogta furan waxay ka caawisaa milkiilayaasha xayawaanka inay qaataan go'aamo xog ogaal ah.

Products and services

Xogta Xaqiiqooyinka Cunnada Xayawaanka Furan waxaa dib loogu isticmaali karaa labadaba mashruucyo aan ganacsi iyo ganacsi ahayn, ilaa iyo inta hagaajinta xogta xogta la wadaago sidoo kale.

Science

Xogta Xaqiiqooyinka Cunnada Xayawaanka Furan waxaa isticmaali kara dhakhaatiirta xoolaha, nafaqeeyayaasha xayawaanka, iyo cilmi-baarayaasha baraya nafaqada iyo caafimaadka xoolaha. Caawi wax ku biirinta sayniska cuntada xayawaanka ee wanaagsan!
