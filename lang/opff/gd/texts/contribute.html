Cuir ri Fiosrachadh Fosgailte mu Bhiadh Pheataichean

All you need is a scan-do attitude! :-)

An urrainn dhuinn gnìomhachas biadh pheataichean a dhèanamh nas fosgailte agus nas follaisiche? Yes we scan!

Everyone can contribute

Bi nad phàirt den stòr-dàta co-obrachail, an-asgaidh agus fosgailte againn de thoraidhean bìdh pheataichean bho air feadh an t-saoghail!

’S e pròiseact neo-phrothaideach a th’ ann an Open Pet Food Facts a chaidh a dhèanamh gu tur le saor-thoilich, agus tha feum againn ort.

Add products

Cleachd an aplacaid Open Food Facts airson Android no iPhone gus còd-barra thoraidhean bìdh pheataichean bhon dachaigh agad no bho stòran pheataichean ionadail a sganadh gu furasta agus dealbhan de na bileagan aca a luchdachadh suas.

Chaidh na h-aplacaidean gluasadach a chur còmhla, agus mar sin faodaidh tu a-nis biadh pheataichean, biadh daonna, cungaidhean maise, agus toraidhean eile a sganadh bhon aon aplacaid Open Food Facts!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Stàlaich an aplacaid gus cur ri follaiseachd biadh pheataichean

Tell the world

A bheil thu dèidheil air Fiosrachadh mu Bhiadh Peata Fosgailte? Tell others about it!

Faodaidh tu am pròiseact a thaisbeanadh do do theaghlach is do charaidean, sealltainn dhaibh mar a stàlaicheas iad an aplacaid agus cur ris, post blog a sgrìobhadh, Open Pet Food Facts a cho-roinn air na meadhanan sòisealta...

And present it live! Tha sinn air Fiosrachadh Fosgailte mu Bhiadh Pheataichean a thaisbeanadh do luchd-èisteachd gu math eadar-dhealaichte ann an suidheachaidhean gu math eadar-dhealaichte, agus bidh sinn an-còmhnaidh a’ faighinn freagairt glè dhealasach nuair a sheallas sinn dè a ghabhas dèanamh nuair a bhios an dàta airson toraidhean bìdh pheataichean fosgailte. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Is urrainn dhuinn obrachadh còmhla gus taic a thoirt do bhrandan is bileagan bìdh pheataichean ionadail agus suaicheantasan a chur ris.

Tòisich no thig còmhla ri coimhearsnachd luchd-tabhartais ionadail: cuir bathar bìdh pheataichean ionadail ris, fastaich caraidean, taisbein am pròiseact aig coinneamhan ionadail agus co-labhairtean msaa.

Expert help needed

Feumaidh eòlas agus eòlas sònraichte ann an iomadh raon eadar-dhealaichte a bhith ann cuideachd airson Fiosrachadh Fosgailte mu Bhiadh Pheataichean a leasachadh:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    We need help to build a better user experience on the Open Pet Food Facts web site and mobile app, to improve their design, to create impactful presentation materials etc.

  • Development

    Tha tòrr obair leasachaidh againn ri dhèanamh: air cùl-raon Open Pet Food Facts (Perl agus MongoDB), API (JSON), làrach-lìn (HTML5, JS, Foundation), agus an aplacaid gluasadach. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Nas cudromaiche buileach, tha tòrr rudan inntinneach ann a bhios luchd-cleachdaidh, luchd-tabhartais agus ath-chleachdaichean a’ dèanamh le Open Pet Food Facts, bhiodh e math barrachd dhaoine fhaighinn a-mach mu dheidhinn. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    Tha tòrr thagraidhean inntinneach is tùsail ann airson dàta fosgailte biadh pheataichean air am b’ urrainn dhuinn obrachadh le lighichean-sprèidh , luchd-beathachaidh pheataichean, luchd-saothrachaidh biadh pheataichean, luchd-rannsachaidh, oilthighean, sgoiltean, buidhnean neo-riaghaltais airson sunnd bheathaichean is eile. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    Faodaidh tu bruidhinn ris a’ choimhearsnachd air an fhòram againn. ’S e àite math a th’ ann airson ceistean fhaighneachd, beachdan a cho-roinn mu bhiadh pheataichean, agus co-obrachadh air pròiseactan.

  • Wiki

    Tha wiki againn cuideachd a bhios sinn a’ cleachdadh gus Open Pet Food Facts agus na fo-phròiseactan aige a chlàradh gu co-obrachail. We also use the wiki to manage translations and to work on the taxonomies / hierarchies of categories, labels etc.

There are many more ways to contribute to Open Pet Food Facts, please join us on Slack (in the #openpetfoodfacts channel) and let's start the discussion!
