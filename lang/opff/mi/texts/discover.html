For everyone

Ko nga raraunga e pa ana ki nga kai mo nga kararehe he mea pai mo te iwi, me tuwhera. The complete database is published as open data and can be reused by anyone and for any use. Check-out the cool reuses or make your own!

Kia pai ake nga whiringa kai mo nga kararehe

Knowledge is power. Ko te mohiotanga kai mo nga kararehe he mana kia pai ake te whangai i o kararehe!

  • Decode labels

    Nga kai mo nga kararehe, nga taapiri, nga uara kai... Ko te Whakatuwherahia nga korero mo te kai mo nga kararehe ka awhina koe ki te mohio ki te pai o te tuhi i runga i nga tapanga hua.

  • Find products

    Our search form includes dozens of criteria so that you can find pet food products that match your pet's needs.

  • Whakataurite me te whiriwhiri

    Kei te mohio koe he aha nga kai kurī he nui te pūmua? Ko ēhea kai ngeru karekau he witi? Ka taea e koe te whakataurite i nga hua mai i nga momo momo momo me te whakatau whakatau mo te hauora o au kararehe.

  • Explore and discover

    Kei te hiahia koe mo te kai mo nga kararehe? Tūhura hua mā ngā āhuatanga pēnei i ngā parani, ngā kāwai, ngā tapanga, ngā momo (kurī, ngeru, aha atu), me ngā kai. Rapua he aha kei roto i te kai a to mokai.

Kai mōkai mō te auaha me te pūtaiao

Our data can be reused for education, new products and services, research and more!

He raraunga tuwhera te papaunga raraunga Open Pet Food Facts i whakaputaina i raro i te Raihana Raihana Tuwhera. Anyone can use it for any purpose - commercial or non-commercial - provided the source of the data is attributed and improvements to the database are shared in a similar way.

Education
