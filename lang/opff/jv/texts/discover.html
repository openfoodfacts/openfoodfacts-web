Nggawe pilihan panganan pet sing luwih apik

Knowledge is power. Kawruh panganan kewan minangka kekuwatan kanggo menehi panganan sing luwih apik!

  • Decode labels

    Bahan pangan pet, aditif, nilai nutrisi ... Open Pet Food Facts mbantu sampeyan ngerteni cetakan sing apik ing label produk.

  • Find products

    Formulir telusuran kita kalebu puluhan kritéria supaya sampeyan bisa nemokake produk panganan pet sing cocog karo kabutuhan pet.

  • Bandingake lan pilih

    Apa sampeyan ngerti panganan asu sing paling akeh protein? Apa panganan kucing sing ora ana gandum? Sampeyan bisa mbandhingake produk saka macem-macem merek lan nggawe keputusan sing tepat kanggo kesehatan kewan sampeyan.

  • Explore and discover

    Penasaran karo panganan pet? Jelajahi produk miturut aspek kaya merek, kategori, label, spesies (asu, kucing, lsp.), lan bahan. Temokake apa sing sejatine ana ing panganan kewan sampeyan.

Our data can be reused for education, new products and services, research and more!

Basis data Open Pet Food Facts minangka data mbukak sing diterbitake miturut Lisensi Open Database. Anyone can use it for any purpose - commercial or non-commercial - provided the source of the data is attributed and improvements to the database are shared in a similar way.
