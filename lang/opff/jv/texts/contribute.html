Dadi bagean saka database kolaboratif, gratis lan mbukak produk panganan pet saka sak ndonya!

Open Pet Food Facts minangka proyek nirlaba sing digawe dening sukarelawan, kita butuh sampeyan.

Add products

Gunakake aplikasi Open Food Facts kanggo Android utawa iPhone supaya gampang mindhai kode bar produk panganan kewan saka omah utawa toko kewan lokal lan upload gambar labele.

Aplikasi seluler wis digabung, dadi saiki sampeyan bisa mindhai panganan kewan, panganan manungsa, kosmetik, lan produk liyane saka aplikasi Open Food Facts sing padha!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Tell the world

Apa sampeyan seneng Open Pet Food Facts? Tell others about it!

Sampeyan bisa nampilake proyek kasebut menyang kulawarga lan kanca-kanca, nuduhake carane nginstal app lan nyumbang, nulis postingan blog, nuduhake Open Pet Food Facts ing media sosial...

And present it live! Kita wis nampilake Open Pet Food Facts kanggo pamirsa sing beda banget ing setelan sing beda banget, lan kita tansah entuk respon sing antusias nalika nuduhake apa sing bisa ditindakake nalika data produk panganan pet mbukak. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Kita bisa bebarengan kanggo ndhukung lan nambah logo kanggo merek pangan pet lokal lan label.

Miwiti utawa gabung karo komunitas kontributor lokal: tambahake produk panganan kewan lokal, rekrut kanca, tampilake proyek kasebut ing rapat-rapat lokal lan konferensi lsp.

Expert help needed

Ngembangake Fakta Pangan Pet Terbuka uga mbutuhake kawruh lan keahlian khusus ing macem-macem wilayah:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    Kita butuh bantuan kanggo mbangun pengalaman panganggo sing luwih apik ing situs web Open Pet Food Facts lan aplikasi seluler, kanggo nambah desaine, kanggo nggawe materi presentasi sing duwe pengaruh lsp.

  • Development

    Kita duwe akeh karya pangembangan sing kudu ditindakake: ing backend Open Pet Food Facts (Perl lan MongoDB), API (JSON), Situs web (HTML5, JS, Foundation), lan aplikasi seluler. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Luwih serius, ana akeh perkara sing apik sing ditindakake dening pangguna, kontributor lan pangguna maneh karo Open Pet Food Facts, luwih apik yen luwih akeh wong ngerti babagan iki. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    There are lots of interesting and original applications of pet food open data that we could work on with veterinarians, pet nutritionists, pet food manufacturers, researchers, universities, schools, animal welfare NGOs etc. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    Sampeyan bisa rembugan karo masyarakat ing kita forum. Iki minangka papan sing apik kanggo takon, nuduhake ide babagan nutrisi kewan, lan kolaborasi ing proyek.

  • Wiki

    Kita uga duwe wiki sing digunakake kanggo nyathet bebarengan Open Pet Food Facts lan sub-proyek. Kita uga nggunakake wiki kanggo ngatur terjemahan lan nggarap taksonomi / hierarki kategori, label, lsp.

Ana akeh cara liyane kanggo kontribusi kanggo Open Pet Food Facts, mangga gabung karo Slack (ing saluran #openpetfoodfacts) lan ayo miwiti diskusi!
