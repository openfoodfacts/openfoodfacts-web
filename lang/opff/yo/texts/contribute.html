Ṣii Awọn Otitọ Ounjẹ Ọsin jẹ iṣẹ akanṣe ti ko ni ere ti a ṣe patapata nipasẹ awọn oluyọọda, a nilo rẹ.

Add products

Lo ohun elo Awọn Otitọ Ounjẹ Ṣiṣii fun Android > tabi iPhone lati ni irọrun ṣe ọlọjẹ kooduopo awọn ọja ounjẹ ọsin lati ile rẹ tabi awọn ile itaja ohun ọsin agbegbe ati gbejade awọn aworan ti awọn aami wọn.

Awọn ohun elo alagbeka naa ti dapọ, nitorinaa o le ṣe ọlọjẹ ounjẹ ẹran-ọsin, ounjẹ eniyan, awọn ohun ikunra, ati awọn ọja miiran lati inu ohun elo Awọn Otitọ Ounjẹ Ṣiṣii kanna!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Tell the world

Ṣe o fẹran Awọn Otitọ Ounjẹ Ọsin Ṣii? Tell others about it!

O le ṣafihan iṣẹ akanṣe naa si ẹbi ati awọn ọrẹ rẹ, fihan wọn bi wọn ṣe le fi app sori ẹrọ ati ṣe alabapin, kọ ifiweranṣẹ bulọọgi kan, pin Awọn Otitọ Ounjẹ Pet Open lori media awujọ…

And present it live! A ti ṣafihan Awọn Otitọ Ounjẹ Ọsin Ṣii si awọn olugbo ti o yatọ pupọ ni awọn eto oriṣiriṣi pupọ, ati a nigbagbogbo gba esi itara pupọ nigbati a ba ṣafihan kini ohun ti o le ṣee ṣe nigbati data fun awọn ọja ounjẹ ọsin ṣii. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

A le ṣiṣẹ papọ lati ṣe atilẹyin ati ṣafikun awọn aami fun awọn ami iyasọtọ ounjẹ ọsin agbegbe ati awọn aami.

Bẹrẹ tabi darapọ mọ agbegbe awọn oluranlọwọ agbegbe: ṣafikun awọn ọja ounjẹ ọsin agbegbe, gba awọn ọrẹ ṣiṣẹ, ṣafihan iṣẹ akanṣe ni awọn ipade agbegbe ati awọn apejọ abbl.

Expert help needed

Idagbasoke Awọn Otitọ Ounjẹ Ọsin Ṣiṣii tun nilo imọ amọja ati oye ni ọpọlọpọ awọn agbegbe oriṣiriṣi:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    A nilo iranlọwọ lati kọ iriri olumulo to dara julọ lori oju opo wẹẹbu Open Pet Food Facts ati ohun elo alagbeka, lati mu ilọsiwaju apẹrẹ wọn, lati ṣẹda awọn ohun elo igbejade ti o ni ipa ati bẹbẹ lọ.

  • Development

    A ni ọpọlọpọ awọn iṣẹ idagbasoke lati ṣe: lori Open Pet Food Facts backend (Perl ati MongoDB), API (JSON), oju opo wẹẹbu (HTML5, JS, Foundation), ati ohun elo alagbeka. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Ni pataki diẹ sii, ọpọlọpọ awọn ohun tutu lo wa ti awọn olumulo, awọn oluranlọwọ ati awọn atunlo tun ṣe pẹlu Ṣii Awọn Otitọ Ounjẹ Pet, yoo jẹ ohun nla lati gba eniyan diẹ sii lati mọ nipa rẹ. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    Ọpọlọpọ awọn ohun elo ti o nifẹ ati atilẹba ti ṣiṣi data ohun ọsin ti a le ṣiṣẹ lori pẹlu awọn oniwosan ẹranko, awọn onimọran ounjẹ ọsin, awọn olupese ounjẹ ọsin, awọn oniwadi, awọn ile-ẹkọ giga, awọn ile-iwe, awọn NGO ti iranlọwọ ẹranko ati bẹbẹ lọ. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    O le jiroro pẹlu agbegbe lori apejọ wa. O jẹ aaye nla lati beere awọn ibeere, pin awọn imọran nipa ounjẹ ọsin, ati ifowosowopo lori awọn iṣẹ akanṣe.

  • Wiki

    A tun ni wiki ti a lo lati ṣe iwe ifowosowopo Open Pet Food Facts ati awọn iṣẹ abẹ rẹ. A tun lo wiki lati ṣakoso awọn itumọ ati lati ṣiṣẹ lori awọn owo-ori / awọn ipo giga ti awọn ẹka, awọn aami abbl.

Ọpọlọpọ awọn ọna diẹ sii wa lati ṣe alabapin si Awọn Otitọ Ounjẹ Ọsin Ṣii, jọwọ darapọ mọ wa lori Slack (ninu ikanni #openpetfoodfacts) ati jẹ ki a bẹrẹ ijiroro naa!
