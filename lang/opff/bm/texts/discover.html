Made by everyone

Open Pet Food Facts ye tɔn ye min tɛ tɔnɔ ɲini, n’o ye baarakɛlaw ka tɔn ye.

So kɔnɔ bagantigiw i n’a fɔ aw bɛ se ka diɲɛ fan bɛɛ ka fɛnw fara u kan ni Open Food Facts app ye Android walima iPhone walima u ka kamera walasa ka barɔdiw sɛgɛsɛgɛ ani ka fɛnw jaw n’u tɔgɔw bila.

For everyone

Kunnafoni minnu bɛ sokɔnɔbaganw ka dumuni kan, olu nafa ka bon kosɛbɛ, wa u ka kan ka da mɔgɔw ɲɛ na. The complete database is published as open data and can be reused by anyone and for any use. Check-out the cool reuses or make your own!

Sokɔnɔbaganw ka dumuni ye kokuradonni ni dɔnniya kama

Our data can be reused for education, new products and services, research and more!

Open Pet Food Facts database ye kunnafoni dafalenw ye minnu bɛ bɔ Open Database Licence kɔnɔ. Anyone can use it for any purpose - commercial or non-commercial - provided the source of the data is attributed and improvements to the database are shared in a similar way.

Education

Aw bɛ sokɔnɔbaganw balocogo dɔn ani ka a faamu fɛn min bɛ dumuni nafama kɛ aw ka wulu, misi walima sokɔnɔbagan wɛrɛw ye. Donan dafalenw bɛ sokɔnɔbaganw tigiw dɛmɛ u ka desizɔnw ta minnu bɛ u hakili la.

Products and services

Open Pet Food Facts kunnafoniw bɛ se ka baara kɛ kokura porozɛw la minnu tɛ jago ye ani jago siratigɛ la, ni ɲɛtaa minnu kɛra kunnafonidilan na, olu fana bɛ tila ɲɔgɔn na.

Science
