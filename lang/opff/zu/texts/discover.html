Isizindalwazi semikhiqizo yokudla kwezilwane ezifuywayo

I-Open Pet Food Facts iyisizinda semininingwane yemikhiqizo yokudla kwezilwane ezifuywayo enezithako, amanani okudla okunempilo, kanye nawo wonke ama-tidbits olwazi esingaluthola kumalebula omkhiqizo.

Made by everyone

I-Open Pet Food Facts iyinhlangano engenzi nzuzo yamavolontiya.

Pet owners like you can add products from around the world using the Open Food Facts app for Android or iPhone or their camera to scan barcodes and upload pictures of products and their labels.

Thola uhlelo lokusebenza ukuze uqale ukuskena ukudla kwesilwane esifuywayo

For everyone

Idatha emayelana nokudla kwesilwane esifuywayo ithakaselwa umphakathi futhi kufanele ivuleke. The complete database is published as open data and can be reused by anyone and for any use. Check-out the cool reuses or make your own!

Yenza ukukhetha okungcono kokudla kwezilwane ezifuywayo

Knowledge is power. Ulwazi lokudla kwezilwane ezifuywayo lunamandla okondla izilwane ezifuywayo kangcono!

  • Decode labels

    Izithako zokudla kwesilwane esifuywayo, izithasiselo, amanani omsoco... I-Open Pet Food Facts ikusiza ukuthi wenze umqondo wokuphrinta kahle kumalebula omkhiqizo.

  • Find products

    Ifomu lethu losesho lihlanganisa inqwaba yemibandela ukuze uthole imikhiqizo yokudla kwezilwane ezifuywayo efana nezidingo zesilwane sakho esifuywayo.

  • Qhathanisa bese ukhetha

    Uyazi ukuthi yikuphi ukudla kwezinja okunamaprotheni amaningi? Yikuphi ukudla kwamakati okungenanhlamvu? Ungaqhathanisa imikhiqizo yemikhiqizo ehlukene futhi wenze izinqumo ezinolwazi ngempilo yezilwane ezifuywayo.

  • Explore and discover

    Ufuna ukwazi mayelana nokudla kwesilwane? Hlola imikhiqizo ngezinhlobo ezifana nemikhiqizo, izigaba, amalebula, izinhlobo (izinja, amakati, njll.), kanye nezithako. Zitholele ukuthi yini ngempela ekudleni kwesilwane sakho esifuywayo.

Ukudla kwezilwane ezifuywayo zokuqamba izinto ezintsha nesayensi

Our data can be reused for education, new products and services, research and more!

Isizindalwazi se-Open Pet Food Facts siyidatha evuliwe eshicilelwe ngaphansi kweLayisense Yesizindalwazi Esivulekile. Anyone can use it for any purpose - commercial or non-commercial - provided the source of the data is attributed and improvements to the database are shared in a similar way.

Education

Funda mayelana nokudla kwezilwane ezifuywayo futhi uqonde ukuthi yini eyenza ukudla okunempilo kwenja yakho, ikati, noma ezinye izilwane ezifuywayo. Idatha evuliwe isiza abanikazi bezilwane ukuthi benze izinqumo ezinolwazi.

Products and services

Idatha ye-Open Pet Food Facts ingasetshenziswa kabusha kumaphrojekthi angewona awokuthengisa nawentengiso, inqobo nje uma ukuthuthukiswa kwesizindalwazi kwabelwana ngakho.

Science

Idatha ye-Open Pet Food Facts ingasetshenziswa odokotela bezilwane, izazi zezilwane ezifuywayo, kanye nabacwaningi abafunda ukondliwa kwezilwane nempilo. Siza ukufaka isandla kusayensi yezilwane ezifuywayo ezingcono!
