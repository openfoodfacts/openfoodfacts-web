Everyone can contribute

Yiba yingxenye yesizindalwazi sethu esisebenzisanayo, samahhala nesivulekile semikhiqizo yokudla kwezilwane ezifuywayo evela emhlabeni wonke!

I-Open Pet Food Facts iphrojekthi engenzi nzuzo eyenziwe amavolontiya ngokuphelele, siyakudinga.

Add products

Sebenzisa uhlelo lokusebenza le-Open Food Facts ye- ye-Android noma i- iPhone ukuze uskene kalula ibhakhodi yemikhiqizo yokudla kwesilwane esifuywayo ekhaya lakho noma ezitolo zezilwane zasendaweni futhi ulayishe izithombe zamalebula azo.

Izinhlelo zokusebenza zeselula zihlanganisiwe, ngakho-ke ungakwazi manje ukuskena ukudla kwezilwane ezifuywayo, ukudla kwabantu, izimonyo, neminye imikhiqizo kusukela kuhlelo lokusebenza olufanayo lwe-Open Food Facts!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Faka uhlelo lokusebenza ukuze unikele ekukhanyeni kokudla kwesilwane esifuywayo

Tell the world

Ingabe uyawathanda amaqiniso okudla okufuywayo avulekile? Tell others about it!

Ungakwazi ukwethula iphrojekthi kumndeni wakho nakubangani, ubabonise indlela yokufaka uhlelo lokusebenza nokunikela, bhala iposi lebhulogi, wabelane nge-Open Pet Food Facts ezinkundleni zokuxhumana...

And present it live! Sethule amaqiniso e-Open Pet Food kuzithameli ezihluke kakhulu kuzilungiselelo ezihluke kakhulu, futhi sihlala sithola impendulo enomdlandla lapho sibonisa ukuthi yini engenziwa uma idatha yemikhiqizo yokudla kwezilwane ivuliwe. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Singasebenza ndawonye ukuze sisekele futhi sengeze amalogo wemikhiqizo yasendaweni yokudla kwezilwane ezifuywayo namalebula.

Qala noma joyina umphakathi wabanikeli basendaweni: engeza imikhiqizo yendawo yokudla kwezilwane ezifuywayo, nxenxa abangani, wethula iphrojekthi kumihlangano yendawo kanye nezinkomfa njll.

Expert help needed

Ukuthuthukisa Amaqiniso Okudla Kwezilwane Ezifuywayo kudinga ulwazi olukhethekile nobungcweti ezindaweni eziningi ezahlukahlukene:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    Sidinga usizo lokwakha ulwazi olungcono lomsebenzisi kuwebhusayithi ye-Open Pet Food Facts kanye nohlelo lokusebenza lweselula, ukuthuthukisa ukuklama kwabo, ukudala izinto zokwethula ezinomthelela njll.

  • Development

    Sinomsebenzi omningi wokuthuthukisa okufanele siwenze: kusizindalwazi se-Open Pet Food Facts (Perl ne-MongoDB), i-API (JSON), iwebhusayithi (HTML5, JS, Foundation), kanye nohlelo lokusebenza lweselula. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Okubaluleke nakakhulu, kunezinto eziningi ezinhle abasebenzisi, abanikeli nabasebenzisi kabusha abazenzayo nge-Open Pet Food Facts, kungaba kuhle ukuthola abantu abaningi bazi ngakho. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    Kunezinhlelo eziningi ezithokozisayo nezangempela zedatha evuliwe yokudla kwesilwane esifuywayo esingasebenza kuzo odokotela bezilwane, ongoti bezilwane ezifuywayo, abakhiqizi bokudla kwezilwane ezifuywayo, abacwaningi, amanyuvesi, izikole, ama-NGO wezenhlalakahle yezilwane njll. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    Ungaxoxisana nomphakathi kuforamu yethu. Yindawo enhle yokubuza imibuzo, ukwabelana ngemibono mayelana nokudla kwezilwane ezifuywayo, nokuhlanganyela kumaphrojekthi.

  • Wiki

    Futhi sine- wiki esiyisebenzisela ukwenza ngokuhlanganyela i-Open Pet Food Facts kanye namaphrojekthi angaphansi kwayo. Futhi sisebenzisa i-wiki ukuze silawule ukuhumusha nokusebenza ku-taxonomies /hierarchies yezigaba, amalebula njll.

Ziningi izindlela zokunikela ku-Open Pet Food Facts, sicela usijoyine ku-Slack (esiteshini esithi #openpetfoodfacts) futhi ake siqale ingxoxo!
