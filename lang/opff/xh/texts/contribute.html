Everyone can contribute

Yiba yinxalenye yethu yentsebenziswano, yasimahla kunye nevulekileyo yogcino lwedatha yeemveliso zokutya kwezilwanyana zasekhaya ezivela kwihlabathi liphela!

I-Open Pet Food Facts yiprojekthi engenzi nzuzo eyenziwe ngokupheleleyo ngamavolontiya, siyakudinga.

Add products

Sebenzisa i-Open Food Facts ye- Android okanye iPhone ukujonga ngokulula ibhakhowudi yeemveliso zokutya kwesilwanyana ekhaya okanye kwiivenkile zasekhaya zezilwanyana zasekhaya kwaye ulayishe imifanekiso yeeleyibhile zabo.

Iiapps eziphathwayo zidityanisiwe, ke ngoku unokuskena ukutya kwesilwanyana sasekhaya, ukutya kwabantu, izinto zokuthambisa, kunye nezinye iimveliso ezikwi-app ye-Open Food Facts!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Tell the world

Ngaba uyathanda iiNyaniso eziVulelekileyo zokuTya izilwanyana? Tell others about it!

Ungabonisa iprojekthi kusapho lwakho kunye nabahlobo, ubabonise indlela yokufaka i-app kunye negalelo, bhala iposti yebhlog, wabelane ngeeNyaniso eziVulekileyo zoKutya kwezilwanyana kwimidiya yoluntu...

And present it live! Sibonise i-Open Pet Food Facts kubaphulaphuli abahluke kakhulu kwiindawo ezahlukeneyo kakhulu, kwaye sihlala sifumana impendulo enomdla kakhulu xa sibonisa into enokwenziwa xa idatha yeemveliso zokutya kwezilwanyana zivuliwe. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Singasebenza kunye ukuxhasa kunye nokongeza iilogo kwiibrendi zokutya kwasekhaya kunye neelebhile.

Qala okanye ujoyine uluntu lwasekuhlaleni lwabaxhasi: yongeza iimveliso zokutya kwesilwanyana sasekhaya, fumana abahlobo, bonisa iprojekthi kwiindibano zasekuhlaleni kunye neenkomfa njl.

Expert help needed

Ukuphuhlisa iiNyaniso eziVulekileyo zoKutya kwezilwanyana zasekhaya kufuna ulwazi olukhethekileyo kunye nobuchule kwiindawo ezininzi ezahlukeneyo:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    Sidinga uncedo lokwakha amava omsebenzisi angcono kwiwebhusayithi ye-Open Pet Food Facts kunye ne-app yeselula, ukuphucula uyilo lwabo, ukudala izixhobo zokubonisa ezinempembelelo njl.

  • Development

    Sinomsebenzi omninzi wokuphuhlisa: kwi-Open Pet Food Facts backend (Perl kunye ne-MongoDB), i-API (JSON), indawo yewebhu (HTML5, JS, Foundation), kunye ne-app yeselula. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Okubaluleke ngakumbi, zininzi izinto ezipholileyo ezenziwa ngabasebenzisi, abaxhasi kunye nabasebenzisi kwakhona nge-Open Pet Food Facts, kuya kuba kuhle ukwenza abantu abaninzi bazi ngayo. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    Zininzi izinto ezinomdla kunye nezicelo zokuqala zedatha evulekileyo yokutya kwesilwanyana esinokusebenza oogqirha bezilwanyana, iingcali zesondlo sezilwanyana zasekhaya, abavelisi bokutya kwesilwanyana sasekhaya, abaphandi, iiyunivesithi, izikolo, ii-NGOs zentlalontle yezilwanyana njl.njl. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    Unokuxoxa noluntu kwiforum yethu . Yindawo entle yokubuza imibuzo, ukwabelana ngezimvo malunga nesondlo sezilwanyana zasekhaya, kunye nokusebenzisana kwiiprojekthi.

  • Wiki

    Sikwanayo wiki esiyisebenzisayo ukuze sibhale ngokubambisana i-Open Pet Food Facts kunye neeprojekthi eziphantsi kwayo. Sikwasebenzisa i-wiki ukulawula iinguqulelo kunye nokusebenza kwi i-taxonomies / hierarchies yeendidi, iilebhile njl.

Zininzi iindlela zokufaka isandla kwi-Open Pet Food Facts, nceda usijoyine kwi-Slack (kwitshaneli ye-#openpetfoodfacts) kwaye masiqale ingxoxo!
