For everyone

Idatha malunga nokutya kwesilwanyana inomdla woluntu kwaye kufuneka ivuleke. The complete database is published as open data and can be reused by anyone and for any use. Check-out the cool reuses or make your own!

Yenza ukhetho olungcono lokutya kwesilwanyana

Knowledge is power. Ulwazi lokutya kwesilwanyana sasekhaya lunamandla okondla izilwanyana zasekhaya ngcono!

  • Decode labels

    Izithako zokutya kwezilwanyana zasekhaya, izongezo, amaxabiso esondlo... Vula iiNyaniso zoKutya kwezilwanyana ezifuywayo zikunceda ukuba wenze intsingiselo yoshicilelo oluhle kwiilebhile zemveliso.

  • Find products

    Ifomu yethu yokukhangela ibandakanya uninzi lwemigaqo ukuze ufumane iimveliso zokutya kwesilwanyana sasekhaya ezihambelana neemfuno zesilo-qabane sakho.

  • Thelekisa kwaye ukhethe

    Ngaba uyazi ukuba kukuphi ukutya kwenja kuneyona protein ininzi? Kukuphi ukutya kwekati okungenazinkozo? Unokuthelekisa iimveliso ezivela kwiimpawu ezahlukeneyo kwaye wenze izigqibo ezinolwazi malunga nempilo yezilwanyana zasekhaya.

  • Explore and discover

    Ngaba unomdla malunga nokutya kwesilwanyana? Phonononga iimveliso ngokweendidi ezifana neebrendi, iindidi, iilebhile, iintlobo (izinja, iikati, njl.njl.), kunye nezithako. Fumanisa ukuba yintoni ngokwenene ekutyeni kwesilwanyana sasekhaya.

Ukutya kwezilwanyana ezifuywayo ukuze kwenziwe izinto ezintsha kunye nenzululwazi

Our data can be reused for education, new products and services, research and more!

I-Open Pet Food Facts yidatha evulekileyo epapashwe phantsi kweLayisensi ye-Open Database. Anyone can use it for any purpose - commercial or non-commercial - provided the source of the data is attributed and improvements to the database are shared in a similar way.
