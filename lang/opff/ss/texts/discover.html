For everyone

Idatha mayelana nekudla kwetilwane letifuywako kuyinshisekelo yemmango futsi kumele ivuleke. The complete database is published as open data and can be reused by anyone and for any use. Check-out the cool reuses or make your own!

Khetsa kudla kwetilwane letifuywako lokuncono

Knowledge is power. Lwati lwekudla kwetilwane letifuywako lunemandla ekondla tilwane takho letifuywako kancono!

  • Decode labels

    Titsako tekudla kwetilwane letifuywako, tintfo letingetiwe, emanani emsoco... Vula Emaciniso Ekudla Kwetilwane Letifuywako akusita kutsi ube nemcondvo wekubhala lokuncane kumalebuli emkhicito.

  • Find products

    Lifomu letfu lekuphenya lifaka ekhatsi inqwaba yemigomo kute utfole imikhicito yekudla kwetilwane letifuywako lehambisana netidzingo tesilwane sakho.

  • Catsanisa bese ukhetsa

    Uyati kutsi ngukuphi kudla kwetinja lokunemaprotein lamanyenti? Ngukuphi kudla kwemakati lokute tinhlavu? Ungacatsanisa imikhicito levela kuma brand lehlukene bese wenta tincumo letinelwati ngemphilo yetilwane takho letifuywako.

  • Explore and discover

    Uyafuna kwati ngekudla kwetilwane letifuywako? Hlola imikhicito ngetincenye letifana netinhlobo, tigaba, emalebuli, tinhlobo (tinja, emakati, njll.), kanye netitsako. Tfola kutsi yini ngempela ekudleni kwesilwane sakho.

Education

Fundza ngekudla kwetilwane letifuywako futsi uvisise kutsi yini leyenta kudla lokunemsoco kwenja yakho, ikati, noma letinye tilwane letifuywako. Idatha levulekile isita banikati betilwane letifuywako kutsi bente tincumo letinelwati.

Products and services

Idatha ye-Open Pet Food Facts ingaphindze isetjentiswe kuwo omabili emaphrojekthi langatsengiswa kanye nekutsengisa, uma nje kwenta ncono ku-database kwabelwana ngako futsi.

Science

Idatha ye Open Pet Food Facts ingasetjentiswa bodokotela betilwane, bodokotela bekudla kwetilwane letifuywako, kanye nebacwaningi labafundza ngekudla kwetilwane kanye nemphilo. Sita kufaka sandla kusayensi lencono yekudla kwetilwane letifuywako!
