Everyone can contribute

Yiba yincenye yetfu yekubambisana, yamahhala nalevulekile yemikhicito yekudla kwetilwane letifuywako levela emhlabeni wonkhe!

Open Pet Food Facts yiphrojekthi lengasebenti inzuzo leyentiwe ngalokuphelele ngemavolontiya, siyakudzinga.

Add products

Sebentisa luhlelo lokusebenta lweMaciniso Ekudla Lavulekile lwe Android nobe iPhone kute uskene kalula ibhakhodi yemikhicito yekudla kwetilwane letifuywako levela ekhaya lakho nobe etitolo tetilwane letifuywako tendzawo bese ulayisha titfombe temalebuli ato.

Letinhlelo tekusebenta temaselula setihlanganisiwe, ngako-ke nyalo ungaskena kudla kwetilwane letifuywako, kudla kwebantfu, tintfo tekutilungisa, kanye naleminye imikhicito kusuka ku-app lefanako ye-Open Food Facts!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Tell the world

Uyatsandza yini Emaciniso Ekudla Kwetilwane Letifuywako? Tell others about it!

Ungetfula lephrojekthi emndenini wakho nebangani bakho, ubakhombise kutsi ungayifaka njani le-app bese unikela, bhala i-blog post, wabelane ngeMaciniso Ekudla Kwetilwane Letifuywako Letivulekile etindzabeni tekuchumana...

And present it live! Setfule Emaciniso Ekudla Kwetilwane Letivulekile kulabo labahluke kakhulu etindzaweni letehluke kakhulu, futsi sihlala sitfola imphendvulo lenemdlandla kakhulu uma sikhombisa kutsi yini lengakwentiwa uma idatha yemikhicito yekudla kwetilwane letifuywako ivulekile. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Singasebentisana kusekela kanye nekwengeta emalogo etinhlobo tekudla kwetilwane letifuywako tendzawo kanye nemalebuli.

Cala noma ujoyine ummango webantfu labafaka sandla endzaweni: yengeta imikhicito yekudla kwetilwane letifuywako tendzawo, funa bangani, yetfula lephrojekthi emihlanganweni yendzawo kanye ne tinkomfa njll.

Expert help needed

Kutfutfukisa Emaciniso Ekudla Kwetilwane Letivulekile nako kudzinga lwati lolukhetsekile kanye nebungcweti etindzaweni letiningi letehlukene:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    Sidzinga lusito lwekwakha lwati loluncono lwemsebentisi kuwebhusayithi ye-Open Pet Food Facts kanye ne-app yeselula, kwenta ncono umklamo wabo, kwenta tintfo tekwetfula letinemtselela njll.

  • Development

    Sinemsebenti lomnyenti wekutfutfukisa lokufanele siwente: ku-backend yeMaciniso Ekudla Kwetilwane Letivulekile (Perl ne-MongoDB), i-API (JSON), iwebhusayithi (HTML5, JS, Sisekelo), kanye ne-app yeselula. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Ngalokubaluleke kakhulu, kunetintfo letinyenti letipholile letentiwa basebentisi, labafaka sandla kanye nebasebentisi labaphindze basebentise nge-Open Pet Food Facts, kungaba kuhle kutsi bantfu labanyenti bati ngako. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    Kukhona ticelo letiningi letijabulisako naletisunguliwe tedatha levulekile yekudla kwetilwane letifuywako lebesingasebenta kuyo ne bodokotela betilwane, bodokotela bekudla kwetilwane letifuywako, bakhiciti bekudla kwetilwane letifuywako, bacwaningi, emanyuvesi, tikolo, ema NGOs enhlalakahle yetilwane njll. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    Ungacoca nemmango enkundleni yetfu . Yindzawo lenhle yekubuta imibuto, kwabelana ngemibono mayelana nekudla kwetilwane letifuywako, kanye nekubambisana kumaphrojekthi.

  • Wiki

    Siphindze futsi sibe ne wiki lesiyisebentisako kubhala ngekubambisana Emaciniso Ekudla Kwetilwane Letifuywako Levulekile kanye nemaphrojekthi ayo lamancane. Siphindze futsi sisebentise i-wiki kulawula emahumusho kanye nekusebenta ku ema-taxonomies / ema-hierarchy etigaba, emalebuli njll.

Kukhona letinye tindlela letinyenti tekunikela ku-Eciniso Yekudla Kwetilwane Letifuywako Levulekile, sicela uhlanganyele natsi ku-Slack (esiteshini se- #openpetfoodfacts) bese sicala ingcoco!
