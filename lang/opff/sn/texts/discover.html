For everyone

Data pamusoro pechikafu chemhuka inofarira veruzhinji uye inofanirwa kuvhurwa. The complete database is published as open data and can be reused by anyone and for any use. Check-out the cool reuses or make your own!

Ita sarudzo dziri nani dzechikafu chemhuka

Knowledge is power. Ruzivo rwechikafu chemhuka isimba rekudyisa zvipfuwo zvako zviri nani!

  • Decode labels

    Zvigadzirwa zvechipfuwo chekudya, zvinowedzera, zvinovaka muviri ... Vhura Pet Chikafu Chokwadi chinokubatsira kuti uite pfungwa yekudhinda kwakanaka pane zvinyorwa zvechigadzirwa.

  • Find products

    Yedu yekutsvaga fomu inosanganisira akawanda emaitiro kuitira kuti uwane zvigadzirwa zvechipfuwo zvinoenderana nezvinodiwa nemhuka yako.

  • Enzanisa uye sarudza

    Unoziva here kuti ndezvipi zvekudya zvembwa zvine mapuroteni akawanda? Ndezvipi zvekudya zvekatsi zvisina zviyo? Iwe unogona kufananidza zvigadzirwa kubva kune akasiyana mhando uye kuita sarudzo dzine ruzivo nezvehutano hwemhuka dzako.

  • Explore and discover

    Unoda kuziva nezvechikafu chemhuka? Ongorora zvigadzirwa nemhando semhando, zvikamu, zvinyorwa, zvipenyu (imbwa, katsi, nezvimwewo), uye zvinongedzo. Ziva zviri mukudya kwechipfuwo chako.

Chikafu chemhuka chekuvandudza nesainzi

Our data can be reused for education, new products and services, research and more!

Iyo Open Pet Food Chokwadi dhatabhesi yakavhurika data yakaburitswa pasi peiyo Open Database Rezinesi. Anyone can use it for any purpose - commercial or non-commercial - provided the source of the data is attributed and improvements to the database are shared in a similar way.

Education

Dzidza nezvekudya kwezvipfuyo uye unzwisise kuti chii chinoita chikafu chine hutano kune imbwa yako, katsi, kana dzimwe mhuka dzinovaraidza. Vhura data inobatsira varidzi vezvipfuyo kuita sarudzo dzine ruzivo.

Products and services

Vhura Pet Food Chokwadi data inogona kushandiswazve mune ese asiri ekutengesa uye ekutengesa mapurojekiti, chero bedzi kuvandudzwa kweiyo dhatabhesi kunogovaniswa zvakare.

Science

Vhura Pet Food Chokwadi data inogona kushandiswa neveterinarians, pet nutritionists, uye vaongorori vari kudzidza chikafu chemhuka uye hutano. Batsira kubatsira kune zvirinani chikafu chinovaraidza sainzi!
