No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Expert help needed

Kugadzira Open Pet Food Chokwadi kunodawo ruzivo rwakanyanya uye hunyanzvi munzvimbo dzakawanda dzakasiyana:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    Tinoda rubatsiro kuvaka ruzivo rwemushandisi zviri nani pawebhusaiti yeOpen Pet Food Chokwadi uye nharembozha, kuvandudza dhizaini yavo, kugadzira zvigadzirwa zvinokurudzira zvekuratidzira nezvimwe.

  • Development

    Tine basa rakawanda rekusimudzira rekuita: paOpen Pet Food Chokwadi backend (Perl neMongoDB), API (JSON), webhusaiti (HTML5, JS, Foundation), uye nharembozha. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Zvakanyanya kukosha, kune zvakawanda zvezvinhu zvinotonhorera izvo vashandisi, vabatsiri uye vashandisi vanoita zvakare Vhura Pet Chikafu Chokwadi, zvingave zvakanaka kuti vanhu vazhinji vazive nezvazvo. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    Pane zvakawanda zvinofadza uye zvekutanga zvekushandisa zvechikafu zvemhuka zvakavhurika data zvatinga shanda nazvo veterinarian, vezvekudya kwezvipfuyo, vanogadzira chikafu chezvipfuyo, vaongorori, mayunivhesiti, zvikoro, maNGOs ekuchengetedza mhuka nezvimwe. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    Unogona kukurukura nenharaunda pane yedu forum. Inzvimbo yakanaka yekubvunza mibvunzo, kugovana mazano nezvekudya kwezvipfuyo, uye kubatana pamapurojekiti.

  • Wiki

    Isu tinewo wiki yatinoshandisa kunyora nekubatana Open Pet Food Chokwadi nemapurojekiti ayo madiki. Isu tinoshandisawo wiki kubata shanduro uye kushanda pa taxonomies / hierarchies yezvikamu, zvinyorwa nezvimwe.

Kune dzimwe nzira dzakawanda dzekupa kuVhura Chikafu Chikafu Chokwadi, ndapota tibatane nesu paSlack (mu #openpetfoodfacts chiteshi) uye ngatitange nhaurirano!
