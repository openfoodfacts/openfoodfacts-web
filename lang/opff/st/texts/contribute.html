Everyone can contribute

Eba karolo ea tšebelisano-'moho, ea mahala le e bulehileng ea lihlahisoa tsa liphoofolo tsa lapeng tse tsoang lefats'eng ka bophara!

Open Pet Food Facts ke projeke e sa etseng phaello e entsoeng ke baithaopi, rea u hloka.

Add products

Sebelisa sesebelisoa sa Open Food Facts bakeng sa Android kapa iPhone ho sheba habonolo libarcode tsa lijo tsa lapeng ho tsoa lapeng kapa mabenkeleng a lapeng le ho kenya linepe tsa mabitso a tsona.

Lisebelisoa tsa mehala li kopantsoe, kahoo joale u ka khona ho hlahloba lijo tsa lapeng, lijo tsa batho, litlolo le lihlahisoa tse ling ho tsoa ho sesebelisoa se tšoanang sa Open Food Facts!

No smartphone? No problem: you can also use your camera to add products directly on the web site.

Kenya sesebelisoa ho kenya letsoho ponaletsong ea lijo tsa liphoofolo tsa lapeng

Tell the world

Na u rata Lintlha tsa Open Pet Food? Tell others about it!

O ka hlahisa projeke ho ba lelapa le metsoalle ea hau, oa ba bontša mokhoa oa ho kenya sesebelisoa le ho kenya letsoho, ngola poso ea blog, arolelana lintlha tsa Open Pet Food mecheng ea litaba tsa sechaba...

And present it live! Re hlahisitse lintlha tsa Open Pet Food ho bamameli ba fapaneng haholo maemong a fapaneng haholo, 'me re lula re fumana karabelo e chesehang ha re bonts'a se ka etsoang ha data ea lihlahisoa tsa liphoofolo tsa lapeng e bulehile. We have lots of presentation materials (slides etc.) that you can adapt or present as-is.

Make it local

You can help to translate the site and mobile app in your language, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Re ka sebetsa 'moho ho ts'ehetsa le ho kenya li-logo bakeng sa mefuta le lileibole tsa lijo tsa lapeng.

Qala kapa ikopanye le sechaba sa lehae sa batšehetsi: kenya lihlahisoa tsa lehae tsa lijo tsa lapeng, kopa metsoalle, hlahisa projeke ka likopano tsa lehae le likopano joalo-joalo.

Expert help needed

Ho nts'etsapele lintlha tsa Open Pet Food ho boetse ho hloka tsebo e khethehileng le boiphihlelo libakeng tse ngata tse fapaneng:

  • Project management

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Design

    Re hloka thuso ho aha boiphihlelo bo betere ba mosebelisi ho webosaete ea Open Pet Food Facts le sesebelisoa sa mehala, ho ntlafatsa moralo oa bona, ho theha lisebelisoa tse nang le tšusumetso jj.

  • Development

    Re na le mosebetsi o mongata oa ntlafatso oo re lokelang ho o etsa: ho Open Pet Food Facts (Perl le MongoDB), API (JSON), sebaka sa marang-rang (HTML5, JS, Foundation), le app mobile. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Community building

    We need to build local communities in all countries and at the same time unite them globally.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Are you one of them?

  • Communication

    We are not sure what to write here, could you help?

    Habohlokoa le ho feta, ho na le lintho tse ngata tse monate tseo basebelisi, bafani le basebelisi ba li sebelisang hape ka Open Pet Food Facts, ho ka ba monate ho etsa hore batho ba bangata ba tsebe ka eona. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Special projects

    Ho na le lits'ebetso tse ngata tse khahlisang le tsa mantlha tsa data e bulehileng ea lijo tsa liphoofolo tseo re ka sebetsanang le tsona lingaka tsa liphoofolo, litsebi tsa phepo ea liphoofolo, baetsi ba lijo tsa liphoofolo tsa lapeng, bafuputsi, liunivesithi, likolo, mekhatlo e ikemetseng ea boiketlo ba liphoofolo jj. Maybe you already have ideas? If you do, please help us to push them forward.

Let's talk!

A lot of ideas, energy and enthusiasm are shared in our contributors community, join us!

Here are some places where you can meet other contributors, talk to them and work with them:

  • Slack

    Slack is the best way to interact and collaborate with other contributors, developers and reusers. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. It completely changed how we work together. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Forum

    U ka buisana le sechaba ho rona forum. Ke sebaka se setle sa ho botsa lipotso, ho arolelana maikutlo mabapi le phepo ea liphoofolo tse ruuoang lapeng, le ho sebelisana 'moho mererong.

  • Wiki

    Hape re na le wiki eo re e sebelisang ho ngola ka kopanelo lintlha tsa Open Pet Food le merero ea eona e ka tlase. Hape re sebelisa wiki ho laola liphetolelo le ho sebetsa ho taxonomies / hierarchies ea lihlopha, li-label jj.

Ho na le mekhoa e meng e mengata ea ho kenya letsoho ho Open Pet Food Facts, ka kopo ikopanye le rona ho Slack (ho #openpetfoodfacts mocha) 'me re qale puisano!
