For everyone

Lintlha tse mabapi le lijo tsa lapeng li khahla sechaba 'me li tlameha ho buloa. The complete database is published as open data and can be reused by anyone and for any use. Check-out the cool reuses or make your own!

Etsa khetho e ntle ea lijo tsa lapeng

Knowledge is power. Tsebo ea lijo tsa lapeng ke matla a ho fepa liphoofolo tsa lapeng hamolemo!

  • Decode labels

    Lisebelisoa tsa lijo tsa liphoofolo tse ruuoang lapeng, li-additives, boleng ba phepo ... Open Pet Food Facts e o thusa ho utloisisa mongolo o motle oa lileibole tsa sehlahisoa.

  • Find products

    Mofuta oa rona oa ho batla oa ho batla o kenyelletsa lintlha tse ngata e le hore o ka fumana lihlahisoa tsa lijo tsa lapeng tse tsamaellanang le litlhoko tsa phoofolo ea hau ea lapeng.

  • Bapisa le ho khetha

    Na ua tseba hore na ke lijo life tsa ntja tse nang le protheine e ngata? Ke lijo life tsa katse tse se nang lijo-thollo? O ka bapisa lihlahisoa tsa mefuta e fapaneng mme oa etsa liqeto tse nepahetseng bakeng sa bophelo bo botle ba liphoofolo tsa lapeng.

  • Explore and discover

    U batla ho tseba ka lijo tsa lapeng? Lekola lihlahisoa ka likarolo tse kang mefuta, mekhahlelo, lileibole, mefuta (lintja, likatse, joalo-joalo), le metsoako. Fumana se hlileng se leng teng ka har'a lijo tsa phoofolo ea lapeng.

Lijo tsa liphoofolo tse ruuoang lapeng bakeng sa boqapi le mahlale

Our data can be reused for education, new products and services, research and more!

Sebaka sa polokelo ea lintlha tsa Open Pet Food ke lintlha tse bulehileng tse hatisitsoeng tlas'a Laesense ea Open Database. Anyone can use it for any purpose - commercial or non-commercial - provided the source of the data is attributed and improvements to the database are shared in a similar way.

Education

Ithute ka phepo e nepahetseng ea liphoofolo tse ruuoang lapeng 'me u utloisise hore na ke eng e etsang hore ntja ea hau, katse, kapa liphoofolo tse ling li je lijo tse phetseng hantle. Lintlha tse bulehileng li thusa beng ba liphoofolo tsa lapeng ho etsa liqeto tse nepahetseng.

Products and services

Lintlha tsa Open Pet Food Facts li ka sebelisoa hape mererong eo e seng ea khoebo le ea khoebo, ha feela lintlafatso tsa database li arolelanoa.

Science

Lintlha tsa Open Pet Food Facts li ka sebelisoa ke lingaka tsa liphoofolo, litsebi tsa phepo ea liphoofolo, le bafuputsi ba ithutang ka phepo e nepahetseng ea liphoofolo le bophelo bo botle ba liphoofolo. Thusa ho kenya letsoho ho mahlale a ntlafetseng a lijo tsa liphoofolo tsa lapeng!
