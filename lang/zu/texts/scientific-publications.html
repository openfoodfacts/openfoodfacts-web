Izincwadi Zesayensi

Amaqiniso Okudla Avulekile ocwaningweni: izincwadi, izingcaphuno, kanye nezinsiza zabacwaningi

Kusukela ngo-2012, i-Open Food Facts isibe umthombo obalulekile kubacwaningi emhlabeni wonke abasebenzisa idatha yethu yokudla ezifundweni zokudla okunempilo, imvelo, impilo yomphakathi, kanye neminye imikhakha eminingi. Isizindalwazi sethu esivulekile sicashunwe emaphepheni esayensi amaningi.

Kwabacwaningi

Ingabe usebenzisa noma ucabangela ukusebenzisa i-Open Food Facts njengengxenye yephrojekthi yocwaningo?

Sicela usazise ku- contact@openfoodfacts.org

Siyajabula ukusiza nokuxhumana namaqembu ocwaningo ukuze sibone ukuthi singawusekela kanjani umsebenzi wakho.

Izindawo Ezibalulekile Zocwaningo

Idatha yethu isekela izizinda zocwaningo ezahlukahlukene

Ukudla Okunomsoco Nempilo Yomphakathi

Abacwaningi basebenzisa isizindalwazi sethu solwazi ukutadisha ikhwalithi yokudla okunempilo, ukwakheka kokudla, kanye nokungenelela kwezempilo yomphakathi. Idatha ye-Open Food Facts ibe negalelo elikhulu ocwaningweni nasekuhlolweni kwe-Nutri-Score.

Umthelela Wezemvelo

Izifundo mayelana nokukhishwa kwekhabhoni, i-Eco-Score, kanye nokusimama kwemvelo kwemikhiqizo yokudla kuzuza kusizindalwazi sethu esiphelele solwazi lomkhiqizo.

Ukucubungula Ukudla

Ucwaningo ngokudla okucutshungulwe kakhulu kusetshenziswa ukuhlukaniswa kwe-NOVA luncike ohlwini lwethu lwezithako olunemininingwane kanye nolwazi lokucubungula.

Isayensi Yekhompyutha kanye ne-AI

Ukufunda komshini, ukuqashelwa kwezithombe, ukucubungula ulimi lwemvelo, kanye namaphrojekthi esayensi yedatha kusebenzisa amasethi ethu edatha avulekile nezithombe.

Ukuziphatha Kwabathengi

Izifundo ezihlola ukukhetha kokudla, imiphumela yokulebula, kanye nokucaca embonini yokudla zisebenzisa idatha ye-Open Food Facts.

Inqubomgomo Yokudla

Abenzi bezinqubomgomo nabacwaningi bahlaziya imithetho yokudla, izidingo zokulebula, kanye nemikhuba yemboni besebenzisa isizindalwazi sethu esisobala.

Izinsiza Zabacwaningi

Indlela yokusebenzisa nokucaphuna amaqiniso okudla avulekile

Ukufinyelela Idatha

I-Open Food Facts inikeza izindlela eziningi zokufinyelela idatha yethu:

  • Ukuthunyelwa kwesizindalwazi: Landa ama-dump aphelele esizindalwazi ngamafomethi ahlukahlukene. Bheka ikhasi lethu ledatha ukuthola imininingwane.
  • I-API: Buza ngedathabheyisi yethu ngendlela ehlelekile nge-API yethu ephelele. Imibhalo ye-API
  • Isixhumi sokusesha: Hlola imikhiqizo ngqo kuwebhusayithi yethu ngamakhono okusesha athuthukisiwe nokuhlunga.

Ilayisensi

Idatha yethu ivulekile futhi ingasetshenziswa kabusha mahhala:

  • Idathabheyisi iyatholakala ngaphansi kwe- Ilayisensi Yedathabheyisi Evulekile (ODbL)
  • Okuqukethwe kwedatha kungaphansi kwe- Ilayisensi Yokuqukethwe Kwedatha (DbCL)
  • Izithombe zomkhiqizo zitholakala ngaphansi kwe- Creative Commons Attribution ShareAlike (CC BY-SA 3.0)

Bheka Imigomo nemibandela yokusebenzisa kanye nokusetshenziswa kabusha ukuze uthole imininingwane ephelele.

Data Quality

Njengesizindalwazi esinabantu abaningi, ikhwalithi yedatha ingahluka. Uma usebenzisa i-Open Food Facts ocwaningweni:

  • Cabanga ngokuhlunga ngamaphuzu okuphelela noma izinkomba zekhwalithi yedatha
  • Qinisekisa amaphuzu edatha abalulekile uma ukunemba kubalulekile
  • Qaphela ukucwasa ngokwendawo kanye nesikhathi ekumbozweni kwedatha yethu
  • Xhumana nathi uma udinga usizo ngokuhlolwa kwekhwalithi yedatha ocwaningweni lwakho

Bambisana Nathi

Siyakwamukela ukubambisana namaqembu ocwaningo:

  • Izidingo zedatha: Sazise uma udinga idatha ethile noma ukuthuthukiswa kwesakhiwo sedatha yethu
  • Skena amaqembu: Hlela imikhankaso yokuqoqwa kwedatha eqondiwe yesifunda sakho socwaningo noma isigaba somkhiqizo
  • Ukwabelana ngemiphumela: Yabelana ngokutholakele kwakho nomphakathi wethu futhi usize ekuthuthukiseni ulwazi
  • Ukuthuthukiswa kwamathuluzi: Sebenzisana ngamathuluzi azuzisa abacwaningi kanye nabathengi

Xhumana nathi ku- contact@openfoodfacts.org ukuze nixoxe ngamathuba okubambisana.
