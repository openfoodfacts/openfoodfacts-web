Lingoliloeng tsa Saense

Lintlha tsa Lijo tse Bulehileng lipatlisisong: lingoliloeng, litšupiso le mehloli bakeng sa bafuputsi

Ho tloha ka 2012, Open Food Facts e fetohile mohloli oa bohlokoa bakeng sa bafuputsi lefatšeng ka bophara ba sebelisang lintlha tsa rona tsa lijo bakeng sa lithuto tsa phepo e nepahetseng, tikoloho, bophelo bo botle ba sechaba le masimo a mang a mangata. Lefapha la rona la polokelo ea litaba le bulehileng le qotsitsoe lipampiring tse makholo tsa mahlale.

Bakeng sa Bafuputsi

Na o sebedisa kapa o nahana ho sebedisa Open Food Facts e le karolo ya projeke ya dipatlisiso?

Ka kopo re tsebise ho contact@openfoodfacts.org

Re thabela ho thusa le ho sebelisana le lihlopha tsa lipatlisiso ho bona hore na re ka tšehetsa mosebetsi oa hau joang.

Libaka tsa Bohlokoa tsa Lipatlisiso

Lintlha tsa rona li tšehetsa libaka tse fapaneng tsa lipatlisiso

Phepo le Bophelo bo Botle ba Sechaba

Bafuputsi ba sebelisa database ea rona ho ithuta boleng ba phepo e nepahetseng, sebopeho sa lijo le mekhoa ea bophelo bo botle ba sechaba. Lintlha tsa Open Food Facts li bile le thuso lipatlisisong le tlhahlobong ea Nutri-Score.

Tšusumetso ea Tikoloho

Lithuto mabapi le khabone e bakang ho fokotseha ha mocheso, Eco-Score, le botsitso ba tikoloho ba dihlahiswa tsa dijo di rua molemo ho database ya rona e felletseng ya tlhahisoleseding ya dihlahiswa.

Ho Sireletsa Lijo

Patlisiso mabapi le lijo tse sebetsitsoeng haholo ho sebelisoa sehlopha sa NOVA e itšetlehile ka lethathamo la rona le qaqileng la metsoako le tlhaiso-leseling ea ts'ebetso.

Saense ea Khomphutha le AI

Ho ithuta ka mochini, ho lemoha litšoantšo, ts'ebetso ea puo ea tlhaho, le merero ea saense ea data li sebelisa li-dataset tsa rona tse bulehileng le litšoantšo.

Boitšoaro ba Bareki

Lithuto tse hlahlobang likhetho tsa lijo, litlamorao tsa ho ngola mabitso, le ponaletso indastering ea lijo li sebelisa data ea Open Food Facts.

Leano la Lijo

Baetsi ba maano le bafuputsi ba sekaseka melaoana ea lijo, litlhoko tsa ho ngola mabitso le mekhoa ea indasteri ba sebelisa database ea rona e bonaletsang.

Ho fihlella Lintlha

Open Food Facts e fana ka litsela tse ngata tsa ho fihlella tlhahisoleseling ea rona:

  • Diromelwantle tsa polokelo ya data: Khoasolla di-dump tse felletseng tsa polokelo ya data ka dibopeho tse fapaneng. Sheba leqephe la rona la la data bakeng sa lintlha tse qaqileng.
  • API: Botsa ka lenaneo la database ea rona ka API ea rona e felletseng. Litokomane tsa API
  • Sebopeho sa ho batla: Hlahloba dihlahiswa ka ho toba webosaeteng ya rona ka bokgoni bo tswetseng pele ba ho batla le ho sefa.

Laesense

Lintlha tsa rona li bulehile 'me li ka sebelisoa hape ka bolokolohi:

  • Database e fumaneha tlasa Laesense ya Database e Butsweng (ODbL)
  • Dikahare tsa polokelo ya data di tlasa Laesense ya Dikahare tsa polokelo ya data (DbCL)
  • Litšoantšo tsa sehlahisoa li fumaneha tlas'a Creative Commons Attribution ShareAlike (CC BY-SA 3.0)

Sheba Melao le maemo a rona a a tšebeliso le tšebeliso hape bakeng sa lintlha tse felletseng.

Data Quality

Jwalo ka database e nang le batho ba bangata, boleng ba data bo ka fapana. Ha o sebedisa Open Food Facts bakeng sa dipatlisiso:

  • Nahana ka ho sefa ka lintlha tse felletseng kapa matšoao a boleng ba data
  • Netefatsa lintlha tsa bohlokoa tsa data haeba ho nepahala ho le bohlokoa
  • Ela hloko leeme la libaka le la nako ha re akaretsa lintlha tsa rona
  • Ikopanye le rona haeba o hloka thuso ka tlhahlobo ea boleng ba data bakeng sa lipatlisiso tsa hau

Sebetsa 'moho le Rona

Re amohela tšebelisano-'moho le lihlopha tsa lipatlisiso:

  • Ditlhoko tsa data: Re tsebise haeba o hloka data e itseng kapa dintlafatso sebopehong sa database ya rona.
  • Mekga ya ho skena: Hlophisa matsholo a ho bokella tlhahisoleseding a reretsweng sebaka sa hao sa dipatlisiso kapa sehlopha sa dihlahiswa
  • Ho arolelana diphetho: Abelana diphihlello tsa hao le setjhaba sa rona mme o thuse ho ntshetsa pele tsebo
  • Ntlafatso ea lisebelisoa: Sebelisana le lisebelisoa tse thusang bafuputsi le bareki

Ikopanye le rona ho contact@openfoodfacts.org ho buisana ka menyetla ea tšebelisano-'moho.

Tšusumetso ea Lipatlisiso

Open Food Facts e entse menehelo e kholo lipatlisisong tsa saense le bophelo bo botle ba sechaba:

  • Lingoliloeng tsa saense tse fetang 600 li qotsitse database ea rona
  • Lihlopha tse kholo tsa lipatlisiso ho kenyeletsoa EREN (Fora), USP (Brazil), le Univesithi ea Liège (Belgium) li sebelisitse lintlha tsa rona.
  • Database ea rona e tšehelitse lipatlisiso tse lebisang liphetohong tsa leano le likhothaletso tsa bophelo bo botle ba sechaba
  • Re nolofalitse lipapiso tsa linaha tse fapaneng le lithuto tsa lefats'e ka bophara mabapi le boleng ba lijo le phepo e nepahetseng
