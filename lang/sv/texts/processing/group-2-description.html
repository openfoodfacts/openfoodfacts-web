Grupp 2. Bearbetade kulinariska ingredienser

Bearbetade kulinariska ingredienser, såsom oljor, smör, socker och salt, är substanser som härrör från grupp 1-livsmedel eller från naturen genom processer som inkluderar pressning, raffinering, krossning, malning och torkning. The purpose of such processes is to make durable products that are suitable for use in home and restaurant kitchens to prepare, season and cook Group 1 foods and to make with them varied and enjoyable hand-made dishes and meals, such as stews, soups and broths, salads, breads, preserves, drinks and desserts. They are not meant to be consumed by themselves, and are normally used in combination with Group 1 foods to make freshly prepared drinks, dishes and meals.
