Contatta il team di Open Food Facts

Se hai una domanda, un commento o un suggerimento su Open Food Facts, puoi inviarci un'e-mail a contact@openfoodfacts.org.

Abbiamo già risposto a molte domande comuni, nelle nostre Domande Frequenti

FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions), or in our wiki.

For journalists, we have a presskit (accessible from the footer of the website). If you are a producer, please get in touch with us at producers@openfoodfacts.org.

For producers, a quick start guide on the homepage of the pro platform. If you are a journalist, please get in touch with us at presse@openfoodfacts.org.

For reusers, we have a data page (on the footer of the website). If you are a reuser (mobile app…), please get in touch with us at reuse@openfoodfacts.org.

Please note: Open Food Facts is a collaborative database of food products, we do not sell any product and we do not have links to their producers. Per domande e reclami riguardo un prodotto, puoi mandare il reclamo alla tua autorità locale, o scrivi direttamente al produttore (indirizzo sull'etichetta del prodotto).
